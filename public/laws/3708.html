Republic Act No. 3708
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SITANGKAI CENTRAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PARANG, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO DATU HALUN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Sitangkai Central School in the Municipality of Parang, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed to Datu Halun Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
