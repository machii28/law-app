Republic Act No. 3495
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DITSAAN-RAMAIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DITSAAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, TO SULTAN ALAUYA ALONTO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Ditsaan-Ramain Elementary School in the Municipality of Ditsaan, Province of Lanao del Sur, is hereby changed to Sultan Alauya Alonto Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
