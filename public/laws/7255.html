Republic Act No. 7255
March 20, 1992
AN ACT DECLARING EVERY 21ST DAY OF JUNE AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABARROGUIS, PROVINCE OF QUIRINO, TO BE KNOWN AS "ARAW NG CABARROGUIS"
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 21st of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Cabarroguis, Province of Quirino, to be known as "Araw ng Cabarroguis."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 4, 1991 and January 23, 1992, respectively.
