Republic Act No. 1390
June 18, 1955
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF THREE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS AS AID TO THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY FOR THE IMPROVEMENT OF ITS PRESENT BUILDINGS OR THE CONSTRUCTION OF NEW PAVILIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated as aid to the Philippine Tuberculosis Society for the improvement of its present buildings or the construction of new pavilions.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1955.
