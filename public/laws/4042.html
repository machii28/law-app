Republic Act No. 4042
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CREATING A BRANCH OFFICE OF THE REGISTER OF DEEDS OF MOUNTAIN PROVINCE WITH PERMANENT STATION IN TABUK, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE
.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby created a branch office of the Register of Deeds of Mountain Province in the Municipality of Tabuk, same province, which shall perform all the duties and functions of the register of deeds for the following municipalities: Tinglayan, Tanudan, Lubuagan, Balbalan, Tabuk, Pinukpuk, Conner, Kabugao, Bayag, Pudtol, Flora and Luna.

SEC. 2. To carry out the purpose of this Act, there is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of ten thousand one hundred four pesos which shall be spent as follows:

(a) One special deputy register of deeds I P2.676.00
(b) One clerical aide...................................1,548.00
(c) One janitor................................ ...........1,440.00
(d) One laborer...........................................1,440.00
(e) Sundry expenses............................. ......3,000.00

Such sums as may be necessary for the same purpose in subsequent years shall be included in the Annual General Appropriations Act

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
