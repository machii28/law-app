Republic Act No. 3455
June 16, 1962
AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO KHALIL KHODR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Khalil Khodr is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto: Provided, That this grant shall become effective upon Khalil Khodr taking an oath of allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines and executing a renunciation of his former citizenship, copies of which shall be recorded in the civil registrar's office in Butuan City: Provided, further, That the citizenship herein granted carries with it all the duties and responsibilities incident to such citizenship.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
