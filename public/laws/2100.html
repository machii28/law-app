Republic Act No. 2100
March 28, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF PUTOL IN THE PROVINCE OF NAGCARLAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO MALAYA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Putol in the Municipality of Nagcarlan, Province of Laguna, is changed to Malaya.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, March 28, 1959.
