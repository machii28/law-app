Republic Act No. 1527
June 16, 1956
AN ACT CONSTITUTING THE SITIOS OF BENGUEN, TARABANAN, BENDOYAN, TALABIGAN, TAGBUAN AND LANGOGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS CONCEPCION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Calagbenguen, Tarabanan, Bendoyan, Talabigan, Tagbuan, and Langogan in the Municipality of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as barrio Concepcion.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
