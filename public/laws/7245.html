Republic Act No. 7245
March 19, 1992
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE IPIL DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Ipil District Hospital in the Municipality of Ipil, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 16, 1991 and December 3, 1991, respectively.
