Republic Act No. 5241
June 15, 1968
AN ACT ABOLISHING THE REQUIREMENT OF NON-SUBVERSIVE AFFIDAVITS BY OFFICERS OF LABOR ORGANIZATIONS, REPEALING FOR THE PURPOSE SUB-PARAGRAPH (2), SUBSECTION (b), SECTION TWENTY-THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED EIGHT HUNDRED SEVENTY-FIVE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sub paragraph (2), subsection (b) of Section twenty-three of Republic Act Numbered Eight hundred seventy-five, entitled "an Act to promote industrial peace and for other purposes," requiring non-subversive affidavits by all the officers of every labor organization, association or union is hereby repealed. All other provisions in said Republic Act referring to such non-subversive affidavits are likewise hereby repealed.

SEC. 2. All Acts, parts of Acts, and regulations inconsist­ent with this Act are hereby repealed.

Approved, June 15, 1968.
