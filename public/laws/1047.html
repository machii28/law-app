Republic Act No. 1047
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TARUG BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF TARUG, MUNICIPALITY OF ALIMODIAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO AMAQUIN MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Tarug Barrio School in the barrio of Tarug, municipality of Alimodian, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Amaquin Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
