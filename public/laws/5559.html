Republic Act No. 5559
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF BANTAYAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LINGAYEN, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, INTO A BARRIO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF BANTAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Bantayan in the Municipality of Lingayen, Province of Pangasinan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Bantayan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
