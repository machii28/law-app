Republic Act No. 6094
August 4, 1969
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF THREE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE MAINTENANCE AND OPERATION OF THE ZIGA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the Ziga Memorial Hospital. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the same purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, August 4, 1969.
