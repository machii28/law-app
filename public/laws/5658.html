Republic Act No. 5658
June 21, 1969
AN ACT DECLARING THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, A PERMANENT SUBPORT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon, is hereby declared a permanent subport.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
