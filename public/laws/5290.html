Republic Act No. 5290
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAWAB, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Mawab, Province of Davao, are constituted as barrios of said municipality, as follows:
(a) The sitios of San Isidro and Mahayag are constituted into a district and
independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Katipunan;

(b) The sitio of Lunoluno is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio
of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Lunoluno;

(c) The sitio of Annaman is hereby converted into a distinct and independent
barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Tan-awan
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
