Republic Act No. 3993
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CONVERTING ALBAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLANGUI, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, INTO A NATIONAL SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS POLANGUI GENERAL COMPRE­HENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF NECESSARY FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Albay High School in the Municipality of Polangui, Province of Albay, is hereby converted into a national high school, to be known as Polangui Genera Comprehensive High School Comprehensive curriculum which includes both academic and vocational courses shall be offered. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize the said school accordingly.

Upon organization of the Polangui General Comprehensive High School,the assets, liabilities, personne1, equipment and records of the Albay High School shall stand transferred to the Polangui General Comprehensive High School.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of the said school for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five. Thereafter the necessary sum for the operation and maintenance of the said school shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
