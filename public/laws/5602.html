Republic Act No. 5602
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LOCTUGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF ROXAS TO JOSE HONTIVEROS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Loctugan Elementary School in the City of Roxas is hereby changed to Jose Hontiveros Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
