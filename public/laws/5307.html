Republic Act No. 5307
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LAMBUNAO CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAMBUNAO, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO EUGENIO LADRIDO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Lambunao Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Lambunao, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Eugenio Ladrido Memorial Elementary School,

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
