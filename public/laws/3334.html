Republic Act No. 3334
June 18, 1961
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, FROM THE BARRIO OF MUCAS TO THE BARRIO OF SALUG.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The seat of government of the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is transferred from the barrio of Mucas to the barrio of Salug.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
