Republic Act No. 1140
June 17, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACUIT IN THE PROVINCE OF PALAWAN TO EL NIDO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the municipality of Bacuit, province of Palawan, is hereby changed to
El Nido.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1954.
