Republic Act No. 3367
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF QUINALE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALABANGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Quinal in the Municipality of Calabanga, Province of Camarines Sur, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Quinale.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
