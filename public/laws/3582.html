Republic Act No. 3582
June 21, 1963
AN ACT TO INCREASE THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CAGAYAN PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN FROM FIFTY TO SEVENTY-FIVE BEDS AND TO AUTHORIZE THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS FOR THE PURPOSE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Cagayan Provincial Hospital in the Province of Cagayan is hereby increased from fifty to seventy-five beds.

SEC. 2. In addition to the appropriations for the said hospital under existing law, the sum of two hundred ninety-six thousand seven hundred twenty-one pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance thereof.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1963.
