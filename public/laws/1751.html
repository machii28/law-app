Republic Act No. 1751
June 21, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM F-031.7, TITLE F, APPENDIX A OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED AND ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item F-031.7, title F, appendix A of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred and eleven, is amended to read as follows:

"F-031.7 River control and protection of Babajurin River, Pinamalayan, Oriental Mindoro ............... P 23,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
