Republic Act No. 1475
June 14, 1956
AN ACT AMENDING TITLE F OF SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Title F of section one of Republic Act Numbered One thousand two hundred is hereby amended by transferring sub-item H (5) of item "af. Negros Occidental to item "n. Capiz" as sub-item (4) thereof.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
