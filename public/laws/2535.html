Republic Act No. 2535
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BUAYAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GENERAL P. SANTOS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, TO LAGAO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Buayan Elementary School in the Municipality of General P. Santos, Province of Cotabato, is changed to Lagao Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
