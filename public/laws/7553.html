Republic Act No. 7553
May 22, 1992
AN ACT DECLARING JUNE NINETEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO BE KNOWN AS "ADLAW NAN SURIGAO DEL NORTE"
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June Nineteen of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Surigao del Norte, to be known as "Adlaw Nan Surigao Del Norte."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days following its publication in two (2) national newspapers of general circulation in the country.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 4, 1991 and January 23, 1992, respectively.
