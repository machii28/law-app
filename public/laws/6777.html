Republic Act No. 6777
November 29, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GUISICAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY GUISICAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LABO, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO FLORENCE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Guisican Elementary School in Barangay Guisican, Municipality of Labo, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Florence Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 16, 1989 and September 15, 1989, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDWIN P. ACOBA (Sgd.) QUIRINO D. ABAD SANTOS, JR.
