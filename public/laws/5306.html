Republic Act No. 5306
June 15, 1968
AN ACT NAMING THE PUBLIC ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AT KAIMO STREET, BARRIO TAFT, MUNICIPALITY OF SURIGAO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, RICARDO NAVARRO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The public elementary school at Kaimo Street, Barrio Taft, Municipality of Surigao, Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby named Ricardo Navarro Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
