Republic Act No. 1822
June 22, 1957
AN ACT REAPPROPRIATING FOR CERTAIN PURPOSES THE APPROPRIATION IN ITEM (19), PAGE ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY-THREE, SUBPARAGRAPH "ab," FOR THE CITY OF MANILA, PARAGRAPH 4, TITLE A, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of ten thousand pesos appropriate in item (19), page one hundred eighty-three, subparagraph the City of Manila, paragraph 4, title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby Appropriated for the following purposes:
  1. Sta. Clara Street Asphalting from Lamayan to Havana ................................. P 6,000.00
  2. Trece de Agosto Street Asphalting from Paz to Estero de Paco ................... 4,000.00
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
