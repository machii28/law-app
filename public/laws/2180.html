Republic Act No. 2180
May 2, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BRIONES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALIBO, PROVINCE OF AKLAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Eu-ok in the barrio of Mabilo, Municipality of Malibo, Province of Aklan, is separated from said barrio, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Briones.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 2, 1959.
