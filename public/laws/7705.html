Republic Act No. 7705
May 5, 1994
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BUHANGIN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BUHANGIN, DAVAO CITY, TO BERNARDO CARPIO BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Buhangin Barangay High School in Barangay Buhangin, Davao City, is hereby changed to Bernardo Carpio Barangay High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

Lapsed into law on May 5, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
