Republic Act No. 4920
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ECONOMIA STREET IN THE DISTRICT OF SAMPALOC, CITY OF MANILA, TO VICENTE G. CRUZ.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Economia street in the District of Sampaloc, City of Manila, is hereby changed to Vicente G. Cruz.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1967.
