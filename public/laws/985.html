Republic Act No. 985
June 2, 1954
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE LAGUNA HIGH SCHOOL IN STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO PEDRO GUEVARA MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the eminent and patriotic services rendered to the country by the late Pedro Guevara, the name of Laguna High School in Sta. Cruz, Province of Laguna, is hereby changed to Pedro Guevara Memorial High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 2, 1954.
