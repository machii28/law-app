Republic Act No. 2570
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF CULI-CULI IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAKATI, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO PIO DEL PILAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Culi-Culi in the Municipality of Makati, Province of Rizal, is changed to Pio del Pilar.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
