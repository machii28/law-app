Republic Act No. 3509
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE UNGUS MATATA CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANDUBAS, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO PANGLIMA BITENG CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Ungus Matata Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Tandubas, Province of Sulu, is changed to Panglima Biteng Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
