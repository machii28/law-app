Republic Act No. 5586
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BARAT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BURGOS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO ABLAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Barat in the Municipality of Burgos, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Ablan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
