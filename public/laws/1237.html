Republic Act No. 1237
June 7, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE BARRIO OF ATELAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ECHAGUE, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO SAN MANUEL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of barrio Atelan, in the Municipality of Echague, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to barrio San Manuel.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 7, 1955.
