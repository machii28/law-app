Republic Act No. 435
June 7, 1950
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF NEW AYUQUITAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, TO "AMLAN" AND THE NAME OF BARRIO OLD AYUQUITAN TO "AYUQUITAN."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the municipality of New Ayuquitan, Province of Negros Oriental is hereby changed to "Amlan", and the name of barrio Old Ayuquitan of said municipality and province is hereby changed to that of "Ayuquitan".

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 7, 1950.
