Republic Act No. 1766
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BEHIA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIAONG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Behia in the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of Quezon, is separated from the barrio of Callejon, and is constituted into a new barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Behia.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
