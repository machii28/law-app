Republic Act No. 2846
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF NEW PIÑAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, TO PIÑAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of New Piñan in the Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is changed to Piñan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
