Republic Act No. 2130
April 7, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BUTUBUT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGSINGAL, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BARRIO MIRAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Butubut in the Municipality of Magsingal, Province of Ilocos Sur, is changed to barrio Miramar.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
