Republic Act No. 1029
June 12, 1954
ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF BARRIO BALAYANG MABINI, PANGASINAN, TO BARRIO LUNA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of barrio Balayang, Mabini, Pangasinan, is hereby changed to barrio Luna.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
