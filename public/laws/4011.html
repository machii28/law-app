Republic Act No. 4011
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OP PANGALALAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO PANGALALAN, MUNICIPALITY OF SINDANGAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, TO JUAN BUGARIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Pangalalan Elementary School to Barrio of Pangalalan, Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby changed to Juan Bugarin Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
