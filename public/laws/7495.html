Republic Act No. 7495
May 13, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NAMUAC ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ANDRES, MUNICIPALITY OF SANCHEZ MIRA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO NAMUAC-SAN ANDRES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Namuac Elementary School in Barangay San Andres, Municipality of Sanchez, Mira, Province of Cagayan, is hereby changed to Namuac-San Andres Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on October 17, 1991 and January 27, 1992, respectively.
