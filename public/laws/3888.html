Republic Act No. 3888
June 18, 1964
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN DIPOLOG, ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, TO BE KNOWN AS DIPOLOG NATIONAL EMERGENCY HOSPITAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established under the direct supervision of the Director of Medical Services, a national emergency hospital in the Municipality of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, to be known as Dipolog National Emergency Hospital.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation, and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-five. The sums necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
