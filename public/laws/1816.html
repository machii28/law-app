Republic Act No. 1816
June 22, 1957

SECTION 1. Item (16), paragraph g., Batangas, subsection three, title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:
"(16)
Construction of Industrial Arts Building, Sabang, Lipa City .............................................. P 4,000.00
(16-a)
Construction of academic building, Sabang, Lipa City ..................................................... 26,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
