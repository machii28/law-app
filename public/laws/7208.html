Republic Act No. 7208
March 6, 1992
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE NORTHERN SAMAR GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATARMAN, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR, FROM ONE HUNDRED (100) TO ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Northern Samar General Hospital in the Municipality of Catarman, Province of Northern Samar, is hereby increased from one hundred (100) to one hundred fifty (150) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives
