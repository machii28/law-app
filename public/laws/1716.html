Republic Act No. 1716
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF MORTHON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALETE, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO BARRIO FULGENCIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Morthon in the Municipality of Balete, Province of Capiz, is hereby changed to barrio Fulgencio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
