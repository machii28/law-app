Republic Act No. 11047
June 29, 2018
AN ACT RENAMING GOVERNORS DIVE, TRAVERSINGSORO-SORO BRIDGE IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA AND THE MUNICIPALITY OF TERNATE, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, AS JUANITO R. REMULLA, SR. ROAD
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Governor's Drive, traversing Soro-Soro Bridge in the City of Binan, Province of Laguna; the municipalities of Carmona and General Mariano Alvarez; the cities of Dasmarinas, General Trias and Trece Martires; and municipalities of Tanza, Naic and Ternate, Province of Cavite, is hereby renamed as Juanito R, Remulla, Sr. Road.

SEC. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.

Approved,
