Republic Act No. 1680
June 20, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (b), GROUP III, PARAGRAPH THIRTY-FIVE, FOR THE MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, SUBSECTION f, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (b), group III, paragraph thirty-five, for the Mountain Province, subsection f, section two of Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"(b) Besao Municipal Building Construction
P5,000.00"


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
