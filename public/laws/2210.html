Republic Act No. 2210
May 15, 1959
AN ACT PROVINDING FOR THE CREATION OF AN ADDITIONAL POSITION OF MUNICIPAL JUDGE FOR THE CITY OF CAVITE, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION SEVENTY-FOUR OF COMMONWEALTH ACT NUMBERED FIVE HUNDRED FORTY-SEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The first paragraph of section seventy-four of Commonwealth Act Numbered Five hundred forty-seven, otherwise known as the Charter of the City of Cavite, is amended to read as follows:
"SEC. 74. Regular, auxiliary and acting judges of municipal court.—There shall be a municipal court for the City of Cavite, for which two municipal judges and an auxiliary municipal judge shall be appointed."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 15, 1959.
