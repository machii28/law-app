Republic Act No. 1317
June 16, 1955
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIOS OF LIDONG, TABA-TABA, MANMONTOY, BORABOD, TAISAN, HINAMPACAN, MANLIMONSITO, POLANTUNA, AND OLIVA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BASUD, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, INTO BAR­RIOS OF SAID MUNICIPALITY WITH THE SAME NAMES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Lidong, Taba-Taba, Manmontoy, Borabod, Taisan, Hinampacan, Manlimonsito, Polantuna, and Oliva in the Municipality of Basud, Province of Cama­rines Norte, are converted into barrios of said municipality with the same names.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
