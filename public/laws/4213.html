Republic Act No. 4213
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DOLORES, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Bagongbong and Cabago-an in the Municipality of Dolores, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Bagongbong and Cabago-an, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
