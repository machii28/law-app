Republic Act No. 2220
May 16, 1959
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, FROM BARRIO TIMAN TO UPPER LILOY BEACH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The seat of government of the Municipal of Liloy, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby transferred from Barrio Timan to upper Liloy Beach.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 16, 1959.
