Republic Act No. 5635
June 21, 1969
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED QAPAC1TY OF BATAC GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF 1LOCOS NORTE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Batac General Hospital in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby increased from twenty-five to fifty beds.

SEC. 2. Such sums as may be needed for the operation of the additional facilities shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
