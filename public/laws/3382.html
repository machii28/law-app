Republic Act No. 3382
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE PROVINCE OF PALAWAN


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Taritien in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Taritien.

SEC. 2. The sitio of Sibaring in Apo-Bugsuk Island in the Municipality of Balabac, same province, is separated from the barrio of Apo-Bugsuk Island and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Sibaring. The barrio of Sibaring shall be from and including the sitio of Balug-Bulugin to and including the sitio of Baring-Baringin.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
