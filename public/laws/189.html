Republic Act No. 189
June 22, 1947
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO SAMBAT, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO LUMANGBAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of barrio Sambat, in the municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Lumangbayan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 22, 1947.
