Republic Act No. 1705
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TARLAC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION. 1. The sitios of Lubigan, Laoang, Lawac-Amulag, Labney and San Juan, Municipality of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Lubigan, Laoang, Lawac-Amulag, Labney and San Juan, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
