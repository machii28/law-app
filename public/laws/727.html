Republic Act No. 727
June 6, 1952
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF TALIPAPA IN QUEZON CITY TO TANDANG SORA
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In honor of the memory of one of the most courageous, heroic and patriotic women in Philippine history, the name of the barrio of Talipapa in Quezon City is hereby changed to Tandang Sora.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 6, 1952.
