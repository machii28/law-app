Republic Act No. 2225
May 17, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Palawan, are constituted into barrios, to wit:
  1. The sitios of Punta Baja and Malabunga to be known as the barrio of Punta Baja; and
  2. The sitios of Maasin and Iwahig to be known as the barrio of Maasin.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 17, 1959.
