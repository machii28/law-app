Republic Act No. 1584
June 16, 1956
AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF WAO IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrios of Wao, Katubungan, Panang and Capigis in the Municipal District of Maguing, Province of Lanao, are separated from the said district, and constituted into a separate district in the same province to be known as the Municipal District of Wao, with its seat of government at the poblacion of Wao.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
