Republic Act No. 2829
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MADAAT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DARAGA, PROVINCE OF ALBAY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Madaat in the barrio of San Vicente Grande, Municipality of Daraga, Province of Albay is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Madaat.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
