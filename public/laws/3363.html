Republic Act No. 3363
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SIBALIW IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANGA, PROVINCE OF AKLAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Banga in the Municipality of Banga, Province of Aklan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Sibaliw. This barrio is bounded on the North by the Panatalian Creek, on the East by the old sitio cemetery, on the South by the boundary line of the Municipality of Balete, and on the West by the sitio of Badiangan in barrio Tarralba.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
