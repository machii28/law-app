Republic Act No. 3972
June 18, 1964
AN ACT APPROVING ANY ASSIGNMENT, SALE, AND TRANSFER OF THE FRANCHISE GRANTED TO RAFAEL CONSING UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIX HUNDRED NINETY-SEVEN IN FAVOR OF MARCELINA S. AQUINO OF BUTUAN CITY.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Subject to the terms and conditions pre­scribed in Act Numbered Thirty-six hundred thirty-six, and to the provisions of the Constitution, approval is hereby given to any assignment, sale and transfer of the franchise granted to Rafael Consing to install, operate and maintain a telephone system in the City of Butuan, under Republic Act Numbered Six hundred ninety-seven, in favor of Marcelina S. Aquino of Butuan City, together with all the property and equipment acquired thereunder.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
