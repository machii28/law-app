SEC. 2. Upon approval of this Act, the respective officer or employee actually occupying any of these positions hereinabove mentioned shall be entitled to the salary provided for the said position without need of a new appointment.

SEC. 3. The salary differential resulting from the adjustment in the salaries as provided in this Act shall be paid proportionately out of the savings and twenty per cent of the income of the Office of the Solicitor General for the fiscal year when this Act is approved. Thereafter, the necessary funds needed to implement the provisions of this Act shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. Enacted without executive approval, June 17, 1972.
