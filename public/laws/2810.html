Republic Act No. 2810
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOGOD, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Sogod, Province of Leyte, are hereby converted or constituted into barrios:
  1. Sitios Kantabuan, Baycasili, Mamingaw, Tagabaca and Kampuwa, into a barrio to be named Barrio Kauswagan;
  2. Sitio Ilo, into a barrio to be named Barrio Malinaw; and
  3. Sitios Hubasan, Agta, Manduduknay, Kabas-an and Cabadbaran, into a barrio to be named Barrio San Juan.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
