Republic Act No. 2814
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CASIGURAN, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following places and sitios in the Municipality of Casiguran, Province of Sorsogon, are declared as barrios thereof, to wit:
  1. The sitios of Cabognahan, Cagdagat, Fabrica, Lubas and Marines, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Boton;
  2. The sitios of Mambot and Namuut, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Burgos;
  3. The barrio of Casay as barrio Casay;
  4. The barrio of Cogon as barrio Cogon;
  5. The barrio of Inlagadean as barrio Inlagadean;
  6. The barrio of Lugñid as barrio Lugñid;
  7. The barrio of Mabini as barrio Mabini;
  8. The sitio of Pamakiran as a barrio to be known as the barrio of Ponong;
  9. sitios of Colambis, Lugi, Malagomgom, Rawag and Tiris, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Rizal;
  10. The barrio of San Antonio as barrio San Antonio;
  11. The sitios of Tigbao and Nabugñaren, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of San Isidro
  12. The sitio of Escuela, as a barrio to be known as the barrio of San Juan;
  13. The sitios of Cadiang and Purog, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Pascual;
  14. The sitios of Buraburan, Calayogan, Sog-ong Talahib, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Santa Cruz;
  15. The sitios of Mauhang and Storeroom, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Trece Martires; and
  16. The sitios of Adovis, Busing, Cawit, Central, Somalot, Timbayog, Tinampo and Tulay, as a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Poblacion.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
