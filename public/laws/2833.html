Republic Act No. 2833
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ALEGRIA STREET IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BILAR, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO JUAN LUNA STREET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Alegria Street in the Municipality of Eilar, Province of Bohol, is hereby changed to Juan Luna Street.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
