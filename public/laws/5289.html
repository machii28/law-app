Republic Act No. 5289
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO CAWAYANON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON, TO VINTAR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Cawayanon in the Municipality of Valencia, Province of Bukidnon, is hereby changed to Vintar.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
