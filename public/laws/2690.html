Republic Act No. 2690
June 18, 1960
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF SEVENTY-THREE THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF THE WESTERN PANGASINAN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL DURING THE FISCAL YEAR NINETEEN HUNDRED SIXTY-ONE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of seventy-three thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the Western Pangasinan Emergency Hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-one. Such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1960.
