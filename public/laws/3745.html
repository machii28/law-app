Republic Act No. 3745
June 22, 1963
AN ACT TO DECLARE BARRIO NALASIN OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIDLIDDA, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, AS THE SEAT OF THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF THE SAID MUNICIPALITY.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Barrio Nalasin of the Municipality of Lidlidda, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby declared as the seat of the municipal government of the said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
