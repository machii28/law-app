Republic Act No. 6687
December 15, 1988
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF POTIA, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, INTO LISTA AND CONFIRMING THE BARANGAY OF STA. MARIA MUNICIPALITY OF LISTA, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, AS THE SEAT OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LISTA
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Potia is hereby changed to the Municipality Lista.

SEC. 2. The present seat of the municipality government in the Barangay of Sta. Maria, Municipality of Lista, Province of Ifugao is hereby confirmed as the Seat government of the Municipality of Lista.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval and in publication in the circular (2 newspapers of general circular).

Approved,
