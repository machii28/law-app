Republic Act No. 3762
June 22, 1963
AN ACT DECLARING THE BARRIO OF ALANGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIMAY, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, A DULY CONSTITUTED BARRIO THEREOF.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1 The barrio of Alangan in the Municipality of Limay, Province of Bataan, is hereby declared a duly constituted barrio of said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
