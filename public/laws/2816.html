Republic Act No. 2816
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF VILLA REYES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN NARCISO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Komaya, Rekudo and Itaa are separated from the barrio of Vigo Cantidang in the Municipality of San Narciso, Province of Quezon, and constituted into a new barrio to be known as Barrio Villa Reyes of the same municipality, which shall be bounded on the North by Cantidang Creek, on the East by Ragay Gulf, on the West by Pugahanin and Mayho Ranges, and on the South by Bahay River.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
