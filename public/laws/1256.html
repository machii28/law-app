Republic Act No. 1256
June 10, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MANUGUIT STREET, A NATIONAL HIGHWAY IN THE CITY OF MANILA, TO JOSE ABAD SANTOS STREET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Manuguit Street, a national highway in the City of Manila, shall hereafter be known as Jose Abad Santos Street.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
