Republic Act No. 2412
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE ALIMODIAN HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALIMODIAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A NATIONAL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Alimodian High School in the Municipality of Alimodian, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a national vocational high school to be known as the Alimodian National Vocational High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education is hereby authorized to reorganize said school accordingly.

SEC. 3. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of said school for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty. Thereafter, the necessary sum for the said purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated are provided for.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
