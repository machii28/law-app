Republic Act No. 6557
September 30, 1972
AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, THE CRISPINA-MARCOS MEMORIAL LIBRARY AND MUSEUM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, the Crispina-Marcos Memorial Library and Museum under the direct supervision of the Director of National Library.

SEC. 2. The Director of National Library shall appoint a curator and librarian and such other personnel as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act. The amount of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated for the maintenance and operation of the Crispina-Marcos Library and Museum during the fiscal year nineteen hundred and seventy-three. Thereafter, such sum as may be necessary for the purpose of this Act shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, September 30, 1972.
