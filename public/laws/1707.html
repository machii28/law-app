Republic Act No. 1707
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MACALANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DASOL, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Macalang, Pantol, Tapac and Pagdagaan, Municipality of Dasol, Province of Pangasinan, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Macalang.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
