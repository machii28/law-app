Republic Act No. 1748
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF DIBANGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Island of Dibangan is constituted into barrio in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, to be known as the barrio of Dibangan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
