Republic Act No. 2783
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF TUCALAN-MIÑAÑGA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LASAM, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO NICOLAS AGATEP.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Tucalan-Miñañga in the Municipality of Lasam, Province of Cagayan, is changed to Nicolas Agatep.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
