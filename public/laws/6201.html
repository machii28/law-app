Republic Act No. 6201
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO CATOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CANDELARIA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Catol in the Municipality of Candelaria, Province of Zambales, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Catol.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
