Republic Act No. 4019
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TAGBAK ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INDANAN, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO PANGLIMA MAMMAH ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Tagbak Elementary School the Municipality of Indanan, Province of Sulu. is charged to Panglima Mammah Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
