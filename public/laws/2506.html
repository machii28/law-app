Republic Act No. 2506
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SANTA CRUZ IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Kalatubog and Kilometer 32 of Puerto Princesa North Road in the Municipality of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Santa Cruz.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
