Republic Act No. 1433
June 14, 1956
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEMS NINETEEN AND TWENTY, aa. LA UNION, PARAGRAPH I, TITLE B, OF SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED. (Re Public Works Act.)
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Items nineteen and twenty, aa. La Union, paragraph I, title B, of section one of Republic Act Numbered One thousand two hundred, are amended to read as follows:
"(19) Leones-Macalva Tubao-Agoo
Completion…………………………P8,000.00

"(20) Sta. Teresa Road, Tubao
Completion………………………….5,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1956.
