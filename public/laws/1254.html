Republic Act No. 1254
June 10, 1955
AN ACT TO AMEND SUB-ITEMS ONE TO SIXTEEN OF ITEM (d) FOR THE PROVINCE OF SAMAR, SUB-SECTION (e), TITLE B, SECTION ONE, RE­PUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED AND TWENTY, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS PUBLIC WORKS ACT OF NINETEEN HUNDRED AND FIFTY-THREE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sub-items one to sixteen of Item (d) for the Province of Samar, sub-section (e), Title B, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred and twenty, otherwise known as the Public Works Act of nineteen hundred and fifty-three, are hereby amended to read as follows:
"(d) For the construction of the municipal
building of San Antonio.............................. P25,000.00

"(d-1) For the completion of the municipal
building of Catubig.............................................8,000.00

"(d-2) For the completion of the municipal
building of Palapag.............................................8,000.00

"(d-3) For the improvement of the 6-room academic
building of the Central Elementary
School of Pambujan...........................................5,000.00

"(d-4) For the completion of the municipal
building of Las Navas..................................... 4,000.00"
Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
