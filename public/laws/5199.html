Republic Act No. 5199
April 27, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE KABAN­KALAN MATERNITY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO DR. GUMERSINDO GARCIA, SR. MEMORIAL HOSPITAL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Kabankalan Maternity Hospital, in the Municipality of Kabankalan, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Dr. Gumersindo Garcia, Sr. Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 27, 1968.
