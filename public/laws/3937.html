Republic Act No. 3937
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GUMACA EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO GUMACA GENERAL HOSPTAL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name Gumaca Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Gumaca, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Gumaca General Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
