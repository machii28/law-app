Republic Act No. 1464
June 14, 1956
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM ONE, SUBPARAGRAPH i, UNDER PROVINCIAL CAPITOLS, PARAGRAPH FOUR, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item one, subparagraph i, paragraph four, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is amended to read as follows:

"1. Nueva Vizcaya Provincial Capitol—(1)
Construction of Annex to
Office of Provincial Governor……………………………. P5,000.00."

SEC. 2. Insert the following item as Item seven, sub-paragraph am, paragraph four, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred to compensate for the deletion in the preceding section:

"(7) Diffun Municipal Town Hall
Construction………………………… ……………….. P10,000.00."

SEC. 3 This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
