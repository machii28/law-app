Republic Act No. 2167
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF VILLA GALED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PATNOÑGON, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO VILLA CRESPO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Villa Galed in the Municipality of Patnoñgon, Province of Antique, is hereby to Villa Crespo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
