Republic Act No. 3322
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING A BRANCH OFFICE OF THE REGISTER OF DEEDS OF THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON, WITH PERMANENT STATION AT THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALER, SUBPROVINCE OF AURORA, SAME PROVINCE, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby created a branch office of the Register of Deeds of the Province of Quezon, with permanent station at the Municipality of Baler, Subprovince of Aurora, same province, which shall perform all the duties and functions of the register of deeds for the following municipalities: Baler, Casiguran, Dipaculao, Maria Aurora and Dingalan.

SEC. 2. To carry out the provision of this Act, there is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of eight thousand two hundred eighty pesos which shall be spent for the following:

(a) Deputy Register of Deeds P2,400.00
(b) One clerk 1,440.00
(c) One janitor messenger 1,440.00
(d) Sundry expenses 8,000.00
P8,280.00


Such sums as may be necessary for the same purpose in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
