Republic Act No. 4895
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BANAGO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALABAGAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, TO BALABAGAN ELEMENTARY COMMUNITY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Banago Elementary School in the Municipality of Balabagan, Province of Lanao del Sur, is hereby changed to Balabagan Elementary Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1967.
