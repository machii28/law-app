Republic Act No. 1458
June 14, 1956
AN ACT AMENDING AND REPEALING CERTAIN ITEMS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Items (c) and (i), paragraph thirty-two, for the Province of Mindoro Oriental, subsection section two of Republic Act numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:
"(c) Extension of Pinamalayan Waterworks to Lumangbayan ...................... P8,000.00

"(i) Pinamalayan Municipal Building, Construction ....................................... 9,000.00"
SEC. 2. Item (v), paragraph thirty-two, for the Province of Mindoro Oriental, subsection "f" section two of the same Act is repealed.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1956.
