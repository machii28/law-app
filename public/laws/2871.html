Republic Act No. 2871
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANILAO, PROVINCE OF ILOILO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Anilao, Province of Iloilo, are constituted and declared as barrios therein, to wit:
  1. The sitios of Janicaman Ilaya, Dinum-at Bugas, Cantoy, Bobog, H. Lourdes and Buena Lastima, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Poblacion;
  2. The sitios of Binun-an Sulod and Janicaman Ilawod,into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as thebarrio of Pantalan;
  3. The sitios of Pangpang Norte, Pangpang Sur, Ilawod Sur, Moros, Baluarte and Balañga-an, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Sta. Rita;
  4. The sitios of Bato, Bat-us, Himogbugan and Mumwick, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Dangula-an;
  5. The sitios of Labigan, Lu-awan, Bogtong Bukid, Payud, Dalipe Sur, Guibuañgan Sur and Bariñgan Sur, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of San Carlos;
  6. The sitios of Oriñgan, Alibayug Ilawod, Agloy-a, Ilawod, Aglankawas, Hulot and Suyakan, into a distinct independent barrio, to be known as barrio of Manganese.
  7. The sitios of Marka Bobog, Agtambo, Olo Anilao, Anilao Cayo, Orol and Mangiyakiya, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Balunos:
  8. The sitios of Buenavista, Guintapukan, Inadlawan, Kabangkalan, Tangcong, Dalipe, Lampuyang, Sibucawan and Guiraguid, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Cag-an;
  9. The sitios of Pangurayan, Kapaclan, Lininti-an, Badiañgan and Agpandan Daku, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Mostro;
  10. The sitios of Guinting, Sta. Ana Sulod, Amador, Kaliburan, Boyongan Talus and Linao Kalayo, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Palaypay ;
  11. The sitios of Bacolod, Bonsod Norte, Hda. San Marcelino, Bairan Sur and Patio, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Sabang Culob;
  12. The sitios of Bonsod Sur, Balagon, Igang and Botitihon, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Badiang;
  13. The sitios of Biga-a and Plantation No. 1, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Serallo;
  14. The sitios of Odok, Kadugang and Genis Bukid, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Medina;
  15. The sitios of Agtalus, Ilawod, Trencera, and Genis Punta Andagao, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of San Juan Crisostomo;
  16. The sitios of Bairan, Baguio, Agtalus, Ilaya and Genis Olo Agbatuan, into a distinct and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Agbatuan;
  17. The sitios of Pitogo, Manise Ilaya, Bagongbong and Olo Pitogo, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Vista Alegre;
  18. The sitios of Manise Ilawod, Bognay, Kangong, Talangban and Dumingding, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Camiros;
  19. The sitios of Namatay, Lanawon, Ilaho, Kagnayahan, Bacolod Kalubihan and Guinbalayan Ato, in tinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Sto. Rosario;
  20. The sitios of Aglay-ao, Malublub and Agbalatong, into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Balabag; and
  21. The sitios of Agcarope, Opao, Alabedhan, Agloy-a, Ilaya, Alibayug and Agbatasan, into a distinct and pendent barrio, to be known as the barrio of Guipes.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
