Republic Act No. 4211
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUINAPUNDAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Palactad, Pana-ugan, Cambilla, Anislag, Continio and Bagto in the Municipality of Quinapundan, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Palactad, Pana-ugan, Cambilla, Anislag, Continio and Bagto, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
