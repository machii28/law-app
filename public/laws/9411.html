Republic Act No. 9411
March 24, 2007
AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 22 OF EACH YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING IN THE CITY OF VIGAN, ILOCOS SUR TO COMMEMORATE ITS ANNIVERSARY AS A CITY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. January 22 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the City of Vigan, Ilocos Sur to commemorate its anniversary as a city.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days from its publication in at least two newspapers of heneral circulation in the Philippines.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 14, 2005 and January 22, 2007 respectively.
