Republic Act No. 4300
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CALASA COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HIMAMAYLAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO DON JOSE T. RATIOS MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Calasa Community School in the Municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Don Jose T. Ramos Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
