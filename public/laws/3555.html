Republic Act No. 3555
June 21, 1963
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF THE SUM OF FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF AN ADDITIONAL BUILDING FOR THE NEW WASHINGTON NATIONAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NEW WASHINGTON, PROVINCE OF AKLAN, INCLUDING THE PURCHASE OF A SITE THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the construction of an additional building for the New Washington National School of Fisheries in the Municipality New Washington, Province of Aklan, including the purchase of a site therefor.

SEC. 2. This Act, shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1963.
