Republic Act No. 4253
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF MASCARIA BARRIO SCHOOL AND VILLA GOMEZ BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO PEDRO CABAGON BARRIO SCHOOL AND ULPIANO CAMACHO BARRIO SCHOOL, RESPECTIVELY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The names of Mascaria Barrio School and Villa Gomez Barrio School in the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Quezon, are hereby changed to Pedro Cabagon Barrio School and Ulpiano Camacho Barrio School, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
