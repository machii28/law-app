Republic Act No. 6184
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO LITAWAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIBUCO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Litawan, Besong, Supit, Palatanan, Cumaron, Lindang, Durian, Sulitan and Timulan in Barrio Panganuran, Municipality of Sibuco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are separated from said barrio, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio in said municipality, to be known as Barrio Litawan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
