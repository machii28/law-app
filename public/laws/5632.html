Republic Act No. 5632
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF NAPAYONG ISLAND, LOCATED IN TAAL LAKE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO LAUREL ISLAND.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Napayong Island, located in Taal Lake, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Laurel Island.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
