Republic Act No. 2801
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BUKAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Alibungbungan in the Municipality of Rizal, Province of Laguna, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bukal.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
