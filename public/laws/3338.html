Republic Act No. 3338
June 18, 1961
AN ACT AUTHORIZING AND APPROVING THE TRANSFER OF THE TEMPORARY PERMIT GRANTED TO MR. HONORIO G. REYES BY REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED EIGHTEEN HUNDRED THIRTY-FIVE IN FAVOR OF MR. ENRIQUE M. OROZCO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The transfer of the temporary permit granted to Mr. Honorio G. Reyes by Republic Act Numbered Eighteen hundred thirty-five in favor of Mr. Enrique M. Orozco, together with all the rights, privileges and permits that may have been granted to the former is hereby authorized and approved: Provided, That this transfer of temporary permit shall be subject to the same terms and conditions provided under Republic Act Numbered Eighteen hundred thirty-five.

SEC. 2. This Act. shall take effect upon Its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
