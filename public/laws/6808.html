Republic Act No. 6808
December 13, 1989
AN ACT NAMING THE PLARIDEL-BUSTOS-ANGAT-NORZAGARAY-SANTA MARIA HIGHWAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN AS THE GENERAL ALEJO G. SANTOS HIGHWAY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Plaridel-Bustos-Angat-Norzagaray-Santa Maria Highway in the Province of Bulacan is hereby named as the General Alejo G. Santos Highway.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 18, 1989 and September 28, 1989, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDWIN P. ACOBA (Sgd.) QUIRINO D. ABAD SANTOS, JR.
