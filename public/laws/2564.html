Republic Act No. 2564
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN VICENTE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Malamnang in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Vicente.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
