Republic Act No. 6190
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CONVERTING SITIO ISLAGAN INTO A REGULAR BARRIO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MASLOG, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO SAN ISIDRO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Islagan is hereby converted into a regular barrio in the Municipality of Maslog, Province of Eastern Samar, to be known as Barrio San Isidro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
