Republic Act No. 5400
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO TAPALAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTS.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Tapalan in Barrio Palandoc, Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Tapalan. The said barrio shall be bounded as follows:
1. North-Starting from the Northeastern corner of Lot
No. 2711, going counterclockwise, proceed to Lot
No. 2716, 2725, 2726, 2728 and 2729;
2. East-Take all areas traversed by Palandoc River;

3. South-By Pls-742;

4. West-Take all areas traversed by Lipakan River. All areas within bounds of delineation comprise the pressed Barrio Tapalan.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
