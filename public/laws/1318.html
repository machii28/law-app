Republic Act No. 1318
June 16, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNIC­IPALITY OF SABANI, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, TO GABALDON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Sabani, Province of Nueva Ecija, is hereby changed to Gabaldon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
