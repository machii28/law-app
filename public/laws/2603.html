Republic Act No. 2603
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF DAPDAP IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN REMIGIO, PROVINCE OF CEBU.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Dapdap in the Municipality of San Remigio, Province of Cebu, is converted into a barrio of the said Municipality to be known as the barrio of Dapdap.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
