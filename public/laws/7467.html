Republic Act No. 7467
April 28, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SALCEDO MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALCEDO, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO SALCEDO HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Salcedo Municipal High School in the Municipality of Salcedo, Province of Samar, is hereby changed to Salcedo High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 28, 1992.
