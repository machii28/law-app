Republic Act No. 3565
June 21, 1963

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (a), page 45, subparagraph (17) III, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, is hereby amended to read as follows:
"(a) Talisay Municipal Hall Construction……………….P75,000.00"
