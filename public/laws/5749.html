Republic Act No. 5749
June 21, 1969
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM 141, PAGE 176, PARAGRAPH "A," TITLE G, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FORTY-EIGHT HUNDRED FIFTY-FOUR (RE PUBLIC WORKS ACT).
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 141, page 176, paragraph "a," Title G, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Forty-eight hundred fifty-four, is hereby amended to read as follows:
"141. Barucbuc Communal Irrigation Project, Mallig, Isabela - P25,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
