Republic Act No. 6213
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PAMAÑGUNAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIASI, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO COUNCILOR ANNI JALMAANI ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Pamañgunan Elementary School in the Municipality of Siasi, Province of Sulu, is changed to Councilor Anni Jalmaani Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
