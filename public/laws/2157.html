Republic Act No. 2157
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF PANGALALAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINDANGAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Pangalalan in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Pangalalan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect, upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
