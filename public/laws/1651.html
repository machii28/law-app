Republic Act No. 1651
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO GARAMPANG, ROSARIO, LA UNION, TO BARRIO SAN JOSE, ROSARIO, LA UNION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of Garampang in the Municipality of Rosario, La Union, shall be renamed barrio San Jose, Rosario, La Union.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
