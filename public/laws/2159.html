Republic Act No. 2159
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN RAFAEL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUSUANGA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of San Rafael in the Municipality Busuanga, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Rafael.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
