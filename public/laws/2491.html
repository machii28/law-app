Republic Act No. 2491
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MONTALBAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONTALBAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL TO E. RODRIGUEZ, JR., ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Montalban Elementary School in the Municipality of Montalban, Province of Rizal, is changed to E. Rodriguez, Jr., Elementary School.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
