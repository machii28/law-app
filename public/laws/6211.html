Republic Act No. 6211
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MANTA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIASI, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO SUSULAN JIMLANI ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Manta Elementary School in the Municipality of Siasi, Province of Sulu, is changed to Susulan Jimlani Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
