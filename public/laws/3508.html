Republic Act No. 3508
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAIMBUNG CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAIMBUNG, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO SULTAN MOHAMAD JAMALUL KIRAM CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Maimbung Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Maimbung, Province of Sulu, is changed to Sultan Mohamad Jamalul Kiram Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
