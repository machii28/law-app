Republic Act No. 2122
April 5, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAYO IN THE PROVINCE 0F CATANDUANES TO PANGANIBAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Payo in the Province of Catanduanes is changed to Panganiban.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 5, 1959.
