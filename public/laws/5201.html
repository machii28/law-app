Republic Act No. 5201
April 27, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE SAME OF BINACAG ELE­MENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ESPIRITU, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO QUIAOIT MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Binacag Elementary School in the Municipality of Espiritu, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Quiaoit Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 27, 1968.
