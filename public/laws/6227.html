Republic Act No. 6227
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SINGALAT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Singalat, Upper Katipa, Calog, Bulawan, Amparo, Bangkal, Mabas, and Tingkob in the barrio of Siloy, Municipality of Calamba, Province of Misamis Occidental, are separated from said barrio and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Singalat.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval

Approved, June 19, 1971.
