Republic Act No. 2621
May 25, 1960
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUMS OF ONE MILLION PESOS AND TWO HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS, RESPECTIVELY, FOR RELIEF AND AID TO FIRE VICTIMS IN THE CITIES OF CAVITE AND BUTUAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There are appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sums of one million pesos for relief and aid to Filipino fire victims in the City of Cavite, and two hundred thousand pesos for Butuan City.

The distribution of the relief and aid shall be made by the Red Cross.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 25, 1960.
