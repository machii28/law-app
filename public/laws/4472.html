Republic Act No. 4472
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF NEW CAGAYANCILLO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALABAC, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Tarumbasi, Malanta, Baring-Baringin, Bonbon and Casayapan in the Municipality of Balabac, Province of Palawan, are hereby constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of New Cagayancillo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
