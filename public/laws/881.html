Republic Act No. 881
June 19, 1953
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CAPALANGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF APALIT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA, TO MACARIO ARNEDO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Capalangan Elementary School in the municipality of Apalit, Province of Pampanga, is hereby changed to Macario Arnedo Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1953.
