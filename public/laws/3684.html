Republic Act No. 3684
June 22, 1963
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PILOT OPPORTUNITY SCHOOL OF FISHERIES AT SANGA-SANGA, MUNICIPALITY OF BONGAO, ISLAND OF TAWI-TAWI, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SULU PILOT OPPORTUNITY SCHOOL OF FISHERIES, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF THREE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Sulu Pilot Opportunity School of Fisheries shall be established at Sanga-Sanga, Municipality of Bongao, Island of Tawi-Tawi, Province of Sulu. The school shall offer opportunity or special courses in fish capture, fish cuIture, fish preservation and such other short-term fishery courses that the Director of Public Schools may deem necessary for the service area. The Director of Public Schools shall promulgate, rules and regulations to implement this Act.

SEC. 2. The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated for its operation and maintenance and shall thereafter be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
