Republic Act No. 2849
June 19, 1960
AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN ITEMS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-THREE HUNDRED AND ONE, ENTITLED "AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (a) on page 441, under Group II for the Province of Negros Oriental, paragraph d, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one is hereby amended to read as follows:

"(a) Lazi Municipal Building-

Construction --------------- P30, 000.00"

SEC. 2. Item No. 4 on page 587, under "Public Buildings," paragraph e, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one is hereby amended to as follows:

"4. Lazi-

(a) Municipal Building ------------- P4,000.00"

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
