Republic Act No. 4784
June 18, 1966
AN ACT CREATING A 1USANCH OFFICE OF THE REGISTER OF DEEDS OF THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON, WITH PERMANENT STATION AT THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, SAME PROVINCE AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby created a branch office of the Register of Deeds of the Province of Quezon, with permanent station at the Municipality of Mauban, same province, which shall perform all the duties and functions if the register of deeds for the following municipalities: Mauban, Sampaloc, infanta, Real, General Nakar, Polillo, purdeos, Jumalig, Panukulan and Patnanungan.

SEC. 2. To carry out the provisions of this Act, there is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of fifteen thousand pesos which shall be spent for the following:

(a) Deputy Register of Deeds
.1,3,720.00
(b) One clerk
.1,800.00
(c) One clerk
1,560.00
(d) One janitor-messenger
1,440.00
(e) Sundry expenses
6,480.00
Total outlay
P 15,000.00


Such sums as may be necessary for the same purpose is subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval

Approved, June 18, 1966.
