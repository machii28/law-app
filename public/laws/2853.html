Republic Act No. 2853
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF AÑGARIAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUGALLON, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, INTO A BARRIO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF AÑGARIAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Añgarian in the Municipality of Bugallon, Province of Pangasinan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Añgarian.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
