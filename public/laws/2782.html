Republic Act No. 2782
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (v), FOR THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, PAGE 16, TITLE B, PARAGRAPH a, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY HUNDRED AND NINETY-THREE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (v), for the Province of Batangas, page 16, Title B, paragraph a, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred and ninety-three is amended to read as follows:

"(v) Darasa feeder road, Tanauan—
Construction P10,000.00."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
