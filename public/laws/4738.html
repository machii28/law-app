Republic Act No. 4738
June 18, 1966
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF PARACALES, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, TO PARACELIS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Paracales, Mountain Province, is hereby changed to Paracelis.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
