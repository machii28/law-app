Republic Act No. 3366
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF LARAPAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAUSWAGAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Larapan in the Municipality of Kauswagan, Province of Lanao del Norte, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Larapan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
