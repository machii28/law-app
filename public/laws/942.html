Republic Act No. 942
June 20, 1953
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF PAGKILATAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATANGAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Pagkilatan is separated from the barrio of Matoco, municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, and constituted into a barrio in the said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Pagkilatan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
