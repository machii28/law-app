Republic Act No. 6228
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO SANTA MARIA IN THE CITY OF IRIGA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Tubigan, Katungdulan, Bagacay, Sabang, Sampaga, Rao, and Cawayan in Barrio Santa Teresita, City of Iriga, are separated from said Barrio and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said city, to be known as Barrio Santa Maria.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
