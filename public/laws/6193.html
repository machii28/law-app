Republic Act No. 6193
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VILLABA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
Be it enacted, by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Villaba, Province of Leyte, are hereby converted into barrios, of said municipality, to wit:
  1. Sitio Tabunoc, to be known as Barrio Tabunoc;
  2. Sitio Hibulangan, to be known as Barrio Hibulangan;
  3. Sitio Camporog, to be known as Barrio Camporog;
  4. Sitio Payao, to be known as Barrio Payao;
  5. Sitio Cabungahan, to be known as Barrio Cabungahan;
  6. Sitio Tinghob, to be known as Barrio Tinghob;
  7. Sitio Catagbacan, to be known as Barrio Gatagbacan;
  8. Sitio Halas, to be known as Barrio Halas;
  9. Sitio Fatima, to be known as Barrio Fatima;
  10. Sitio Iligay, to be known as Barrio Iligay;
  11. Sitio Baga-Buga to be known as Barrio Baga-Buga;
  12. Sitio Capinyahan, to be known as Barrio Capinyahan;
  13. Sitio Poblacion Norte, to be known as Barrio Poblacion Norte; and
  14. Sitio Poblaeion Sur, to be known as Barrio Poblacion Sur.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19 1971
