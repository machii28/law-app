Republic Act No. 2866
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE APPROPRIATION OF TWO THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED PESOS FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF A ONE-ROOM PREFABRICATED SCHOOL BUILDING IN AYAHAG, CAJIDIOCAN, ROMBLON, UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED AND THIRTEEN, TO CANTAGDA OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Priority 31, for the Province of Romblon, under School Building Schedules, Appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred and thirteen, is amended to read as follows:
"31 Cajidiocan............
Cantagda..............Construction of one room prefabricated.
2,600.00..............Not registered"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
