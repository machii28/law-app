Republic Act No. 1742
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MACROHON, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following barrios are created in the Municipality of Macrohon, Province of Leyte, to be constituted as follows:

Barrio of Ilihan shall be composed of the sitios of Ilihan Proper, Camalig, Sindangan, Gawisan, Guinabonan, Lunang, Mabanga and Danao;

Barrio Villa Jacinta shall be composed of the sitios of Upper Villa Jacinta Proper, Peng-on, Mahayahay, Canodto, Laray, Luan, Santa Rosa and Camangahan; and Barrio Lower Villa Jacinta shall be composed of the sitios of Lower Villa Jacinta Proper, Suba, Central, Ba-bayon, Muldes, Boayahan and Pancil.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
