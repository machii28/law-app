Republic Act No. 2007
June 22, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED, ENTITLED "AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS AND AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED AND TWENTY."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Items (153) and (156), under paragraph "aa. Leyte," section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred are amended to read as follows:
"(153) Construction of Home Economics Building, Central School, Capoocan
P10,000.00"
"(156) Construction of upper Caghalo Barrio School, Carigara
P10,000.00"
SEC. 2. Under paragraph "aa. Leyte," section two of the same Act, the following item, to be known as item (162), is inserted:
"(162) Construction of San Vicente Barrio
School Building, Alang-alang P20,000.00"
SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
