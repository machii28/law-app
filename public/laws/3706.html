Republic Act No. 3706
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GABALDON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VINTAR, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO FLORENTINO CAMAQUIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Gabaldon Elementary School in the Municipality of Vintar, Province of llocos Norte, is hereby changed to Florentino Camaquin Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall lake effect upon its approval.

Approved, .June 22, 1963.
