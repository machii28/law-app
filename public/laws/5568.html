Republic Act No. 5568
June 21, 1969
AN ACT PROVIDING THAT BARRIO SAMPONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, BE HEREAFTER KNOWN AS BARRIO SAN ROQUE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Barrio Sampong in the Municipality of Bayawan, Province of Negros Oriental, shall hereafter be known as Barrio San Roque.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
