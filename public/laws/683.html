Republic Act No. 683
May 9, 1952
AN ACT TO NAME THE BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF LIBAS, MUNICIPALITY OF BANGA, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, "URQUIOLA MEMORIAL SCHOOL."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Libas Barrio School in the barrio of Libas, Municipality of Banga, Province of Capiz, shall hereafter be known as "Urquiola Memorial School."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
