Republic Act No. 3064
June 17, 1961
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF TWENTY THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE PURCHASE OF THE COPYRIGHT OF THE BOOK, ENTITLED "MAGSAYSAY, THE KNIGHT OF THE MASSES," BY JOSE TOPACIO NUENO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of twenty thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the purchase of the copyright of the book, entitled "Magsaysay, the Knight of the Masses," by Jose Topacio Nueno.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1961.
