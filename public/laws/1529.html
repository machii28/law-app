Republic Act No. 1529
June 16, 1956
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM 1, UNDER "ad. MINDORO OCCIDENTAL," SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED AND ELEVEN, ENTITLED "AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 1, under "ad. Mindoro Occidental," section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred and eleven, is amended to read as follows:

"1. Wawa Barrio School Building, Abra de Ilog, Construction P4,500.00"


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
