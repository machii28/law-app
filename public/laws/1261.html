Republic Act No. 1261
June 10, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF OTEIZA, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO, TO MARIHATAG.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Oteiza in the Province of Surigao is hereby changed to Marihatag.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
