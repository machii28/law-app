Republic Act No. 4297
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CAMAYSA PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF DAPDAP, MUNICIPALITY OF TAYABAS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO DAPDAP PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Camaysa Primary School in the Barrio of Dapdap, Municipality of Tayabas, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Dapdap Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
