Republic Act No. 2151
April 24, 1959
AN ACT TO PROVIDE THAT THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGLIPAY, NUEVA V1ZCAYA, BE LOCATED AT GUINALBIN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The seat of government of the Municipality of Aglipay, Nueva Vizcaya, shall be located at Guinalbin.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
