Republic Act No. 5579
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO SARAWAGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Sarawagan in Barrio Balakan, Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Sarawagan. The barrio of Sarawagan shall be bounded as follows:
  1. North—Starting from the corner of Lot No. 4014- 4013-2891-4005-3213-3212-3211, 3294-S291-3290-3270 and 3268;

  2. East—Salug River and Guisapong Creek;

  3. South—Starting from corner of Lot 2908, going clockwise proceed to Lot No. 2895, 2907, 2906, 2905, 2904, 2903, 3362, 3363, 3409, 3405, and 3404;

  4. West—Coyong and Sarawagan Creeks.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
