Republic Act No. 7704
May 5, 1994
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN PASCUAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN PASCUAL, MUNICIPALITY OF BASUD, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO PEDRO S. RADA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the San Pascual Elementary School in Barangay San Pascual, Municipality of Basud, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Pedro S. Rada Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

Lapsed into law on May 5, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
