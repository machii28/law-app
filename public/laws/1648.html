Republic Act No. 1648
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF WAWA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF WAWA, MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO DAVID JIMENEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Wawa Elementary School in the barrio of Wawa, Municipality of Rosario, Province of Cavite, is changed to David Jimenez Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
