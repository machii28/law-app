Republic Act No. 7237
March 19, 1992
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CULASI EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CULASI, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Culasi Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Culasi, Province of Antique, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be charged against the lump-sum appropriations authorized for the purpose under the current General Appropriations Act. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Approved, March 19, 1992.
