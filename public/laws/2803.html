Republic Act No. 2803
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALIBON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Talibon, Province of Bohol, are declared as barrios of said municipality, to wit:
  1. Sitio Nueva Esperanza as Barrio Esperanza;
  2. Sitio Nueva Estrella as Barrio Estrella;
  3. Sitio Mandawa as Barrio Mandawa;
  4. Sitio Malingin as Barrio Malingin;
  5. Sitio Maomawan as Barrio Maomawan;
  6. Sitio Sagasa as Barrio Sagasa;
  7. Sitio Burgos as Barrio Burgos;
  8. Sitio Rizal as Barrio Rizal;
  9. Sitio Tanghaligue as Barrio Tanghaligue;
  10. Sitio Mahanay as Barrio Mahanay;
  11. Sitio Hebale as Barrio Hebale;
  12. Sitio Santo Niño as Barrio Santo Niño;
  13. Sitio Santa Fe as Barrio Santa Fe;
  14. Sitio Cantubod as Barrio Cantubod;
  15. Sitio Remedios as Barrio Remedios;
  16. Sitio Dagohoy as Barrio Dagohoy;
  17. Sitio Zamora as Barrio Zamora;
  18. Sitio San Roque as Barrio San Roque;
  19. Sitio Sikatuna as Barrio Sikatuna;
  20. Sitio Magsaysay as Barrio Magsaysay;
  21. Sitio San Jose Sur as Barrio San Jose Sur;
  22. Sitio Balintawak as Barrio Balintawak; and
  23. Sitio Nonocnocan as Barrio Nonocnocan.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval June 19, 1960.
