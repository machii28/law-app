Republic Act No. 3718
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CADLAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO RODRIGUEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Cadlan Elementary School in the Municipality of Pili, Province of Camarines Sur, is changed to Rodriguez Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
