Republic Act No. 1945
June 22, 1957
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE ABELLANA VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF CEBU INTO A NATIONAL VOCATIONAL SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS ABELLANA NATIONAL VOCATIONAL SCHOOL AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Abellana Vocational High School in the City of Cebu is hereby converted into a national vocational school to be known as the Abellana National Vocational School.

SEC. 2. The sum of three hundred fifty thousand pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the purposes of this Act during the current fiscal year. Thereafter, the necessary amount for the operation of said school shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
