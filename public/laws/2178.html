Republic Act No. 2178
May 2, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF FAIRE, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Calassitan, Nagbayugan and Cabalansaan in the Municipality of Faire, province of Cagayan, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Cabassitan, Nagbayugan and Cabalansaan, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 2, 1959.
