Republic Act No. 3375
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MAURINGON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ARACELI, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Mauringon in the Municipality of Araceli, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Mauringon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
