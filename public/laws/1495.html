Republic Act No. 1495
June 16, 1956
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF DANAWAN, MUNICIPALITY OF CUYO, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, INTO A BARRIO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Danawan, Municipality of Cuyo Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Danawan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
