Republic Act No. 3781
June 22, 1963
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NATIONAL RURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVERIA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CLAVERIA NATIONAL RURAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Director of Public Schools, a national rural high school in the Municipality of Claveria, Province of Misamis Oriental, to be known as the Claveria National Rural High School

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-four. There-after, such sum as may be needed for its operation and Maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
