June 18, 1966
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES TO SELL PORTIONS OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT TO THE VISAYAS-MINDANAO CONFEDERATION OF TRADE UNIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. SEC. 2. This conveyance is made for the exclusive purpose of enabling the Visayas-Mindanao Confederation of Trade Unions or any subsidiary under its control to achieve its aims and purposes in promoting the welfare of its members and the working people in general.

SEC. 3. The title of the land conveyed by this Act shall not be transferred by the said organization to another person without previous authorization by the President of the Republic of the Philippines.

SEC. 4. In case the Visayas-Mindanao Confederation of Trade Unions is dissolved, or ceases to exist, or for any reason no longer needs the land, or the land is used for purposes other than using it as the site for their Regional Headquarters, the said land shall automatically revert to the National Government together with all the improvements introduced thereon without any compensation.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
