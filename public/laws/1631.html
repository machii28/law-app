Republic Act No. 1631
June 20, 1957
AN ACT INCREASING TO TWO HUNDRED THE NUMBER OF LETTER CARRIERS IN THE CITY OF MANILA AND GRANTING THEM AN ALLOWANCE OF ONE PESO PER DAY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be in the City of Manila two hundred letter carriers who shall each be paid an allowance of one peso per day.

SEC. 2. There is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of ninety thousand pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, to carry out the provisions of this Act. Such sums as thereafter may be needed for the same purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
