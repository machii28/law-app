Republic Act No. 2086
June 14, 1958
AN ACT APPROPRIATING ANNUALLY THE SUM OF ONE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS AS AID TO THE MUSIC PROMOTION FOUNDATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated annually, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of one hundred thousand pesos as aid of the Government to the Music Promotion Foundation in the prosecution of the objectives of the said Foundation.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1958.
