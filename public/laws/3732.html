Republic Act No. 3732
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CONVERTING THE APARRI VOCATIONAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF APARRI, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, INTO A SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES TO BE KNOWN AS THE APARRI SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Aparri Vocational School in the Municipality of Aparri, Province of Cagayan, is hereby converted into a school of arts and trades to be known as the Aparri School of Arts and Trades. Courses of collegiate level shall be offered in said school.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize said school accordingly.

SEC. 3. The sum of seventy thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds of the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-four. Thereafter, such sum as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
