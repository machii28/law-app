Republic Act No. 5027
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF NORTHERN PADADA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF GUIHING, MUNICIPALITY OF HAGONOY, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO GUIHING ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in- Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Northern Padada Elementary School in the barrio of Guihing, Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Davao, is hereby changed to Ginning Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
