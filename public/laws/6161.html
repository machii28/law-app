Republic Act No. 6161
November 9, 1970
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LALIG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIAONG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO DOÑA CONCEPCION H. UMALI ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Lalig Elementary School in the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Doña Concepcion H. Umali Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, November 9, 1970.
