Republic Act No. 5509
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CONVERTING THE LANAO DEL NORTE PROVINCIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAROY, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE, INTO A NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Lanao del Norte Provincial High School in the Municipality of Baroy, Province of Lanao del Norte, is hereby converted into a national comprehensive high school to be known as the Lanao del Norte National Comprehensive High School.

SEC. 2. The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-nine. Thereafter, after such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969
