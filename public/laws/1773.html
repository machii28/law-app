Republic Act No. 1773
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Aramaywan, Isugod, Tabon, Sawangan, Calumpang, Campong-Ulay, Ransang, Cadawaga, Culasian, Panalingaan, Taburi, Latud, and Canipaan in the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Palawan, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Aramaywan, Isugod, Tabon, Sawangan Calumpang, Campong-Ulay, Ransang, Cadawaga, Culasian Panalingaan, Tabura, Latud, and Canipaan, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
