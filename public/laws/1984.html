Republic Act No. 1984
June 22, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (18) UNDER "N. CAPIZ," SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (18) under "n. Capiz," section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
"18. Centro Barrio School Building, Dao
Construction P5.000.00."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
