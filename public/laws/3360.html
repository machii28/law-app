Republic Act No. 3360
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO CAMOGTONG, MUNICIPALITY OF DESPUJOLS, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON, TO MABINI.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Barrio Camogtong in the Municipality o'f Despujols, Province of Romblon, shall hereafter be known as Barrio Mabini.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
