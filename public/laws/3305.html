Republic Act No. 3305
June 18, 1961
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (u), PAGE TWO HUNDRED EIGHTY-ONE, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (u), page two hundred eighty-one, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(u) Lambunao Municipal building", construction, improvement and/or repair
P25,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
