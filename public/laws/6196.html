Republic Act No. 6196
June 19, 1971
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF ILOCOS NORTE PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of llocos Norte Provincial Hospital is hereby increased from fifty to one hundred fifty beds.

SEC. 2. Such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance and other facilities shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
