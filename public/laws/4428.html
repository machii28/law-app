Republic Act No. 4428
June 19, 1965
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE PAMPLONA NATIONAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established under the direct supervision of the Director of the Bureau of Vocational Education, a school of fisheries in the Municipality of Pamplona, Province of Cagayan, to be known as the Pamplona National School of Fisheries. The site for the said school shall be determined by the Director of the Bureau of Vocational Education in consultation with the Commissioner of Fisheries.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the said school for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-six. Such sums as may be necessary for its operation and Maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
