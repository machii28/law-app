Republic Act No. 3759
June 22, 1963
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE WESTERN PANGASINAN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALAMINOS, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Western Pangasinan Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Alaminos, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby increased to fifty beds.

SEC. 2. The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds of the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
