Republic Act No. 5437
September 9, 1968
AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO PATRICIA V. ROSAL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Patricia V. Rosal is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, September 9, 1968.
