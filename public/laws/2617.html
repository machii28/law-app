Republic Act No. 2617
March 15, 1960
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (E), PAGE 212, SUB-PARAGRAPH (6), FOR THE PROVINCE OF BATAAN, PARAGRAPH F, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (e), page 212, subparagraph (6), for the Province of Bataan, paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is hereby amended to read as follows:
"(e) Pantalan Luma-Wawa Streets,

Orani
Improvement P2,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, March 15,
