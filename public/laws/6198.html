Republic Act No. 6198
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO NAPILAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Napilan in the poblacion of the Municipality of Rizal, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is separated therefrom, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio in said municipality, to be known as Barrio Napilan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
