Republic Act No. 5567
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUNAWAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in. Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of San Andrea and Ko-o in the Municipality of Bunawan, Province of Agusan, are hereby converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as barrios San Andres and San Rafael, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
