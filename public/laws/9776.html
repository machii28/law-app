Republic Act No. 9776
November 17, 2009
AN ACT DECLARING AUGUST 10 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN SAN JOSE CITY, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. August 10 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the City of San Jose, Province of Nueva Ecija to commemorate its creatin as a city.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE
