Republic Act No. 956
June 20, 1953
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM NUMBERED TWENTY-TWO, UNDER "BATANGAS", SUBSECTION (a), HEADING "A. MISCELLANEOUS," SECTION ONE, OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIX HUNDRED AND SEVENTY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item numbered twenty-two, under "Batangas," subsection (a), heading "A. Miscellaneous," section one of Republic Act Numbered Six hundred and seventy, is amended to read as follows:
"22. Repair of Tala Elementary School Building, Nasugbu
P500.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
