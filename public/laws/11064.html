Republic Act No. 11064
August 23, 2018
AN ACT RENAMING PANGLAO ISLAND CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD TRAVERSING BARANGAYS TANGNAN, BIL-ISAN, LOOC, POBLACION, DANAO, TAWALA, BOLOD AND LIBAONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANGLAO AND BARANGAY TOTOLAN, SONGCULAN, TABALONG, BINGAG, DAO, SAN ISIDRO, BIKING 1, BIKING 2, CATARMAN, MAYACABAC, POBLACION AND UNION IN THEMUNICIPALITY OF DAUIS, AS ANOS FONACIER CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD (DAUIS-PANGLAO)
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the remarkable achievements and contributions of the late Anos Fonacier in the development of Bohol's tourism, the Panglao Island Circumferential Road which traverses barangays Tangnan, Bil-isan, Looc, Poblacion, Danao, Tawala, Bolod and Libaong in the Municipality of Panglao and barangays Totolan, Songculan, Tabalong, Bingag, dao, San Isidro, Biking 1, Biking 2, Catarman, Mayacabac, Poblacion and Union in the Municipality of Dauis, is hereby renamed as Anos Fonacier Circumferential Road (Dauis-Panglao).

SEC. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways is hereby directed to implement the provision of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in any newspaper of general circulation.
