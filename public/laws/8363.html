Republic Act No. 8363
October 9, 1997
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TUROD NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN CABUGAO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO CABUGAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Turod National High School located at Barangay Turod, Municipality of Cabugao, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Cabugao National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, Lapsed into law on October 9, 1997, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
