Republic Act No. 7799
August 26, 1994
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TALISAY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a district hospital in the Municipality of Talisay, Province of Cebu, to be known as the Talisay District Hospital.

SEC. 2. The Department of Health, in consultation and coordination with local government officials concerned, shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary for the establishment of the hospital shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following the enactment of this Act into law. Thereafter, funds necessary for its maintenance and operation shall be provided in accordance with the provision of the Local Government Code.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, August 26, 1994.
