Republic Act No. 2851
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OP BANTOG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Bantog in the Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bulacan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Ban tog.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
