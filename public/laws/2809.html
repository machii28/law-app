Republic Act No. 2809
June 19, 1960
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SCHOOL OF NURSING AND MIDWIFERY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATARMAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, TO BE ATTACHED TO THE NORTHERN SAMAR GENERAL HOSPITAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the supervision of the Director of Medical Services, a school of nursing and midwifery in the Municipality of Catarman, Province of Samar, to be attached to the Northern Samar General Hospital.

SEC. 2. The Director of Medical Services shall, with the approval of the Secretary of Health, issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry into effect the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said school.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
