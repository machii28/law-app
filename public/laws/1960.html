Republic Act No. 1960
June 22, 1957
AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A BREEDING STATION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOBON, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a breeding station in the Municipality of Bobon, Province of Samar, under the supervision of the Director of Animal Industry.

SEC. 2. The Director of Animal Industry shall appoint such personnel as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.

SEC. 3. Such sum as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act is hereby appropriated, out of the funds of the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said station for the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred fifty-seven.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
