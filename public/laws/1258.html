Republic Act No. 1258
June 10, 1955
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF LIBERTAD, FORMERLY KNOWN AS BARRIO UMABAY EXTERIOR, MUNICIPALITY OF MOBO, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, TO SAN GREGORIO.
Be it enacted by the Semite and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the eminent and patriotic services rendered by the late Gregorio Castillo, the name of the barrio of Libertad, Province of Masbate, is changed to San Gregorio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
