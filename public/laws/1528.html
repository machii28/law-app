Republic Act No. 1528
June 16, 1956
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF BAGONGBAYAN IN THE BARRIO OF ILIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, INTO A BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Bagongbayan in the Barrio of Ilian, Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the Barrio of Bagongbayan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
