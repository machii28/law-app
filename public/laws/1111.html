Republic Act No. 1111
June 15, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUMARAN, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO ARACELI.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the municipality of Dumaran in the Province of Palawan is changed to Araceli.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1954.
