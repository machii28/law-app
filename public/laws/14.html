Republic Act No. 14
September 7, 1946
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE PROVINCE OF TAYABAS TO QUEZON
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the eminent and patriotic services rendered to the country by President Quezon, the name of the Province of Tayabas is hereby changed to Quezon.

SEC. 2. All laws inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, September 7, 1946.
