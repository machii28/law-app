Republic Act No. 5392
June 15, 1968
AN ACT AMENDING SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FORTY HUNDRED TWENTY


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section one of Republic Act Numbered Forty hundred twenty is hereby amended to read as follows:
" SECTION 1. There is hereby granted to Virginia Development Corporation (VIRDECOR), its successors or assigns, a temporary permit to construct, maintain and operate within the Philippines, subject to the approval of Secretary of Public Works and Communications, private fixed point-to-point and land-based and land-mobile radio stations for the reception and transmission of wire-messages on radiotelegraphy or radiotelephony, each station to be provided with a radio transmitting apparatus and a radio receiving apparatus."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
