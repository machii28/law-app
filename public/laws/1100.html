Republic Act No. 1100
June 15, 1954
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF HINANGAYON, MOGPOG, MARINDUQUE, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF HINANGAYON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Hinangayon, barrio of Argao, Municipality of Mogpog, Province of Marinduque, is hereby into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Hinangayon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1954.
