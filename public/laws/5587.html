Republic Act No. 5587
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO BATULI, MUNICIPALITY OF PASUQUIN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO SAN ISIDRO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Batuli in the Municipality of Pasuquin, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio San Isidro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
