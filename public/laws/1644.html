Republic Act No. 1644
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CAINBUÑGAN BARRIO SCHOOL, MUNICIPALITY OF CADIZ, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO LUIS CONSING MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Cainbuñgan Barrio School, Municipality of Cadiz, Province of Negros Occidental, is changed to Luis Consing Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
