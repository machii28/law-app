Republic Act No. 4509
June 19, 1965
AN ACT TO AMEND A CERTAIN ITEM IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTY-EIGHT HUNDRED AND FORTY-SIX REGARDING THE CONSTRUCTION OF A HYDRO-ELECTRIC PLANT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BADIAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 3, page 510, Listing B, Group II, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-eight hundred and forty-six (Short-term Rural Progress Projects Under Socio-Economic Program), is amended to read as follows:
"3. Hydro-Electric Plant, Barrio Basak, Badian.
Construction, improvement and/or
repair ............................................................. P205,000.00."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
