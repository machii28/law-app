Republic Act No. 4101
June 19, 1964
AN ACT CONVERTING ROXAS MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN ROXAS CITY INTO A NATIONAL GENERAL HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS ROXAS MEMORIAL GENERAL HOSPITAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Roxas Memorial Hospital in Roxas City is converted into a national general hospital with seventy-five-bed capacity to be known as Roxas Memorial General Hospital.

SEC. 2. The sum of four hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for its operation, maintenance and physical expansion facilities during fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five. Such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1964.
