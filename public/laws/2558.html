Republic Act No. 2558
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING BARRIOS IN THE PROVINCE OF ISABELA
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Isabela, are constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, as follows: Sitios Salay and Narien to Barrio Salay.

SEC. 2. The following sitios in the Municipality of Tumauini, Province of Isabela, are constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, as follows:
Sitios Sical and Cabal to Barrio Namnama.
SEC. 3. The sitio of Puroc No. 6, Salinuñgan, in the Municipality of San Mateo, Province of Isabela, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Salinuñgan East.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
