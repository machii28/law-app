Republic Act No. 7494
May 13, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN FABIAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN ECHAGUE, ISABELA, TO ECHAGUE EAST CENTRAL SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the San Fabian Elementary School in Echague, Isabela, is hereby changed to Echague East Central School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on April 5, 1990 and December 11, 1991, respectively.
