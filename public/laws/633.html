Republic Act No. 633
June 8, 1951
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANTATET IN THE PROVINCE OF ISABELA TO LUNA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the municipality of Antatet, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to Luna.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 8, 1951.
