Republic Act No. 9118
April 19, 2001
AN ACT DECLARING THE FIRST DAY OF MARCH OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MUNTINLUPA TO BE KNOWN AS THE MUNTINLUPA CITY CHARTER DAY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March 1 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the City of Muntinlupa to be known as "Muntinlupa City Charter Day" in order to commemorate the Charter Anniversary of the said city.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) AQUILINO Q. PIMENTEL JR.
(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR.
