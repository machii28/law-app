Republic Act No. 6667
June 22, 1988
AN ACT RENAMING THE JANIUAY NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL, IN MUNICIPALITY OF JANIUAY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE DON TIBURSIO A. LUTERO NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Janiuay National Comprehensive High School in Municipality of Janiuay, Province of Iloilo is hereby changed to Don Tibursio A. Lutero National Comprehensive High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA
(Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate
Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representative was finally passed by the Senate and the House of Representatives on May 27, 1988 and April 28, 1988 respectively.
