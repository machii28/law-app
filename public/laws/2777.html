Republic Act No. 2777
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BABALLASIOAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA MARIA, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BARRIO PINSAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Baballasioan in the Municipality of Santa Maria, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Barrio Pinsal.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
