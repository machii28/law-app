Republic Act No. 4275
June 19, 1965
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A RURAL HEALTH CENTER IN CARABAO ISLAND, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON, TO BE KNOWN AS CARABAO ISLAND RURAL HEALTH CENTER AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established under the supervision of the Director of Health Services, a rural health center in Carabao Island, Province of Romblon, to be known as Carabao Island Rural Health Center.

SEC. 2. The sum of thirty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said health center during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-six. Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
