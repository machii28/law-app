Republic Act No. 2797
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF STO. NIÑO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALANG, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Balas in the Municipality of Magalang, Province of Pampanga, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Sto. Niño.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
