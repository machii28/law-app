Republic Act No. 9463
May 15, 2007
AN ACT NAMING THE SAN NICOLAS—SOLSONA PORTION OF THE ILOCOS NORTE—APAYAO ROAD AS THE JOSEFA LLANES ESCODA NATIONAL HIGHWAY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. This Act shall be known as the "Josefa Llanes Escoda National Highway Act".

SEC. 2. The San Nicolas—Solsona portion of the Ilocos Norte—Apayao Road starting from Km. Post 484+000 to Km. Post 524+000 in the Province of Ilocos Norte is hereby named as the Josefa Llanes Eecoda National Highway.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
