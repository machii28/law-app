Republic Act No. 1688
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF TAUG NORTE, BARRIO OF TAUG, MUNICIPALITY OF LILA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, INTO BARRIO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF LA FORTUNA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Taug Norte, barrio of Taug, Municipality of Lila, Province of Bohol, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of La Fortuna.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
