Republic Act No. 6219
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BIBIHAN SCHOOL IN BARRIO PUTING-TUBIG, MUNICIPALITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO PEDRO C. TOLENTINO MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Bibihan School, in Barrio Puting-Tubig, Municipality of Calapan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Pedro C. Tolentino Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
