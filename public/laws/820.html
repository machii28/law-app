SEC. 2. A committee composed of three Senators and three Representatives to be appointed by the presiding officers of each House, respectively, is hereby constituted to look into the allotments and expenditures of the "Counterpart Fund" and the aid which the Philippines has already received and will receive from the United States Mutual Security Agency. The said Committee is authorized to function during the recess of the Congress and shall submit its reports of findings and recommendations to the Congress.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, August 14, 1952.
