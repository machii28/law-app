Republic Act No. 10474
April 16, 2013
AN ACT SEPARATING THE ESTEBAN MADRONA MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL – BINONGAAN ANNEX IN BARANGAY BINONGAAN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN AGUSTIN, PROVINCE OF ROMBLONFROM THE ESTEBAN MADRONA MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL, CLONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BA KNOWN AS EDUARDO M. MORENO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING ACT FUNDS THEREFOR
Be enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Separation and Conversion into a National High School. – The Esteban Madrona Memorial High School – Binongaan Annex in Barangay Binongaan, Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Romblon is hereby Separated from the Esteban Madrona Memorial High School and converted into an independent national high school to be known as Eduardo M. Moreno National High School.

SEC. 2. Transfer of Assets and Lialibilities. – All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Esteban Madrona Memorial High School – Binongaan Annex are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Eduardo M. Moreno National High School.

SEC. 3. Appropriations. – The Secretary of Education shall immediately include the Department’s program the operationalization of the Eduardo M. Moreno National High School, the initial funding of which shall be charge against the current year’s appropriations of the Esteban Madrona Memorial High School – Binangonan Annex. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. Implementation. – The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations that may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 5. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
