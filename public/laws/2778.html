Republic Act No. 2778
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAGNAY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Sagnay, Province of Camarines Sur, are constituted as distinct and independent barrios of said municipality, to wit:

  1. The sitios of Aniog and Bolo, to be known as the barrio of Aniog; and
  2. The sitios of Bognalon, Suboc, Oyagno, Amislag and Cagbalogo, to be known as the barrio of Bognalon.

SEC. 2 This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
