Republic Act No. 3978
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MANGCRUZ-PAMORANGON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DAET, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO GREGORIO PIMENTEL MEMORIAL SCHOOL
.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Mangcruz-Pamorangon Elementary School in the Municipality of Daet, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Gregorio Pimentel Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
