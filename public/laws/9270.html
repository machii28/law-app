Republic Act No. 9270
March 19, 2004
AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL ROAD FROM THE INTERSECTION OF GOVERNOR CAMINS ROAD AND VETERANS AVENUE UP TO LICOMO IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA TO MARIA CLARA L. LOBREGAT HIGHWAY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The portion of the national road starting from the intersection of Governor Camins Road and Veterans Avenue up to Licomo in the City of Zamboanga is hereby named Mana Clara L. Lobregat Highway.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON (Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR.
President of the Senate Speaker of the House Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 21, 2004 and January 19, 2004, respectively.
