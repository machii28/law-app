Republic Act No. 6434
June 17, 1972
AN ACT REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOUR THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED SIXTY, AND RESTORING THE OLD NAME LAKE LANAO IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Republic Act Numbered Four thousand two hundred sixty is hereby repealed and the old name Lake Lanao in the Province of Lanao del Sur is hereby restored.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1972.
