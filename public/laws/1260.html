Republic Act No. 1260
June 10, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF VISITA STREET IN THE DISTRICT OF PACO, CITY OF MANILA, TO BURGOS STREET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Visita Street in the District of Paco, City of Manila, is hereby changed to Burgos Street.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
