Republic Act No. 4190
May 4, 1965
AN ACT DECLARING CERTAIN PLACES IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR AS NATIONAL PARKS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following places in the Province of Lanao del Sur are hereby declared as national parks:
  1. Rungkiman Park, located in the Municipality of Ramain;
  2. Lake Dapao, located in the Municipality of Pualas;
  3. Lake Butig, located in the Municipality of Butig;
  4. Sacred Mountain together with all the lands therein, located in Marawi City;
  5. Salikata Park, located in the Municipality of Lumba Bayabao; and
  6. Pantuwaraya Lake, located in Saguiaran.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 4, 1965.
