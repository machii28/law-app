Republic Act No. 5331
June 15, 1968
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF ONE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE THOUSAND PESOS TO IMPLEMENT REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FORTY-TWO HUNDRED SEVENTY-THREE AND FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE SALARIES OF PERSONNEL (re San Joaquin Breeding Station, Iloilo).


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated., out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to implement Republic Act Numbered Forty-two hundred seventy-three, and the sum of twenty-five thousand pesos is likewise appropriated for the payment of the salaries of personnel for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-nine. Such sum as may needed for the operation of the San Joaquin Breeding Station in the Municipality of San Joaquin, Province of Iloilo, in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
