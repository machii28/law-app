Republic Act No. 2109
April 3, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF POSCOLON IN THE BARRIO OF CALAIGANG, MUNICIPALITY OF BAROTAC VIEJO, PROVINCE OF ILOILO INTO A BARRIO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF POSCOLON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Poscolon in the barrio of Calaigang, Municipality of Barotac Viejo, Province of Iloilo, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Poscolon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 3, 1959
