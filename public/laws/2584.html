Republic Act No. 2584
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF THE BARRIO OF CROSSING MACASILAO OR BARRIO YONSON IN THE' MUNICIPALITY OF CALATRAVA, AND THE BARRIO OF BINABONGOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ESCALANTE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO BARRIO CASTELLANO AND BARRIO MAGSAYSAY, RESPECTIVELY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Crossing Macasilao or Barrio Yonson in the Municipality of Calatrava, Province of Negros Occidental, is changed to Barrio Castellano.

SEC. 2. The name of the barrio of Binobongol in the Municipality of Escalante, same province, is changed to Barrio Magsaysay.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
