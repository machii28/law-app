Republic Act No. 2221
May 16, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MANYAYA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MURCIA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Manyaya in the barrio of Sta. Rosa, Municipality of Murcia, Province of Negros Occidental, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Manyaya.

The boundary of the proposed barrio of Manyaya and the barrio of Sta. Rosa, Municipality of Murcia, Province of Negros Occidental, shall be the Puncian River.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 16, 1959.
