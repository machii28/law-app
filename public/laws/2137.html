Republic Act No. 2137
April 7, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BAGACAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAPINIG, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Kajaguekjekan and Lobok in the Municipality of Lapinig, Province of Samar, are reby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bagacay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
