Republic Act No. 6530
July 22, 1972
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO TJ UPPER TINALIAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ABUYOG, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Cabatangan in the Municipality of Abuyog, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio TJ Upper Tinalian.

The proposed barrio shall be bounded as follows:
"On the North, from Cabato-an up to Tinalian Creek; on the East, from Cabato-an up to Cancaba-bat; on the South, from Big Lauan Tree up to Cancababat; and on the West, from Tinalian Creek: up to Cabatangan and the Big Lauan Tree."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without the Executive approval, July 22, 1972.
