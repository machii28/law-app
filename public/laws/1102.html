Republic Act No. 1102
June 15, 1954
AN ACT FIXING THE BOUNDARY LINE BETWEEN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUM AND THE MUNICIPALITIES OF DOÑA ALICIA AND COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The boundary line between the municipalities of Tagum and Doña Alicia shall be the Dumlan River from the Gulf of Davao to the source of said river and a straight line from the said source running northeastward to the concrete monument in the sitio of Libaylibay. The boundary line between the municipalities of Tagum and Compostela shall be the Hijo River.

SEC. 2. Executive Order No. 596, series of 1953 is amended accordingly.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1954.
