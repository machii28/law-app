Republic Act No. 3359
June 18, 1961
AN ACT DIVIDING BARRIO LIMON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOOC, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON, INTO TWO BARRIOS TO BE KNOWN AS LIMON NORTE AND LIMON SUR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Barrio Limon in the Municipality of Looc, Province of Romblon, is hereby divided into two barrios to be known as Limon Norte and Limon Sur. Barrio Limon Norte shall comprise the sitios of Cogon, Polacon, Guintigui-an, Lumandong, Bongan-bongan, Ma-init, Boñgoran Norte, Bagacota, Bataha, Narontan, Cayhagan and Agbayang, and Barrio Limon Sur, the sitios of Bongan-bongan Sur, Lumbia, Baiyan, Grando, Silagon, Canduroy, pusdan and Ambolong.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
