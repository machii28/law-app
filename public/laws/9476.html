Republic Act No. 9476
May 22, 2007
AN ACT RENAMING THE ENTIRE PORTION OF GEN. TRIAS — AMADEO — TAGAYTAY ROAD FROM GOVERNOR'S DRIVE TO TAGAYTAY CITY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE TO CRISANTO M. DE LOS REYES AVENUE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In due recognition of the generous contributions and unending support of Crisanto M. De Los Reyes in the patriotic cause of out Filipino soldiers in Cavite during the Spanish occupation, the Gen. Trias — Amadeo — Tagaytay Road from Governor's Drive to Tagaytay City is hereby renamed as Crisanto M. De Los Reyes Avenue.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate


This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on June 6, 2005 and February 19, 2007, respectively.
