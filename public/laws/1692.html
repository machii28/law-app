Republic Act No. 1692
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF TUBOG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO SAN GABRIEL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Tubog in the Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to San Gabriel.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
