Republic Act No. 2841
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PARAGOS COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYAMBANG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO PARAGOS-SAN GABRIEL II COMMUNITY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Paragos Community School in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby changed to Paragos-San Gabriel II Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
