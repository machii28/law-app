Republic Act No. 4023
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CONVERTING THE DIFFUN MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIF-FUN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, INTO A NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE CALLED NORTHERN NUEVA VIZCAYA NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Diffun Municipal High School in the Municipality of Diffun, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, is hereby converted into a national agricultural high school to be called Northern Nueva Vizcaya National Agricultural High School. ,

SEC. 2. The Director of Vocational Education shall reorganize the said school accordingly.

SEC. 3. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five. Thereafter the sums necessary therefor shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
