Republic Act No. 6709
February 10, 1989
AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER FIVE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November five of every year is hereby declared a special non-working holiday in the Province of Negros Occidental.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House
of Representatives
