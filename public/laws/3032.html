Republic Act No. 3032
June 17, 1961
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (21), PAGE 208, TITLE (A) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED AND THIRTEEN.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (21), page 208, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred and thirteen is amended to read as follows:

"(21) Dumangas Municipal Building, Iloilo
P20,000
"(21a) Dumangas Public Market, Iloilo
40,000
"(21b) Dumangas Public Plaza or Parks, Iloilo
20,000.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1961.
