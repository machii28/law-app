Republic Act No. 2401
June 21, 1959
AN ACT REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED SEVENTY-FIVE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Repesentatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred seventy-five, entitled “An act to grant to Amador Dayola and R. Mulet (partners) a franchise for an electric light, heat and power in the Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao,” is hereby repealed.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
