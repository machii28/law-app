Republic Act No. 4454
June 19, 1965
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF SAN ANTONIO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF CAVITE TO JULIAN FELIPE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the eminent and patriotic services rendered to the country by the late Julian Felipe, the name of San Antonio Elementary School in the City of Cavite is hereby changed to Julian Felipe Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
