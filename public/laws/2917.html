Republic Act No. 2917
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF MALUSAK IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOGPOG, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE, INTO A BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Malusak in the Municipality of Mogpog, Province of Marinduque, is converted into a barrio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. Enacted without

Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
