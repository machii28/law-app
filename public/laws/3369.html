Republic Act No. 3369
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONIFACIO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following places are constituted or declared as distinct and independent barrios in the Municipality of Bonifacio, Province of Misamis Occidental, to Wit:

1. The sitios of Tinago, P. Valencia and Digson, to be known as the barrio of Poblacion;
2. The sitios of Lower Calolot and Upper Calolot, to be known as the barrio of Calolot;
3. The sitios of Tusik East, Tusik West, Pinacio and Burnao, to be known as the barrio of Tusik;
4. The sitios of Tiaman East, Tiaman West, Tiaman Napo and Gaga, to be known as the barrio of Tiaman;
5. The sitio of Linconan, to be known as the barrio of Linconan;
6. The sitio of Osugan Centro, to be known as the barrio of Upper Osugan;
7. The sitios of Dullan Centro and Lower Dullan, to be known as the barrio of Dullan;
8. The sitio of Kanao-kanao, to be known as the barrio of Kanao-kanao;. 9. The sitios of Upper Dimalinao and Mayamaya, to be known as the barrio of Maramara;
10.The sitios of Lower Bolinsong, Upper Bolinsong, Mitan-ag and Corporacion, to be known as the barrio of Bolinsong; ,
11.The sitios of Upper Libato, Lower Bag-ong Anonang and Upper Bag-ong- Anonang, to be known as the barrio of Bag-ong Anonang;
12.The sitios of Sapi Bagol, Upper Diwan, Upper Sibukang and Upper Calabat, to be known as the barrio of Bitianan;
13.The sitio of Sibuyon, to be known as the barrio of Sibuyon;
14.The sitio of Upper Lodiong, to be known as the barrio of Tangab; 15.The sitios of Migsali, Kanangkaan and Karangan, to be known as the barrio of Mapurog;
16.The sitio of Diwat, to be known as the barrio of Buenavista;
17.The sitio of Guinatang, to be known as the barrio of Remedios; 18.The sitios of Matun-og and Macalas, to be known as the barrio of Montol;
19.The sitios of Upper Dimalco and Lower Dimalco, to be known as the barrio of Dimalco;
20.The sitios of Upper Pisa-an and Lower Pisa-an, to be known as the barrio of Pisa-an;
21.The sitios of Upper Buracan, Lower Buracan and Dapagan, to be known as the barrio of Buracan;
22.The sitios of Dupol and Baybay, to be known as the barrio of Bagumbang;
23.The sitios of Kabaluran and Pongol, to be known as the barrio of Migpangi;
24.The sitio of Abaga, to be known as the barrio of Liloan; and
25.The sitio of Lower Osugan, to be known as the barrio of Lower Osugan.


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
