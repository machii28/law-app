Republic Act No. 3331
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of San Miguel, Arasan, New Cuyo, Salvacion and Narra in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of San Miguel, Arasan, New Cuyo, Salvacion and Narra, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
