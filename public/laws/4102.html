Republic Act No. 4102
June 19, 1964
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF GUIHULNGAN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATIONS OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of Guihulngan Emergency Hospital is hereby increased from twenty-five to fifty.

SEC. 2. The financing of this hospital shall be in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-nine, otherwise known as the Hospital Financing Act. The necessary sums for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1964.
