Republic Act No. 3549
June 21, 1963
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ABULUG, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Sirit and Canayun in the Municipality of Abulug, Province of Cagayan, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Sirit and Canayun, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1963.
