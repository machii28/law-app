Republic Act No. 3494
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CAMP JAMES ORD IN SAN MIGUEL, TARLAC, TARLAC, TO CAMP GENERAL SERVILLANO AQUINO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Camp James Ord in San Miguel, Tarlac, Tarlac, shall henceforth be known as Camp General Seryillano Aquino.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
