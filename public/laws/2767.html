Republic Act No. 2767
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIARGAO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Sominot in the Municipality of Liargao, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is changed to Oliva.

SEC. 2. The name of the barrio of Lower Malating in the same municipality is changed to Magsaysay.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
