Republic Act No. 4923
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF A CERTAIN BARRIO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, TO BARRIO SAN ISIDRO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Tete in the Municipality of Aroroy, Province of Masbate, is changed to Barrio San Isidro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
