Republic Act No. 2101
March 28, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAYAN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, TO TADIAN MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Kayan in the Mountain Province is changed to Municipality of Tadian.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, March 28, 1959.
