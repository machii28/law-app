Republic Act No. 1536
June 16, 1956
AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF DINGALAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALER, SUBPROVINCE OF AURORA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of Dingalan in the Municipality of Baler, subprovince of Aurora, Province of Quezon, is hereby converted into a municipal district.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
