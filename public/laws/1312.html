Republic Act No. 1312
June 16, 1955
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MADALANIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF WRIGHT, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Madalanit, in the Municipality of Wright, Province of Samar, is hereby constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Madalanit.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
