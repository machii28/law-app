Republic Act No. 5644
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF FAIRE IN THE PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN TO SANTO NIÑO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Faire, in the Province of Cagayan is hereby changed to Santo Niño.

SEC, 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
