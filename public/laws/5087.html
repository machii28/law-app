Republic Act No. 5087
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO PATPAT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON, TO LAPU-LAPU.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Patpat in the Municipality of Malaybalay, Province of Bukidnon, is hereby changed to Lapu-Lapu.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
