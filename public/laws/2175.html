Republic Act No. 2175
April 26, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF LIMBUJAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GENERAL MACARTHUR, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Limbujan in the Municipality of General MacArthur, Province of Samar, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Limbujan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 26, 1959.
