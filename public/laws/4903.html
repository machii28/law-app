Republic Act No. 4903
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF OTIEZA, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, TO SAN AGUSTIN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Otieza in the Province of Surigao del Sur is hereby changed to San Agustin.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1967.
