Republic Act No. 2556
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAMAL, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following places in the Municipality of Samal, Province of Davao, are constituted as barrios of said municipality, to wit:
  1. Anunang as Barrio Anunang;
  2. Aumbay as Barrio Aumbay;
  3. Aundanao as Barrio Aundanao;
  4. Dadatan as Barrio Dadatan;
  5. Del Monte as Barrio Del Monte;
  6. Guilon as Barrio Guilon;
  7. Kana-an as Barrio Kana-an;
  8. Katagman as Barrio Katagman;
  9. Kawag as Barrio Kawag;
  10. Libertad as Barrio Libertad;
  11. Licup as Barrio Licup;
  12. Limao as Barrio Limao;
  13. Linusutan as Barrio Linusutan;
  14. Mambago A as Barrio Mambago A;
  15. New San Remegio as Barrio New San Remegio;
  16. Pangubatan as Barrio Pangubatan;
  17. San Isidro as Barrio San Isidro;
  18. San Miguel as Barrio San Miguel;
  19. Sion as Barrio Sion;
  20. Sta. Cruz as Barrio Sta. Cruz;
  21. Tagbaobo as Barrio Tagbaobo;
  22. Tagbay as Barrio Tagbay;
  23. Tagbitanag as Barrio Tagbitanag; and
  24. x. Tagdaliao as Barrio Tagdaliao.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
