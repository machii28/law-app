Republic Act No. 5621
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BUNAWAN, ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AT BUNAWAN, DAVAO CITY, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO DON DANIEL PEREZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Bunawan Elementary School at Bunawan, Davao City, Province of Davao del Sur, is hereby changed to Don Daniel Perez Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon, its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
