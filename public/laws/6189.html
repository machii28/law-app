Republic Act No. 6189
June 19, 1971
AN ACT SITIO LAPGAP INTO A REGULAR BARRIO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYDOLONG, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO LAPGAP.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Lapgap is hereby converted into a regular barrio in the Municipality of Maydolong, Province of Eastern Samar, to be known as Barrio Lapgap.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
