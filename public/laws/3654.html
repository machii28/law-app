Republic Act No. 3654
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TERESA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ABORLAN, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Catubangan, Bato-Bato and Pinagdogoan in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of Teresa.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
