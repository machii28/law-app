Republic Act No. 7585
May 27, 1992
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MUERTIGUE HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a public high school in Barangay Daja, Municipality of San Isidro, Province of Leyte, to be known as the Muertigue High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 29, 1992 and January 27, 1992, respectively.
