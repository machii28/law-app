Republic Act No. 3303
June 18, 1961
AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-SEVEN HUNDRED AND ONE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Items (p) and (r) under Group III, for the Province of Cebu, page 340, paragraph (c), Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and one are hereby amended to read as follows:
"(p) Carcar Municipal Hall, Carcar Construction
P50,000.00"
"(r) Tungkop Elementary School Building, Minglanilla Repair and remodeling
7,500.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
