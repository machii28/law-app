Republic Act No. 7528
May 21, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SOUTHERN ISLANDS MEDICAL CENTER IN THE CITY OF CEBU TO VICENTE SOTTO MEMORIAL MEDICAL CENTER
Be it enacted by the Senate and House Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Southern Islands Medical Center in the City of Cebu is hereby changed to Vicente Sotto Memorial Medical Center.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on August 1, 1991 and February 3, 1992, respectively.
