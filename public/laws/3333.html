Republic Act No. 3333
June 18, 1961
AN ACT MAKING THE REGISTER OF DEEDS OF THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO EX-OFFICIO REGISTER OF DEEDS FOR THE CITY OF ILOILO, BY AMENDING THE CHARTER OF THE CITY OF ILOILO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby inserted after Section eighty-one, Article X of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and fifty-eight, otherwise known as the Charter of the City of Iloilo, a new section which shall read as follows:

"SEC. 81-A. Register of Deeds.&mdashThe Register of Deeds of the Province of Iloilo shall be ex-officio Register of Deeds of the City of Iloilo and as such shall receive an additional compensation, to be paid out of the city funds, in the sum of one thousand two hundred pesos per annum."


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
