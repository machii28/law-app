Republic Act No. 6703
February 10, 1989
AN ACT RENAMING THE SAN RAFAEL-BOLILAO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SITUATED IN BARANGAY SAN RAFAEL, MUNICIPALITY OF MANDURRIAO, CITY OF ILOILO, TO DONATO PISON MEMORIAL SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The San Rafael-Bolilao Elementary School in Barangay san Rafael, Mandurriao, Iloilo City, shall hereafter be known as the Donato Pison Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House
of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 22, 1988 and December 2, 1988, respectively.
