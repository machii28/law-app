Republic Act No. 1251
June 10, 1955
AN ACT AMENDING SUB-ITEMS (g), (i) AND (j) OF ITEM 37, SUBSECTION (e), TITLE B, AND ITEM 55, GROUP I, SUBSECTION (a), TITLE G, OF SECTION 1, REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED AND TWENTY, ENTITLED "AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS".
Be it enacted, by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sub-items (g), (i) and (j) of Item 37, Subsection (e), Title B of section one, Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred and twenty, are hereby amended read as follows:
"(g) For construction and/or repairs of Barrio Cansayang
Elementary School, San Juan…………………… P10,000.00

(i) Purchase of municipal building site andconstruction
of municipal building, Siaton ..................... 10,000.00

(j) Repair of the Palimpinon-Caidyocan
Road, Valencia.................................... 5,000.00"
SEC. 2. Item 55, Group I, subsection (a), Title G of section one, Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred and twenty, is hereby amended to read as follows:
"55. Liloan feeder road, Maria, Negros
Oriental............................................P15,000.00"
SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
