Republic Act No. 3307
June 18, 1961
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM 34, FOR THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL, PAGE 499, PARAGRAPH g, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 34, for the Province of Rizal, page 499, paragraph g, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"34. Municipal Building, Morong
P15,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
