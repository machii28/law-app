Republic Act No. 6183
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIWAN, PROVINCE OF KALINGA-APAYAO, TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Liwan in the Province of Kalinga-Apayao is changed to the Municipality of Rizal.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
