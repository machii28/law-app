Republic Act No. 700
May 9, 1952
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF TWO HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS AS NATIONAL ANNUAL AID TO THE REVOLVING FUND OF THE ALABANG SERUM AND VACCINE LABORATORIES, DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise obligated, there is hereby authorized to be appropriated the sum of two hundred fifty thousand pesos (P250,000) as national annual aid to the Revolving Fund of the Alabang Serum and Vaccine Laboratories, Department of Health.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
