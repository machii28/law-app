Republic Act No. 682
May 9, 1952
AN ACT TO NAME THE BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIOS OF UGSUD AND CUPANG, MUNICIPALITY OF BANGA, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, "CAPITAN TAZAN MEMORIAL SCHOOL."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Ugsud-Cupang Barrio School in the barrios of Ugsud and Cupang, Municipality of Banga, Province of Capiz, shall hereafter be known as "Capitan Tazan Memorial School."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
