Republic Act No. 883
June 19, 1953
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE JESUS DE LA PESA BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIKINA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO LEODEGARIO VICTORINO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Jesus de la Pena Barrio School in the municipality of Marikina, Province of Rizal is hereby changed to Leodegario Victorino Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1953.
