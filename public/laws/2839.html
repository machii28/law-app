Republic Act No. 2839
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BUGAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Bugay, Bato, Baboy, Manobilan, Manduaw and Bucao in the Municipality of Bayawan, Province of Negros Oriental, are hereby constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as Barrio Bugay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
