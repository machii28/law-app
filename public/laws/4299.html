Republic Act No. 4299
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CONVERTING LOOC HIGH SCHOOL IN THE PROVINCE OF ROMBLON INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS LOOC NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Looc High School in the Province of Romblon is hereby converted into a national high school, to be known as Looc National High School.

SEC. 2. The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-six. Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
