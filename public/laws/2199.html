Republic Act No. 2199
May 7, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF HAGICHIKAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Hagichikan, Bagakay, Hubasan, Cambonot and Gisok in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Leyte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Hagichikan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
