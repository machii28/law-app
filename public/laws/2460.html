Republic Act No. 2460
June 21, 1959
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (g), PAGE 205, PARAGRAPH f, TITLE M, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY HUNDRED NINETY-THREE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (g), for the Province of Cebu, page 205, paragraph f, Title M, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred ninety-three is amended to read as follows:






"(g1)
Kalangyawon Barrio School, Carcar Construction of 1-room school building
4,000.00








"(g2)

 Bolinawan Primary School, Carcar Completion, repair and Painting
3,500.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
