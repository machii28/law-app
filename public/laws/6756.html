Republic Act No. 6756
August 11, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE KAUSWAGAN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN PALO, LEYTE, TO KAUSWAGAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Kauswagan Barangay High School in Palo, Leyte, is hereby changed to Kauswagan National High School.

SEC. 2. This shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved: August 11, 1989.
