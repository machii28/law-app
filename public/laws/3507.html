Republic Act No. 3507
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SIBUTU CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SITANGKAI, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO DATU JAAFAR CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Sibutu Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Sitangkai, Province of Sulu, is changed to Datu Jaafar Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
