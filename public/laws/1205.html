Republic Act No. 1205
April 20, 1955
AN ACT CONVERTING ALL SPECIALLY ORGANIZED PROVINCES INTO REGULARLY ORGANIZED PROVINCES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The provisions of chapter fifty-six of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended, and all laws or parts thereof now applicable to regularly organized provinces are hereby extended and applied to all specially organized provinces.

SEC. 2. Officers in any of such provinces appointed to or occupying elective positions shall continue to hold office until their successors shall have been elected and qualified in the next regular elections for provincial and municipal officers.

SEC. 3. Chapter sixty-three of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended, is hereby repealed.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 20, 1955.
