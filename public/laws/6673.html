Republic Act No. 6673
September 5, 1988
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GENERAL TRIAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GENERAL TRIAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO PAARALANG PANG-ALAALA KAY DIEGO MOJICA (DIEGO MOJICA MEMORIAL SCHOOL)
Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the General Trials Elementary School in the Municipality of General Trias, Province of Cavite, is changed to Paaralang Pang-alaala kay Diego Mojica (Diego Mojica Memorial School).

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA
(Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
