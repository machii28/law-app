Republic Act No. 1634
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO SCHOOL OF CAPOOCAN IN THE CITY OF CALBAYOG, TO "TIBURCIO TANCINCO MEMORIAL SCHOOL."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio school of Capoocan in the City of Calbayog, shall hereafter be known as "Tiburcio Tancinco Memorial School," in honor of the late Tiburcio Tancinco, who was a Representative from the first district of the Province of Samar and later Judge of the Court of first Instance.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
