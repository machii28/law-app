Republic Act No. 4759
June 18, 1966
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF KAWIT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO AGUINALDO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the great service rendered to the country by the late General Emilio Aguinaldo, the name of Kawit Elementary School in the Municipality of Kawit, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Aguinaldo Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
