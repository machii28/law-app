Republic Act No. 2894
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIOS OF BALANIOG AND PULACAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LABANGAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Bulanit, East Tawagan Norta and Dalapang in the Municipality of Labangan, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Balaniog.

SEC. 2. The sitios of Lower Pulacan, Limpang Proper, West Limpang, Waterfalls and Lantawan in the Municipality of Labangan, same province, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Pulacan.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
