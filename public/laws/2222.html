Republic Act No. 2222
May 16, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING TO MARILAG THE NAME OF BARRIO PINAGKAANURAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Pinagkaanuran in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas, is hereby Ranged to Marilag.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 16, 1959.
