Republic Act No. 2165
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIOS OF SIGUINON, LUSOD, CANTOMOHA, HAGNAYA AND KARAPDAPAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALCEDO. PROVINCE OF SAMAR, TO BARRIOS TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIOS OF S1GUINON, LUSOD, CANTOMOHA, HAGNAYA AND KARAPDAPAN, RESPECTIVELY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Siguinon, Lusod, Cantomoha, Hagnaya and Karapdapan in the Municipality of Salcedo, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Siguinon, Lusod, Cantomoha, Hagnaya and Karapdapan, respectively.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
