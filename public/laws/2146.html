Republic Act No. 2146
April 24, 1959
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE KIAÑGAN HOSPITAL TO IFUGAO HOSPITAL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Kiañgan Hospital in the Municipality of Ifugao, Mountain Province, is hereby changed to Ifugao Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall lake effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
