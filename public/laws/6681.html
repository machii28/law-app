Republic Act No. 6681
November 4, 1988
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF WRIGHT, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, TO MUNICIPALITY OF PARANAS
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Wright, Province of Samar, is hereby changed to Municipality of Paranas.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval by a majority of votes cast in a plebiscite to be conducted in accordance with law.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA
(Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate
Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representative was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 23, 1988 and September 12, 1988, respectively.
