Republic Act No. 3296
June 18, 1961
AN ACT REAPPROPRIATING THE SUM OF ONE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY-EIGHT PESOS AND TWENTY-THREE CENTAVOS WHICH IS THE UNEXPENDED BALANCE OF TWO THOUSAND PESOS APPROPRIATED UNDER ITEM (p), PAGE 80, GROUP II, SUB-PARAGRAPH (27), FOR THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE, PARAGRAPH f, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of one thousand, six hundred seventy-eight pesos and twenty-three centavos which is the unexpended balance of two thousand pesos appropriated under item (p), page 80, group II, subparagraph (27), for the Province of Leyte, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, to wit:

"(p) Apid Artesian well, Inopacan Construction P2,000.00"


is hereby reappropriated for the construction of water tank in Apid, Inopacan, Province of Leyte.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
