Republic Act No. 1022
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SASA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF DAVAO TO FRANCISCO BANGOY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Sasa Elementary School in the City of Davao shall hereafter be known as the Francisco Bangoy Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
