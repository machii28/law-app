Republic Act No. 890
June 19, 1953
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF CATMON, MUNICIPALITY OF SIBALOM, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO PAJARITO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Catmon in the municipality of Sibalom, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Pajarito.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1953.
