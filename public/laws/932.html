Republic Act No. 932
June 20, 1953
AN ACT TO MAKE THE PRESENT SITIO OF KAWIT OF THE BARRIO OF BAYANAN, MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS LAUREL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The present sitio of Kawit of the barrio of Bayanan, municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, with the entire territory comprised within the jurisdictional limits of said sitio, is hereby made a barrio to be known as Laurel.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
