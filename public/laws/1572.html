Republic Act No. 1572
June 16, 1956
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF TWO HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS AS ADDITIONAL FUND TO PAY BACK COMPENSATION OF CRAFTSMEN AND APPRENTICES-WORKING IN THE TECHNICAL DIVISIONS IN THE BUREAU OF PRINTING WHO ARE ENTITLED TO SUCH BENEFIT UNDER SECTION ONE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED FIFTY-SEVEN OF THE REVISED ADMINISTRATIVE CODE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of two hundred fifty thousand pesos is appropriated, out of the funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, as additional fund to pay back extra compensation of all craftsmen and apprentices working in the technical divisions of the Bureau of Printing who are entitled to such privilege granted under section one thousand six hundred fifty-seven of the Revised Administrative Code.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
