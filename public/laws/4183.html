Republic Act No. 4183
April 21, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF INUDARAN CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARANTAO, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, TO SULTAN GURO CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Inudaran Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Marantao, Province of Lanao del Sur, is changed to Sultan Guro Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 21, 1965.
