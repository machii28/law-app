Republic Act No. 7488
May 5, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TIBSOC PRIMARY SCHOOL, SITUATED IN BARANGAY TIBSOC, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ENRIQUE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO EUSEBIO M. QUITCO PRIMARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Tibsoc Primary School, situated in Barangay Tibsoc, Municipality of San Enrique, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Eusebio M. Quitco Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 5, 1992.
