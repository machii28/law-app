Republic Act No. 2188
May 7, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF THE EMERGENCY HOSPITALS IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF LOPEZ AND CATANAUAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Lopez Elementary Hospital in the Municipality of Lopez, and the name of the Catanauan Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Catanauan, Province of Quezon, are hereby changed to Magsaysay Memorial Hospital and Bondoc Peninsula General Hospital, respectively.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
