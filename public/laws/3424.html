Republic Act No. 3424
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ATIMONAN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL TO DONA MARTA MEMORIAL HOSPITAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the invaluable services rendered to the people of the municipality of Atimonan, Province of Quezon, by the late Dona Marta Montes Guinto, a former civic and spiritual leader of the said municipality, the name of the Atimonan Emergency Hospital is changed to Dona Marta Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
