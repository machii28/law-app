Republic Act No. 2153
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF DA-O IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Little Doñgon, Big Doñgon, Patag, Ongos-ongos and Dao-Fishpond in the Municipality of San Jose, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Da-o.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
