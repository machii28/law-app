Republic Act No. 8464
January 19, 1998
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN KAPALANGAN, GAPAN, NUEVA ECIJA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE KAPALANGAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS), a high school in Barangay Kapalangan, Gapan, Nueva Ecija, to be known as the Kapalangan National High School.

SEC. 2. The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

Approved, lapsed into law on January 19, 1998, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
