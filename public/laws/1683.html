Republic Act No. 1683
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TAGODTOD IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN NICOLAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Tagodtod is separated from the barrio of Munlawin, Municipality of San Nicolas, Province of Batangas, and constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Tagodtod.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
