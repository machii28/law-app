Republic Act No. 2026
June 22, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (23), UNDER "NUEVA VIZCAYA," SUBSECTION G. SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (23), under "Nueva Vizcaya," subsection g, section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:
"23. Solano-Wacal Road P7,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
