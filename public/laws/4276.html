Republic Act No. 4276
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CONVERTING ROMBLON MATERNITY AND CHILDREN'S HOSPITAL IN THE PROVINCE OF ROMBLON INTO A GENERAL HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS ROMBLON GENERAL HOSPITAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Romblon Maternity and Children's Hospital in the Province of Romblon is hereby converted into a general hospital to be known as Romblon General Hospital.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the conversion, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-six. Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
