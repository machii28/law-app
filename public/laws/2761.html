Republic Act No. 2761
June 19, 1960
AN ACT APPROPRIATING SEVENTY-FIVE THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE ALLEN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALLEN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of seventy-five thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation of the Allen Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Allen, Province of Samar.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
