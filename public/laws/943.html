Republic Act No. 943
June 20, 1953
ACT TO CREATE THE BARRIO OF SAN TEODORO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Pook ng Buhangin in the barrio of Ilat and the sitio of Cupang in the barrio of Gelerang Kawayan, both in the municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, are constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Teodoro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
