Republic Act No. 1498
June 16, 1956
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF SAN MARCELO, MUNICIPALITY OF MALABON, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO ACACIA OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY AND SEPARATING IT FROM THE BARRIO OF TUGATOG OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of San Marcelo in the Municipality of Malabon, Province of Rizal, is hereby separated from the barrio of Tugatog and converted into a distinct barrio to be known as barrio Acacia of the same municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
