Republic Act No. 4471
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CALUMPANG COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, SUB-PROVINCE OF BILIRAN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO FATHER SERGIO EAMIGUEL COMMUNITY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Calumpang Community School in the Municipality of Naval, Sub-Province of Biliran, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Father Sergio Eamiguel Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
