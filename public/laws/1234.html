Republic Act No. 1234
June 7, 1955
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MANALO MUNICIPALITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Materingen, Tandayag, Nasedoc, and Panlawagan are separated from the barrio of royogon, Municipality of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan, and are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Manalo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 7, 1955.
