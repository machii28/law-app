Republic Act No. 2145
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINDANGAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Tigbao, Nato, Pangalalan and Maras in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga dal Norte, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Tigbao, Nato, Pangalalan and Maras respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
