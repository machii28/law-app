Republic Act No. 1712
June 21, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM TWENTY-SIX, PARAGRAPH ONE, FOR THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item twenty-six, paragraph one, for the Province of Camarines Norte, section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is amended to read as follows:

"(26) Construction of one-room (semi-permanent) building, San Jose Barrio School, Jose Pañganiban P5,000.00"


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
