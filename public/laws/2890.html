Republic Act No. 2890
June 19, 1960
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (24), PAGE 208, PARAGRAPH c, TITLE A, SECTION FOUR OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED AND THIRTEEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (24), page 208, paragraph c, Title A, Section four of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred and thirteen, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"(24) Januiay Town Hall, Iloilo-
Completion...........P50,000.00."

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
