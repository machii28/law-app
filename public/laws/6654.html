Republic Act No. 6654
May 20, 1988
AN ACT REPEALING SECTION FORTY OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NUMBERED EIGHT HUNDRED SEVEN, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE CIVIL SERVICE DECREE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section Forty of Presidential Decree Numbered Eight hundred seven, otherwise known as the Civil Service Decree, is hereby repealed.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
 (Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate

This Act which originated in the Senate was passed by the same on November 9, 1987, and considered concurred in by the House of Representatives when it passed House Bill No. 1521 which is identical to Senate Bill No. 38 on May 9, 1988.

(Sgd.) QUIRINO D. ABAD SANTOS, JR.
