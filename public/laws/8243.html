Republic Act No. 8243
December 20, 1996
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY SALLACONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANTAY, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BARANGAY SAN MARIANO
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barangay Sallacong in the Municipality of Bantay, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Barangay San Mariano.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, December 20, 1996.
