Republic Act No. 2864
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SITIO OF DAMMANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ENRILE, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO SAN JUAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the sitio of Dammang in the Municipality of Enrile, Province of Cagayan, is changed to San Juan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
