Republic Act No. 2163
April 24, 1959
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF HAGONOY, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO THE SITE OF THE BARRIO OF PAWA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The seat of government of the Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Davao, is hereby transferred to the site of the barrio of Pawa.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
