Republic Act No. 1646
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SACUB PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANSALAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO LANURO PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Sacub Primary School in the Municipality of Bansalan, Province of Davao, shall hereafter be known as the Lanuro Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
