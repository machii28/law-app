Republic Act No. 9754
November 10, 2009
AN ACT CONVERTING THE NAME OF SAN NARCISO VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN SAN NARCISO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON TO GODOFREDO M. TAN MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SEC. 1. Renaming of High School. — The name of San Narciso Vocational High School in the Municipality of San Narciso, Province of Quezon is hereby changed to Godofredo M. Tan Memorial School of Arts and Trades.

SEC. 2. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE
