Republic Act No. 3723
June 22, 1963
AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTY-THREE HUNDRED SEVENTY-THREE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-three hundred seventy-three is hereby amended to read as follows:

"SECTION 1. The barrios of Lood, Amaga, Kilugan, Busdak, Patnanungan Sur and Patnanungan Norte, including the territorial jurisdiction of the Islands of Lumaya, Pinaglunglungan, Mayawak, Daidi, Minalolo Malaki, Minalolo Munti, Ikikon, Minamata, Katakian Grande and Katakian Chika, are separated from the Municipality of Burdeos, Province of Quezon, and are hereby constituted into a distinct and independent municipal district, to be known as the Municipal District of Patnanungan, Province of Quezon, with the barrio of Patnanungan Sur as the seat of its municipal government."


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
