Republic Act No. 2758
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND PRIORITY NUMBER 33, PAGE 90-B, GROUP II, FOR THE PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, APPENDIX B OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED THIRTEEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Priority Number 33, page 90-B, Group II, for the Province of Sorsogon, Appendix B of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is amended to read as follows:

"33. Pilar Ginablan
Construction of one room semi-permanent P5,000.00."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
