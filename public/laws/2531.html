Republic Act No. 2531
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO BOHÓ IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CASIGURAN, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, TO SAN ANTONIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Bohó in the Municipality of Casiguran, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby changed to San Antonio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
