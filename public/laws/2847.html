Republic Act No. 2847
June 19, 1960
AN ACT REAPPROPRIATING CERTAIN SUMS UNDER TITLE A, TITLE B AND TITLE D, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED AND SECTION TWO OF SAME ACT, AND CERTAIN SUMS UNDER TITLE B, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-THREE HUNDRED ONE AND SECTION TWO OF THE SAME ACT, FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF THE DALANAS BRIDGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BARBAZA, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of twenty-five thousand pesos appropriated under item (27), page five, paragraph a, Title A, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred; the sum of five thousand pesos appropriated under item (e), and a partial sum of one hundred thousand pesos under item (f), both on page twenty, subparagraph five, paragraph a, Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred; the sum of three thousand pesos appropriated under item (j) and the sum of ten thousand pesos under item (n), both on page twenty-one, sub-paragraph five, paragraph a, Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred; the sum of seventy-five thousand pesos appropriated under item (c), page one hundred thirty-three, subparagraph five, paragraph a, Title D, Section one of Republic Act Numbered en hundred; the sum of thirty thousand pesos appropriated under item (y), and the sum of fifteen thousand pesos appropriated under item (z), both on page two hundred ten, subparagraph five, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred; and the sum of forty thousand pesos appropriated under item (24) page three, paragraph a, Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred one; and the sum of ten thousand pesos appropriated under item (e), the sum of ten thousand pesos appropriated under item (g) the sum of ten thousand pesos appropriated under item (q), and the sum of ten thousand pesos appropriated under item (s), all on page two hundred thirty-four, sub-paragraph five, paragraph a, Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred one; and the sum of five thousand pesos appropriated under item (o) on page three hundred forty-six, subparagraph five, paragraph d, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one; all amounting to the total sum of three hundred forty-eight thousand pesos, are hereby reappropriated for the construction of the Dalanas Bridge in the Municipality of Barbara, Province of Antique.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
