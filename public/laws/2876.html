Republic Act No. 2876
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF LOOC NORTE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ASTURIAS, PROVINCE OF CEBU.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Looc Norte in the Municipality of Asturias, Province of Cebu, is converted into a barrio of the said municipality, to be known as barrio of Looc Norte.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
