Republic Act No. 7817
November 10, 1994
AN ACT NAMING THE GRANADA-MURCIA ROAD AND EXTENSION (CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD) WHICH IS A NATIONAL ROAD IN BACOLOD CITY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AS THE CARLOS HILADO HIGHWAY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Granada-Murcia Road and Extension (Circumferential Road), a national road in Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, is hereby named as the Carlos Hilado Highway.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, November 10, 1994.
