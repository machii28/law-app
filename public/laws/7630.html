Republic Act No. 7630
July 17, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DINAGAT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINAGAT, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO DON RUBEN E. ECLEO, SR. MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Dinagat National High School in the Municipality of Dinagat, Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby changed to Don Ruben E. Ecleo, Sr. Memorial National High School.

SEC. 2. The Department of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby directed to take cognizance and implement the provision of Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1992.
