Republic Act No. 6186
June 19, 1971
AN ACT NAMING THE PILAR-BAGAC-MORONG NATIONAL ROAD IN THE PROVINCE OF BATAAN TO GOVERNOR JOAQUIN J. LINAO NATIONAL ROAD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Pilar-Bagac-Morong National Road in the Province of Bataan is hereby named Governor Joaquin J. Linao National Road.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
