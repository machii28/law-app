Republic Act No. 1722
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF CAMPITIC IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PALO, PROVINCE LEYTE, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF CAMPITIC.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Campitic in the Municipality of Palo, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Campitic.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
