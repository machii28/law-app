Republic Act No. 2891
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE BARRIO OF BALOGO AND THE SITIO OF TABE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYDOLONG, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALANGKAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of Balogo and the sitio of Tabe in the Municipality of Maydolong, Province of Samar, are separated from said municipality, and are made part of the Municipality of Balangkayan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
