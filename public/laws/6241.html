Republic Act No. 6241
June 19, 1971
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR TWO ASSISTANT PROVINCIAL FISCALS FOR THE PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, BY FURTHER AMENDING SECTION SIXTEEN HUNDRED SEVENTY-FOUR OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, is further amended by inserting a subparagraph relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Camiguin, to read as follows:

"Camiguin, two assistant provincial fiscals;"

SEC. 2. There is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the sum of twenty thousand pesos or so much .thereof as may be necessary to pay the share of the National Government for the positions provided for in this Act for the current fiscal year. Thereafter, appropriations for the succeeding fiscal years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
