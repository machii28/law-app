Republic Act No. 2194
May 7, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TAGABACA IN THE CITY OF BUTUAN
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Tagabaca in the City of Butuan is converted into a barrio of said city to be known as the barrio of Tagabaca.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
