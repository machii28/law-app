Republic Act No. 3975
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PAMPANGA HIGH SCHOOL TO JOSE ABAD SANTOS HIGH SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, Pampanga High School in San Fernando Pampanga, shall hereafter be known and referred to Jose Abad Santos High School

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
