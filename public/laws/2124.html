Republic Act No. 2124
April 5, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF KAPITAN, BARRIO OF PILA, MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, INTO A BARRIO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF KAPITAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Kapitan, barrio of Pila, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Kapitan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 5, 1959.
