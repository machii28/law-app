Republic Act No. 9455
May 15, 2007
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ODIONGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN JOSE, CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL TO EGMIDIO V. MANZO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of Elementary School. — The name of Odiongan Elementary School in Barangay San Jose, City of Tanjay, Province of Negros Oriental is hereby changed to Egmidio V. Manzo Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on April 5, 2006 and February 19, 2007, respectively.
