Republic Act No. 4212
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIUAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Cajoy, San Jose and Bitaugan in the Municipality of Guiuan, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Cajoy, San Jose and Bitaugan, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
