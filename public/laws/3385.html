Republic Act No. 3385
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. (a) The sitio of Balanga in the barrio of San Andres, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Sto. Nino Domingo; and (b) The sitio of San Antonio-Aplaya in the barrio of San Antonio, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Bagong Danglayan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
