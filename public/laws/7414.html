Republic Act No. 7414

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE POLILLO MEDICARE COMMUNITY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLILLO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, FROM FIFTEEN (15) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Polillo Medicare Community Hospital in the Municipality of Polillo, Province of Quezon, is hereby increased from fifteen (15) to twenty-five (25) beds.
