Republic Act No. 1133
June 16, 1954
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (h), GROUP I, FOR THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO, SUBSECTION (a), TITLE C, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED TWENTY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (h), Group I, for the Province of Iloilo, subsection (a), Title C, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty is amended to read as follows:
“(h) For the roadside development of the Dingle-San Enrique road, including the improvement of the Moroboro Spring and the establishment of a travelers' rest-house….. P25,000.00”
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1954.
