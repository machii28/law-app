Republic Act No. 7364
April 8, 1992
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE ILOCOS REGIONAL HOSPITAL IN SAN FERNANDO, LA UNION, FROM ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) TO TWO HUNDRED (200) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Ilocos Regional Hospital in San Fernando, La Union, is hereby increased from one hundred fifty (150) to two hundred (200) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,
(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 26, 1991 and December 10, 1991, respectively.
