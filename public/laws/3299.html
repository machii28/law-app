Republic Act No. 3299
June 18, 1961
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM 39, PAGE 136, FOR THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, SECTION TWO-G OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED THIRTEEN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 39, page 136, for the Province of Batangas, Section two-g of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is hereby amended to read as follows:

"39. Malaking Pulo Barrio School, Tanauan P10,000.00"


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
