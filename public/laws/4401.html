Republic Act No. 4401
June 19, 1965
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE EAST VISAYAN SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES TO OFFER TEACHER EDUCATION CURRICULA LEADING TO THE DEGREE OF BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN INDUSTRIAL EDUCATION AND THE DEGREE OF BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN INDUSTRIAL ARTS, TO OFFER EVENING CLASSES IN SUCH COURSES AND IN TRADE AND INDUSTRIAL EDUCATION, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS FOR THE PURPOSE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The East Visayan School of Arts and Trades situated in the City of Dumaguete is hereby authorized to offer education curricula leading to the degree of Bachelor of Science in Industrial Education and the degree of Bachelor of Science in Industrial Arts. Evening classes shall be offered in such courses and in trade and industrial education.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said curricula during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five to nineteen hundred sixty-six. Thereafter, the necessary funds for the purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
