Republic Act No. 3561
June 21, 1963
AN ACT CREATING ONE ADDITIONAL POSITION OF ASSISTANT PROVINCIAL FISCAL IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION SIXTEEN HUNDRED SEVENTY-FOUR OF THE REVISED ADMINISTRATIVE CODE, AS AMENDED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The provision of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended, relative to assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Iloilo, is further amended to read as follows:
"Iloilo, nine assistant provincial fiscals;"
SEC.2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1963
