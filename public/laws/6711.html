Republic Act No. 6711
February 10, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MEDICION ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN MEDICION 2ND, MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO GOV. DOMINADOR CAMERINO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Medicion Elementary School in the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Gov. Dominador Camerino Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House
of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 22, 1988 and December 2, 1988, respectively.
