Republic Act No. 1645
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN AGUSTIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF SAN AGUSTIN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO DON FELIX DE LEON MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of San Agustin Elementary School in the barrio of San Agustin, Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bulacan, is changed to Don Felix de Leon Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
