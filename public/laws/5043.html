Republic Act No. 5043
June 17, 1967
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM 9, FOR THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE, PAGE 547, GROUP II, UNDER LISTING B, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTY-EIGHT HUNDRED FORTY-SIX.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 9, for the Province of Leyte, page 547, Group II, under listing B, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-eight hundred forty-six is hereby amended to read as follows:
"9. Barrio School Building, Eyusan, Almeria

Construction, improvement and/or repair …………………….1,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
