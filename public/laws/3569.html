Republic Act No. 3569
June 21, 1963
AN ACT CONSTITUTING THE FIRST CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF CEBU INTO A SEPARATE ENGINEERING DISTRICT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the first congressional district of the Province of Cebu is hereby constituted into a separate engineering district to be known as the Second Engineering District of the Province of Cebu, with its office at the City of Danao.

The district engineer for said engineering district shall be appointed in accordance with existing laws.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1963.
