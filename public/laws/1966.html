Republic Act No. 1966
June 22, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM SIXTY-ONE, PARAGRAPH "at" UNDER THE PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item sixty-one, paragraph "at" under the Province of Sorsogon, section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is amended to read as follows:
" (61) Construction of two-room semipermanent school building, Basud,
Sorsogon P10,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
