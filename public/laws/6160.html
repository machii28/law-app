Republic Act No. 6160
November 9, 1970
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PANSOL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO PANSOL, MUNICIPALITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO MABABANG PAARALAN DON EDUARDO E. BARRETO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Pansol Elementary School in the barrio of Pansol, Municipality of Calamba, Province of Laguna, is hereby changed to Mababang Paaralan Don Eduardo E. Barreto.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, November 9, 1970.
