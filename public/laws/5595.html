Republic Act No. 5595
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PORO, PROVINCE OF CEBU.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Hereafter, Barrio Tiguis shall Toe known as Barrio Martinez; Barrio Cagcagan as Barrio Garciano; and Barrio Cansabusab as Barrio Sosmeña.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
