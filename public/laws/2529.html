Republic Act No. 2529
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PANTAL-SAMAT PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF PANTAL-SAMAT, MUNICIPALITY OF BUGALLON, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO MAGSAYSAY PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Pantal-Samat Primary School in the barrio of Pantal-Samat, Municipality of Bugallon, Province of Pangasinan, is changed to Magsaysay Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
