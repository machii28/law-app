Republic Act No. 6432
June 17, 1972
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO SAN ANGEL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSALES, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO SILVESTRE DOMINGO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio San Angel in the Municipality of Rosales, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby changed to Silvestre Domingo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1972.
