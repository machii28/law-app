Republic Act No. 1421
June 10, 1956
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF GATA, PROVINCE OF LANAO, TO MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF POONA BAYABAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipal District of Province of Lanao, is changed to Municipal District Poona Bayabao.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1956.
