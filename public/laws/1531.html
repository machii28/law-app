Republic Act No. 1531
June 16, 1956
AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED ELEVEN, ENTITLED, "AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven is hereby amended on the following particulars:

  1. On page 103, Subdivision II:

    "(a) Asphalt paving of the following streets, Municipality of Bayawan, Negros Oriental: Buenavista Street from Zamora Street to Mabini Street; Mabini Street from Progreso Street to Burgos Street; Burgos Street from Mabini Street to General Luna Street; General Luna Street from Burgos Street to Nueva Street; Independence Street from Provincial Road to Nueva Street; La Libertad Street from Provincial Road to Rizal Street; Martirez Street from Provincial Road to Progreso Street; Progreso Street from General Luna Street to Martirez Street; Rizal Street from General Luna Street to La Libertad Street; Nueva Street from General Luna Street to Independence Street
    P15,000.00"


  2. On page 541:

    "13. Barrio Minalolan, Maria, Elementary School Building Construction of additional rooms
    P10,000.00"


  3. On page 168-A, Appendix A, Item B-362.14, the words under Column, "name and location of project," be changed to read as follows:

    "B-362.14 Arellano Street, leading to cemetery. Zamboanguita repair and improvement"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
