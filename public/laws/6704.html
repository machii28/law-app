Republic Act No. 6704
February 10, 1989
AN ACT RENAMING THE BANARAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BANARAN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAPA-SAPA, PROVINCE OF TAWI-TAWI, AS THE DATU ISMAEL YUSOP MEMORIAL CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Banaran Elementary School in Barangay Banaran, Municipality of Sapa-Sapa, Province of Tawi-Tawi, is hereby renamed as the Datu Ismael Yusop Memorial Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House
of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 22, 1988 and December 2, 1988, respectively.
