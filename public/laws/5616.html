Republic Act No. 5616
June 21, 1969
AN ACT FORMALIZING INTO LAW THE NAME OF PAOAD ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO ACOP, MUNICIPALITY OF TUBLAY, PROVINCE OF BENGUET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The public school located in Barrio Acop, Municipality of Tublay, Province of Benguet, is hereby formally named Paoad Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
