Republic Act No. 2789
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BULALACAO IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO TO SAN PEDRO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Bulalacao in the Province of Oriental Mindoro is changed to San Pedro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
