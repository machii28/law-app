Republic Act No. 2569
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF AGPAÑGI ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATRAVA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO RAMON MAGSAYSAY MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Agpañgi Elementary School in the Municipality of Calatrava, Province of Negros Occidental, is changed to Ramon Magsaysay Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
