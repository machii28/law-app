Republic Act No. 3308
June 18, 1961
AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (b), paragraph II, page 218, Section 2-f, of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(b) Orense Park, Bauan Construction and/or improvement
P10,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
