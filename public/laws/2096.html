Republic Act No. 2096
March 22, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF ULDOGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN AGUST1N, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO LAOAG.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Uldogan in the Municipality of San Agustin, Province of Isabela, is changed to Laoag.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, March 22, 1959.
