Republic Act No. 4999
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CREATING A CERTAIN BARRIO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Kisaw and Can-apo are hereby constituted into a distinct and independent barrio, to be known as Barrio Mahayag, in the Municipality of Valencia, Province of Bool.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
