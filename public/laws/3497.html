Republic Act No. 3497
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE UPPER BAYANG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYANG, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, TO SULTAN PANDAPATAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Upper Bayang Elementary School in the Municipality of Bayang, Province of Lanao del Sur, is changed to Sultan Pandapatan Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
