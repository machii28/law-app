Republic Act No. 7525
May 21, 1992
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE VIGAN PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIGAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, FROM ONE HUNDRED (100) TO ONE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE (125) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Vigan Provincial Hospital in the Municipality of Vigan, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby increased from one hundred (100) to one hundred twenty-five (125) beds.

SEC. 2. The Department of Health shall take the necessary steps for the immediate implementation of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 7, 1992 and December 10, 1991, respectively.
