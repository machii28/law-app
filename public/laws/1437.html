Republic Act No. 1437
June 14, 1956
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MANILA BOULEVARD IN THE CITY OF CAVITE TO RAFAEL PALMA BOULEVARD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the distinguished services to the country of Rafael Palma, first delegate of the Province of Cavite to the Philippine Assembly, the Manila Boulevard in the City of Cavite shall hereafter be known as Rafael Palma Boulevard.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1956.
