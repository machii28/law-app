June 16, 1962
AN ACT GRANTING TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF LEGANES, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, IN FULL OWNERSHIP, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN SAID PROVINCE BY AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THREE HUNDRED AND SIXTY-SIX.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section one of Republic Act Numbered Three hundred and sixty-six is amended to read as follows:
