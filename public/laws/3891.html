Republic Act No. 3891
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CONSTITUTING THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE INTO TWO ENGINEERING DISTRICTS.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, upon the recommendation of the Commis­sioner of Public Highways and upon approval of the Secretary of Public Works and Communications, the Prov­ince of Zamboanga del Norte shall be constituted into two engineering districts. The first and second district engineers' offices shall be established in the municipalities of Dipolog and Liloy, respectively. The Secretary of Public Works and Communications is hereby authorized to fix the territory of each engineering district.

Each engineering district shall have one highway district engineer who shall be appointed in accordance with existing laws.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
