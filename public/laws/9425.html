Republic Act No. 9425
April 10, 2007
AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER 13 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN GENERAL TRIAS IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1: December 13 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the Municipality of General Trias in the Province of Cavite to commemorate its founding day.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 7, 2007 and January 22, 2007, respectively.
