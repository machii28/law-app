Republic Act No. 2024
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO TAPUL, MUNICIPALITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO BARRIO SALVACION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of barrio Tapul in the Municipality of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan, is changed to barrio Salvacion.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
