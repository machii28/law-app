Republic Act No. 3451
April 25, 1962
AN ACT APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES FOR THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING JUNE THIRTY, NINETEEN HUNDRED SIXTY-TWO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of three million pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated as additional funds for the operation of the House of Representatives for the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred sixty-two, itemized as follows:
1. For settlement of unpaid obligations with the Bureau of Printing
P500,000.00
2. For repairs and improvement of rooms and buildings
250,000.00
3. For payment of overtime services of employees during session
157,000.00
4. For expenses in connection with committee meetings
250,000.00
5. For terminal leave of officials and employees
400,000.00
6. For the payment of retirement and insurance premiums
443,000.00
7. For employment of supplementary personnel for a period of three months
450,000.00
8. For supplies and equipment
550,000.00
TOTAL
P 3,000,000.00
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 25, 1962.
