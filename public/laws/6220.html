Republic Act No. 6220
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LUMBANG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAJIDIOCAN, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON, TO DAMASO RAMILO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Lumbang Elementary School in the Municipality of Cajidiocan, Province of Romblon, is hereby changed to Damaso Ramilo Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
