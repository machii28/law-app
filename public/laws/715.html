Republic Act No. 715
June 6, 1952
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO TINIGAO BONGOE, MUNICIPALITY OF KALIBO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO MOBO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio Tinigao Bongoe, municipality of Kalibo, Province of Capiz, is hereby changed to Mobo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 6, 1952.
