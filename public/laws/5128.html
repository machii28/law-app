Republic Act No. 5128
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CEBU NORMAL SCHOOL IN CEBU CITY TO CEBU NORMAL COLLEGE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Cebu Normal School in Cebu City is hereby changed to Cebu Normal College.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 17, 1967.
