Republic Act No. 7727
May 19, 1994
AN ACT CONVERTING THE TUNGA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Tunga Barangay High School in the Municipality of Tunga, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a national high school, to be known as the Tunga National High School.

Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 19, 1994.
