Republic Act No. 3923
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MAPOTI PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NULING, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, TO NIBUAYA PRIMARY SCHOOL.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Mapoti Primary School in the Municipality of Nuling, Province of Cotabato, is hereby changed to Nibuaya Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964
