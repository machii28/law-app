Republic Act No. 3556
June 21, 1963
AN ACT TO INCREASE THE BED CAPACITY OF THE MINDORO ORIENTAL PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Mindoro Oriental Provincial Hospital is hereby increased from seventy-five beds to one hundred beds.

SEC. 2. The financing of this hospital shall be in accordance with the Hospital Financing Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1968.
