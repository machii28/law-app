Republic Act No. 1523
June 16, 1956
AN ACT TO CREATE THE BARRIO OF BUÑGAHAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IBAAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Buñgahan in the Municipality of Ibaan, Province of Batangas, is hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality, to be known as the barrio Buñgahan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
