Republic Act No. 2430
June 21, 1959
AN ACT TO MAINTAIN A NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO BE KNOWN AS EASTERN PANGASINAN AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND TO AUTHORIZE THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Bureau of Public Schools shall maintain a national agricultural school of secondary level to be known as Eastern Pangasinan Agricultural High School in the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Province of Pangasinan.

SEC. 2. The Director of Public Schools is authorized to locate and acquire a suitable site for the school, which may be public or private lands situated in the said Municipality of Sta. Maria.

SEC. 3. The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the school during the first year of its operation, including the acquisition of the site of the school.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated shall have been provided for.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
