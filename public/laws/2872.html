Republic Act No. 2872
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE BARRIO OF SAN VICENTE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, SAME PROVINCE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of San Vicente in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, is separated from said municipality, and is transferred to and made part of the Municipality of Puerto Princesa, same province.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
