Republic Act No. 3355
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING BARRIOS IN THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Pagsulhugon and Victory in the Municipality of Babatngon, Province of Leyte, are converted into barrios of said muinicipality, to be known as the barrios of Pagsulhugon and Victory, respectively.

SEC. 2. The following sitios in the Municipality of Santa Fe, Province of Leyte, are constituted as barrios of said municipality, to wit:

Sitio Hinapolon of barrio San Juan to Barrio Hinapolon;
Sitio Badiangay of barrio San Miguel to Barrio Badiangay;
Sitio Pinangotoran of barrio San Juan to barrio Katipunan;
Sitio Curba to Barrio Curba; and Sitio Km, 16 to Barrio Milagrosa.


SEC. 3. The following sitios in the Municipality of Tanauan, Province of Leyte, are converted to barrios of said municipality, to wit:

Sitio Cambalisara to Barrio Cambalisara;
Sitio Cahumay-humayan to Barrio Cahumay-humayan;
Sitio Casogoran to Barrio Casogoran;
Sitio Catmon to Barrio Catmon;
Sitio Guindag-an to Barrio Guindag-an;

SEC. 4. The following sitios in the Municipality of Burauen, Province of Leyte, are converted to barrios of said municipality, to wit:

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
