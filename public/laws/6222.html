Republic Act No. 6222
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO NAPARASAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GAMAY, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Naparasan, Cadahonan, Capatagan, Guinawitan, Maticawol, Cabogawan, and Lonoy in the Municipality of Gamay, Province of Northern Samar, are hereby constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Naparasan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
