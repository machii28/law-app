Republic Act No. 5562
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CONVERTING CERTAIN SITIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUPON, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, INTO BARRIOS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Bagumbayan, San Isidro, Corporacion, and Upper Libodon, all in the Municipality of Lupon, Province of Davao, are hereby converted into barrios to be known as the barrios of Bagumbayan, San Isidro, Corporacion and Libodon, respectively, of the same municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
