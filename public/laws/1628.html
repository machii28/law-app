Republic Act No. 1628
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NAUJAN FARM SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO MARIANO P. LEUTERIO MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Naujan Farm School in the Municipality of Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is changed to Mariano P. Leuterio Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
