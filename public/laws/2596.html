Republic Act No. 2596
June 21, 1959
AN ACT DIVIDING THE BARRIO OF CASIBARAG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABAGAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, INTO TWO BARRIOS TO BE KNOWN AS CASIBARAG NORTE AND CASIBARAG SUR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio, of Casibarag in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, is hereby divided into two barrios to be known as Casibarag Norte and Casibarag Sur.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
