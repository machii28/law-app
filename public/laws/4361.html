Republic Act No. 4361
June 19, 1965
AN ACT APPROPRIATING EIGHTY THOUSAND PESOS FOR OPERATIONAL EXPENSES AND OTHER INCIDENTAL EXPENSES OF THE EASTERN REGIONAL ORGANIZATION FOR PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION FOR ITS COMING CONSULTATIVE MEETING OF THE REPRESENTATIVES OF MEMBER NATIONS TO BE HELD IN MANILA, PHILIPPINES, IN SEPTEMBER, 1965.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. For the purpose of defraying the operational and other incidental expenses including the expenses for a consultative meeting, and the living and transportation expenses of experts, of the holding of the Eastern Regional Organization for public Administration (EROPA) in Manila in September, 1965, the sum of eighty thousand pesos is hereby appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, which sum shall be disbursed by the Department of Foreign Affairs.

SEC. 2 This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
