Republic Act No. 2545
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO SAN FERNANDO, MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO CAMOHAGUIN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio San Fernando, Municipality of Gumaca, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Camohaguin.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
