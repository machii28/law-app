Republic Act No. 2743
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND A CERTAIN ITEM IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED FOR THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 67, page 470 of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"67. Municipal building, Hinundayan P10,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
