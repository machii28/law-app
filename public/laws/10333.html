Republic Act No. 10333
November 15, 2012
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ROMBLON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL-ODIONGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ODIONGAN, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON TO ODIONGAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of a National High School. – The name of Romblon National High School - Odiongan in the Municipality of Odiongan, Province of Romblon is hereby changed to Odiongan National High School.

SEC. 2. Location of the Main Campus. - The main campus of the Odiongan National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in the Municipality of Odiongan, Province of Romblon.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
