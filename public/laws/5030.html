Republic Act No. 5030
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TUACA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BASUD, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO PEDRO RADA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Tuaca Elementary School in the Municipality of Basud, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Pedro Rada Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
