Republic Act No. 1752
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ANTIP0L0 ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO JUAN SUMULONG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Antipolo Elementary School, Municipality of Antipolo, Province of Rizal, is changed to Juan Sumulong Elementary School.

SEC. 2. Ths Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
