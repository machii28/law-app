Republic Act No. 1658
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BARASANAN (C) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DAO, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO BALLESCAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Barasanan (C) in the Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Ballescas.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
