Republic Act No. 3374
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MANLABANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAIBIRAN, SUB-PROVINCE OF BILIRAN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Manlabang, Jubasan, Balilid and Toril in the Municipality of Caibiran, Sub-province of Biliran, Province of Leyte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Manlabang.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
