Republic Act No. 3387
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF CURVA IN THE MUNICIPLAITY OF MEDELLIN, PROVINCE OF CEBU.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress Assembled:

SECTION 1. The sition of Curva in the Municipality in Medellin, Province of Cebu, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Curva.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
