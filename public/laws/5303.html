Republic Act No. 5303
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF NAGPATPATAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOLSONA, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO HILARIO VALDEZ MEMORIAL COMMUNITY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Nagpatpatan Elementary School, in the Municipality of Solsona, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Hilario Valdez Memorial Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
