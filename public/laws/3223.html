Republic Act No. 3223
June 17, 1961
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE MANILA CHRONICLE TO SELL, TRANSFER OR ASSIGN IN FAVOR OF THE CHRONICLE BROADCASTING NETWORK ITS RIGHTS AND PRIVILEGES ACQUIRED UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTEEN HUNDRED AND FORTY-THREE.


Be it enacted by the Senate, and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Manila Chronicle is hereby authorized and empowered to sell, transfer, or assign all the rights and privileges acquired by it under Republic Act Numbered Thirteen hundred and forty-three, entitled "An Act granting the Manila Chronicle a permit to construct, maintain and operate radio broadcasting stations and stations for television in the Philippines." in favor of the Chronicle Broadcasting Network, a corporation dully registered under the laws of the Philippines. The said vendee transferee, or assignee shall be subject to all the conditions, terms, restrictions and limitations of the said permit as fully and completely and to the same extent as if the said permit had been originally granted to the said vendee, transferee or assignee.

SEC. 2, This Act shall take eifect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1961.
