Republic Act No. 904
June 20, 1953
AN ACT TO AMEND COMMONWEALTH ACT NUMBERED FIVE HUNDRED THIRTY-TWO, ENTITLED "AN ACT TO FIX THE SALARY OF THE AUDITOR GENERAL AT FIFTEEN THOUSAND PESOS PER ANNUM."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section one of Commonwealth Act Numbered hundred thirty-two is hereby amended so as to read as follows:
“SECTION 1. The Auditor General shall receive an annual salary of sixteen thousand pesos.”
SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provision of this Act for the rest of the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred fifty-three, shall be taken from the savings of the appropriation for the General Auditing Office for said fiscal year.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
