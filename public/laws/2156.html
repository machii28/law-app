Republic Act No. 2156
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF BALITE IN THE BARRIO OF BANOYO, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN LUIS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, INTO A BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Balite in the barrio of Banoyo Municipality of San Luis, Province of Batangas, is hereby separated from the said barrio and converted into a new barrio to be known as the barrio of Balite.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
