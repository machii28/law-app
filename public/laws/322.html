Republic Act No. 322
June 19, 1948
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PLAZA GUIPIT IN THE CITY OF MANILA TO CAYETANO ARELLANO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In order to perpetuate the memory of the greatest jurist this country has ever produced — that pure and erudite Titan of Philippine jurisprudence — the late first Chief Justice Cayetano Arellano, who, more than any other man in our history, made of the Supreme Court of the Philippines the torchlight and fountainhead of dignity, purity, and integrity in our judicial structure, the Plaza now called Guipit in the City of Manila shall henceforth be known as Plaza Cayetano Arellano.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1948.
