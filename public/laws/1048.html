Republic Act No. 1048
June 12, 1954
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF TAGO CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO FALCON CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the philanthropic services of Bernardo Falcon to the municipality of Tago, Province of Surigao, the name of Tago Central Elementary School is hereby changed to Falcon Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
