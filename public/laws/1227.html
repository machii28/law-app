Republic Act No. 1227
May 31, 1955
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF TWO THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED PESOS TO CARRY OUT THE PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED EIGHTY-EIGHT, REGARDING PAYMENT OF LIFE PENSION TO THE WIDOW OF THE LATE COMMISSIONER PEDRO GUEVARRA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of two thousand four hundred pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred eighty-eight regarding payment of life pension to widow of the late Commissioner Pedro Guevarra for fiscal year 1954-1955.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 31, 1955.
