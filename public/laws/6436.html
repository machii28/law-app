Republic Act No. 6436
June 17, 1972
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SITIO RAWRAWANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ESPIRITU, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO DON MARIANO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Sitio Rawrawang in the Municipality of Espiritu, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Don Mariano.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1972.
