Republic Act No. 1639
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MARIANO PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF MARIANO, MUNICIPALITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, TO RAMON AREVALO PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Mariano Primary School in the barrio of Mariano, Municipality of Gingoog, Province of Misamis Oriental, is changed to Ramon Arevalo Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
