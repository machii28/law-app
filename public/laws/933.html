Republic Act No. 933
June 20, 1953
AN ACT TO NAME "HALSEMA MOUNTAIN ROAD" THE NATIONAL HIGHWAY FROM BAGUIO TO BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The national highway from Baguio to Bontoc, Mountain Province, is named "Halsema Mountain Road".

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
