Republic Act No. 3088
June 17, 1961
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABALIAN IN THE PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE TO SAN JUAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Cabalian, Province of Southern Leyte, is hereby changed to San Juan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1961.
