Republic Act No. 3496
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE RUMAYAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUMBA-BAYABAO, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, TO DAGALANGIT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Rumayas Elementary School in the Municipality of Lumba-Bayabao, Province of Lanao del Sur, is changed to Dagalangit Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
