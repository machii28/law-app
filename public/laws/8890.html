Republic Act No. 8890
August 22, 2000
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PANTOC, MUNICIPALITY OF GABALDON, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA TO BE KNOWN AS GABALDON VOCATIONAL AGRICULTURE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a national high school in Barangay Pantoc, Municipality of Gabaldon, Province of Nueva Ecija to be known as Gabaldon Vocational Agriculture High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,
