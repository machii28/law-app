Republic Act No. 7595
June 1, 1992
AN ACT UPGRADING AND CONVERTING THE PRESENT ARAKAN VALLEY MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE PROVINCE OF COTABATO, INTO A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE ARAKAN VALLEY DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Arakan Valley Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Antipas, Province of Cotabato, is hereby upgraded and converted into a twenty-five-bed capacity district hospital, to be known as the Arakan Valley District hospital.

SEC. 2. This amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. Thereafter, such sum its may be necessary for the maintenance and operation of the said hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 4, 1991 and January 30, 1992, respectively.
