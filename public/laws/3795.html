Republic Act No. 3795
June 22, 1963
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR FOUR ASSISTANT PROVINCIAL FISCALS FOR THE PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, BY FURTHER AMENDING SECTION SIXTEEN HUNDRED SEVENTY-FOUR OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to assistant provincial fiscals, is further amended so that the Province of Lanao del Sur shall have four assistant provincial fiscals.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
