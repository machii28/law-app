Republic Act No. 9457
May 15, 2007
AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY SAN CARLOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINANGONAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay San Carlose. — The San Carlos Heights Subdivision in Barangay Tayuman, Municpality of Binangonan, Province of Rizal is hereby separated from the said barangay and constituted into a district and independent barangay to be known as Barangay San Carlos.

SEC. 2. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. — Pursuan to Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160, otherwise known as the Local Government Code of 1991, the new barangal shall exist upon the majority of the votes cast by the qualified voters of Barangay San Carlos in a plebiscite to be conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) in the area affected within ninety (90) days from the effectivity of this Act.

SEC. 3. Barangay Officials. — The first set of officials of the said barangays shall be appointed by the provincial governor of the Province of Rizal and shall hold office until such time that their successors have been duly elected and qualified.

SEC. 4. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All existing public infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new barangay.

SEC. 5. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangay San Carlos shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160, otherwise known as the Local Government Code of 1991.

SEC. 6. Appropriations. — The amount necessary to conduct the plebiscite as provided for under this Act shall be borne by the Municipality of Binangonan, Province of Rizal.

SEC. 7. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
