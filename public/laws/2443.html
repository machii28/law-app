Republic Act No. 2443
June 21, 1959
AN ACT TO INCREASE THE BED CAPACITY OF THE EASTERN PANGASINAN GENERAL HOSPITAL AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS FOR THE PURPOSE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Eastern Pangasinan General Hospital is hereby increased from twenty-five to fifty.

SEC. 2. The amount of fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the hospital in addition to its existing funds.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1959.
