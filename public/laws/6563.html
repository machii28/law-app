Republic Act No. 6563
September 30, 1972
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF SAN MIGUEL EMERGENCY HOSPITAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the San Miguel Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of San Miguel, Province Of Bulacan, is hereby increased from twenty-five to fifty.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of said hospital in addition to its existing funds.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, September 30, 1972.
