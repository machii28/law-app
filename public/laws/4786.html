Republic Act No. 4786
June 18, 1966
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF NUMANCIA, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO DEL CARMEN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The niime of the Municipality of Numancia in the Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby changed to Del Carmen.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
