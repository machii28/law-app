Republic Act No. 952
June 20, 1953
AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item three, section one, of Republic Act Numbered Six hundred seventy, regarding public works projects for the Province of Rizal, is hereby amended to read as follows:
For the asphalting of Haig Street, from the corner of Shaw Boulevard and Haig, going northward up to the corner of General Kalentong Street
P3,000.00.”
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
