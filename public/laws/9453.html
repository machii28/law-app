Republic Act No. 9453
May 15, 2007
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF POTIA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PINTO, MUNICIPALITY OF ALFONSO LISTA, PROVINCE OF ALFONSO LISTA, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO TO PINTO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of National High School. — The name of Potia National High School in Barangay Pinto, Municipality of Alfonso Lista, Province of Ifugao is hereby changed to Pinto National High School.

SEC. 2. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on June 6, 2005 and February 19, 2007, respectively.
