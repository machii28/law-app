Republic Act No. 485
June 10, 1950
AN ACT TO APPROPRIATING AN ADDITIONAL SUM OF TWELVE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED THIRTY-FIVE PESOS FOR THE MAINTENANCE AND OPERATION OF THE BUNAWAN NATIONAL JUNIOR AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated out of any funds in the Philippine Treasury not otherwise appropriated, an additional sum of twelve thousand five hundred thirty-five pesos for the maintenance and operation of the Bunawan National Junior Agricultural School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 1950.

Approved, June 10, 1950.
