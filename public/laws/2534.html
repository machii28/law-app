Republic Act No. 2534
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TAGPUPUÑGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BABAK, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Tagpupuñgan in the Municipality of Babak, Province of Davao, is converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Tagpupuñgan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
