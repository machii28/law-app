Republic Act No. 1953
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OP THE SINASAHAN BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF SINASAHAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PEÑARANDA, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, TO KAPITAN PEDRO VILLANUEVA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Sinasahan Barrio School in the barrio of Sinasahan, Municipality of Peñaranda, Province of Nueva Ecija, is changed to Kapitan Pedro Villanueva Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
