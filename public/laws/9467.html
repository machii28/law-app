Republic Act No. 9467
May 15, 2007
AN ACT RENAMING THE OZAMIZ CITY BY-PASS ROAD LOCATED IN OZAMIZ CITY AS CONGRESSMAN HILARION J. RAMIRO BY-PASS ROAD
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Ozamiz City By-pass Road in Ozamiz City is hereby renamed as Congressman Hilarion J. Ramiro By-pass Road.

SEC. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways is hereby directed to implement the provision of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate


This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 29, 2005 and February 19, 2007 respectively.
