Republic Act No. 6757
August 11, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PAGSULHUGON BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BABATNGON, LEYTE, TO PAGSULHUGON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Pagsulhugon Barangay High School in Babatngon, Leyte, is hereby changed to Pagsulhugon National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved: August 11, 1989.
