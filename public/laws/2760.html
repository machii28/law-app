Republic Act No. 2760
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (67), PAGE 820, SUBPARAGRAPH (av), FOR THE PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, PARAGRAPH b, TITLE A, SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (67), page 820, subparagraph (av), for the Province of Sorsogon, paragraph b, Title A, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(67) Construction of, fencing and improvement of site for, Industrial Arts Bldg., Basud Barrio School, Sorsogon P10,000.00."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
