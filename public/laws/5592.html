Republic Act No. 5592
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BARANGOBONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of San Patricio, Abulao and Barangobong are separated from the barrio of Magallanes in the Municipality of Tayug, Province of Pangasinan, and constituted into a new separate and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Barangobong in the said municipality and province.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
