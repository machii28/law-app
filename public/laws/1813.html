Republic Act No. 1813
June 22, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (af), UNDER "(27) LEYTE," PARAGRAPH f, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED THIRTEEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (af), under "(27) Leyte," paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen, is amended to read as follows:
"(af)
For the construction of the Bahay Liloan, Leyte, barrio school building .................................... P5,000.00
"(ag)
For the completion of Nueva Estrella school building, Pintuyan, Leyte ..................................... P5,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
