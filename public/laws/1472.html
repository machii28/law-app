Republic Act No. 1472
June 14, 1956
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM G-038.10 UNDER THE PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, TITLE G, APPENDIX A OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED AND ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item G-038.10 under the Province of Nueva Vizcaya, Title G, Appendix A of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred and eleven, is amended to read as follows:
"G-038.10—Bone Communal Irrigation System, Conduction and repair of canals—GR-3-3-3."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
