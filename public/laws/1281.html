Republic Act No. 1281
June 14, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO MALPAG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BARRIO BABULAK.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Barrio Malpag in the Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Leyte, shall hereafter be known as barrio Babulak.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1955.
