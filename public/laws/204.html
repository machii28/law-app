Republic Act No. 204
May 28, 1948
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIOS OF ANGAS, BIGA, KAMANDUNGAN, KILOKILO AND MAKULAPNIT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE, INTO INDEPENDENT BARRIOS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Angas of the barrio of Tagum, Biga of the barrio of Alobo, Kamandungan of the barrio of Lusok, Kilokilo of the barrio of San Antonio, and Makulapnit of the barrio of Devilla, within the jurisdiction of the municipality of Santa Cruz, Province of Marinduque, are hereby converted into regular and independent barrios to be henceforth known as barrios of Angas, Biga, Kamandungan, Kilokilo and Makulapnit, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 28, 1948.
