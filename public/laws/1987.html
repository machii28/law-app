Republic Act No. 1987
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF PALOMA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VALLADOLID, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Paloma in the Municipality of Valladolid, Province of Negros Occidental, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Paloma.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957
