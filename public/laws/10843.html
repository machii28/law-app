Republic Act No. 10843
May 11, 2016
AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAKEVIEW ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AND THE PEDRO E. DIAZ HIGH SCHOOL - LAKEVIEW ANNEX IN BARANGAY PUTATAN, CITY OF MUNTINLUPA, METRO MANILA INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS LAKEVIEW INTEGRATED SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Lakeview Elementary School and the Pedro E. Diaz High School - Lakeview Annex in Barangay Putatan, City of Muntinlupa, Metro Manila are hereby converted into an integrated school to be known as Lakeview Integrated School.

SEC. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Lakeview Elementary School and the Pedro E. Diaz High School - Lakeview Annex are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Lakeview Integrated School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Lakeview Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. The Secretary of Education shall issue the rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
