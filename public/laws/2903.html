Republic Act No. 2903
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN RAMON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMBUJAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Magalotgamot, Catanagan and Paray in the Municipality of Pambujan, Province of Samar, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Ramon. The proposed barrio is bounded on the north by the Banase Brook, on the east by the dapdap tree, point one; on the south by the coconut tree, point two; and on the west by the coconut tree, point three.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
