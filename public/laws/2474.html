Republic Act No. 2474
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIOS OF CAPUTI-AN, BONIFACIO, MAXVILLA, BUENOS AIRES, BUENASUERTE AND SAN LAZARO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALLEN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following barrios are hereby created in the Municipality of Allen, Province of Samar:
  1. Barrio of Caputi-an, consisting of the sitios of Lo-ngib, Danao and Bangon;
  2. Barrio of Bonifacio, consisting of the sjtios of Egot, Salay, Mamatayan and Palacpac;
  3. Barrio of Maxvilla, consisting of the sitios of Pina-yagan, Palanas, Canpitolc, and Guinbog-noan;
  4. Barrio of Buenos Aires (formerly Borobodiongan), consisting of the sitios of Patag, Canlong-canlong, Lo-ok and Vesoria;
  5. Barrio of Buenasuerte, consisting of the sitios of To-og, Cag-olango, Pinamonayan and Cadadiangaoan; and
  6. Barrio of San Lazaro, consisting of the sitios of Sorok, Honop, and Bunglasan.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1959.
