Republic Act No. 2010
June 22, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (9), PAGE 558, PARAGRAPH "am," FOR THE PROVINCE OP PALAWAN, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (9), page 588, paragraph "am," for the Province of Palawan, section two of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is amended to read as follows:
"(9) Construction of building for industrial
shop, Bintuan Barrio School,
Coron P4.000.00."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
