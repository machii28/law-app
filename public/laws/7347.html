Republic Act No. 7347
April 1, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE JIPAPAD BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JIPAPAD, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO JIPAPAD HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Jipapad Barangay High School in the Municipality of Jipapad, Province of Eastern Samar, is hereby changed to Jipapad High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES
(Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on April 5, 1990 and January 23, 1992, respectively.
