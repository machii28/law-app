Republic Act No. 1676
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF KALANGKANG, BARRIO OF KASILY, MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF KALANGKANG OF SAID MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Kalangkang, barrio of Kasily, Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Marinduque, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Kalangkang.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
