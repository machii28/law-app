Republic Act No. 2014
June 22, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (a), GROUP IV, SUBPARAGRAPH 28, FOR THE CITY OF MANILA, PARAGRAPH "f," SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN, AND TO ADD THERETO A NEW ITEM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (a), page eighty-six, group IV, subparagraph twenty-eight, for the City of Manila, paragraph "f," section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:
"(a) Public Baths and Toilets, 4th District Construction
P15,000.00."
SEC. 2. Between items (a) and (b), page eighty-six, group IV, subparagraph twenty-eight, for the City of Manila, paragraph “f,” section two of the same Act, the following new item is inserted:2014
"(a-l) Araullo High School, Isaac Peral Branch Construction of fence
P 20,000.00" ."
SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
