Republic Act No. 5781
June 21, 1969
AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FIFTY-ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY-SEVEN (REPUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS IN THE PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES).
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (e), subparagraph 11, for the Province of Catanduanes, page 424, paragraph "a", Title E, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Fifty-one hundred eighty-seven is hereby amended to read as follows:
"(e) San Andres Seawall 110,000"
SEC. 2. Item 42, page 1211, paragraph "a", Title J, Section five of the same Act is hereby amended to read as follows:
"42. Port of Virac, Catanduanes, including shore protection 500,000"
SEC. 3. Item 107, page 1215, paragraph "b", Title J, Section five of the same Act is hereby amended to read as follows:

"107. Port of Calolbon, Catanduanes, including shore protection 200,000"

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
