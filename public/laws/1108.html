Republic Act No. 1108
June 15, 1954
AN ACT TO CREATE THE BARRIOS OF LINGASAD SILAWE, ISIS, AND LOBOC IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLANCO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Lingasad, Silawe, Isis, and Loboc in the municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are hereby constituted into barrios in the said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Lingasad, Silawe Isis, and Loboc, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1954.
