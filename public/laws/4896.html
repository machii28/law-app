Republic Act No. 4896
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SARAVIA IN THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL TO ENRIQUE B. MAGALONA IN HONOR OF THE LATE SENATOR ENRIQUE B. MAGALONA.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Saravia in the Province of Negros Occidental is hereby changed to Enrique B. Magalona in honor of the late Senator Enrique B. Magalona.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1967.
