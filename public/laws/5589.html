Republic Act No. 5589
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO CARISMOAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. BARBARA, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BARRIO GENERAL MARTIN DELGADO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Carismoan in the Municipality of Sta. Barbara, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Barrio General Martin Delgado.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
