Republic Act No. 2011
June 22, 1957
AN ACT APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL FUND FOR EMERGENCY LABORERS AND FOREMEN TO BE EMPLOYED IN THE PREVENTION OR CONTROL OF SCHISTOSOMIASIS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of thirty-four thousand five hundred pesos is appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, as additional fund to be expended by the Department of Health for emergency laborers and foremen to be employed by it in the prevention or control of schistosomiasis during the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred fifty-eight.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
