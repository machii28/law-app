Republic Act No. 7550
May 22, 1992
AN ACT DECLARING FEBRUARY 16 OF EVERY YEAR AS CALOOCAN CITY DAY, A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN CALOOCAN CITY, METRO MANILA
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. February 16 of every year is hereby declared as Caloocan City Day.

SEC. 2. February 16 of every year shall be a special nonworking holiday in Caloocan City, Metro Manila, and the city government and people therein are urged to celebrate the occasion with fitting ceremonies, activities and programs that are beneficial to all concerned.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 22, 1992.
