Republic Act No. 2118
April 5, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF DEL PILAR IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

Section 1. The sitio of Malaking Pook Ibaba is separated from the barrio of Malaking Pook, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, and is constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Del Pilar.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take the effect upon its Approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 5, 1959.
