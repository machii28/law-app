Republic Act No. 2780
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (14), PAGE 157, PARAGRAPH FOUR, SECTION ONE-a OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (14), page 157, paragraph four, Section One-a of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(14) New municipal building, Tanauan, Batangas—
Construction and/or improvement P20,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
