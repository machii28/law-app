Republic Act No. 4429
June 19, 1965
AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A VOCATIONAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINUKPUK, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, TO BE KNOWN AS PINUKPUK VOCATIONAL SCHOOL, AND TO AUTHORIZE THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the direct supervision, of the Director of Vocational Education, a vocational school in the Municipality of Pinukpuk, Mountain Province, to be known as Pinukpuk Vocational School.

SEC. 2. Upon the recommendation of the Director of Vocational Education, the President of the Philippines shall set aside and reserve such portion of the public lands located within the Municipality of Pinukpuk, Mountain Province, as may be necessary and convenient for the establishment of the school site.

SEC. 3. The sum of one hundred thousand, pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the said school for the first year. Thereafter, the necessary sums for said purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
