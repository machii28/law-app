Republic Act No. 2097
March 22, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BASILIO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO SAN JOSE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Basilio in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to San Jose.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, March 22, 1959.
