Republic Act No. 2763
June 19, 1960
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM 84, FOR THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, PAGE 184, PARAGRAPH g, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED THIRTEEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 84, for the Province of Pangasinan, page 184, paragraph g, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred thirteen is hereby amended to read as follows:

"84. Pogon-Sili Barrio School Building, Aguilar. P5,000.00."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
