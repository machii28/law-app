Republic Act No. 1326
June 16, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF TANIONGBOBOG, MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE'S POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO SARAZA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Tanongbobog, Municipality of Brooke's Point, Province of Palawan, is changed to Saraza.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
