Republic Act No. 2197
May 7, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN ISIDRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TINGLOY, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Banalo, Camastilisan, Pook, Pinagiluhan and Look in the barrio of Papaya, and the sitios of Nangcaan and Bucal in the barrio of Pisa, all in the Municipality of Tingloy, Province of Batangas, are separated from the said barrios, and are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Isidro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
