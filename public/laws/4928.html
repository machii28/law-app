Republic Act No. 4928
June 17, 1967
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONNER, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, TO BE KNOWN AS CONNER VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Director of Vocational Education, a vocational high school in the Municipality of Conner, Mountain Province, to be known as Conner Vocational High School.

SEC. 2. Upon recommendation of the Director of Vocational Education, the President of the Philippines shall set aside and reserve such portion of the public lands located within the Municipality of Conner, Mountain Province, as may be necessary and convenient for the establishment of the school site.

SEC. 3. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the said school for the first year. Thereafter, the necessary sums for said purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
