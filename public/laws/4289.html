Republic Act No. 4289
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF CARABAO ISLAND IN THE PROVINCE OF ROMBLON TO MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF SAN JOSE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipal District of Carabao Island in the Province of Roniblon shall hereafter be known for all intents and purposes as the Municipal District of San Jose.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
