Republic Act No. 3592
June 22, 1963
AN ACT INCREASING THE BASE PAY OF COMMISSIONED OFFICERS OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The monthly base pay of commissioned Officers of the Armed Forces of the Philippines shall not be less than the following:
Second Lieutenant or Ensign

P320.00

First Lieutenant or lieutenant Junior
Grade
370.00
Captain or Lieutenant Senior Grade
450.00
Major or Lieutenant Commander
550.00
Lieutenant Colonel or Commander
700.00
Colonel or Captain (PN)
900.00
Brigadier General or Commodore
1,100.00
Major General or Rear Admiral
1,300.00
Lieutenant General or Chief of Staff
1,500.00


SEC. 2. The rates of pay prescribed by Executive Order Numbered Twenty-three dated October first nineteen hundred and sixty-two is modified accordingly.

SEC. 3. The increase in salaries as provided for in this Act shall be paid from out of any funds in the National Treasury, not otherwise appropriated. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary to pay the increased salaries as provided for in this Act shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect on July first nineteen hundred and sixty-three.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
