Republic Act No. 2773
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NIPA BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF NIPA, MUNICIPALITY OF PALAPAG, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, TO BERNABE QUIBAL MEMORIAL COMMUNITY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Nipa Barrio School in the barrio of Nipa, Municipality of Palapag, Province of Samar, is changed to Bernabe Quibal Memorial Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
