Republic Act No. 9429
April 10, 2007
AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER IS OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT, TO COMMEMORATE ITS CHARTER DAY ANNIVERSARY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 18 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the City of Tacurong, Province of Sultan Kudarat to commemorate its charter day anniversary.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 7, 2007 and January 22, 2007, respectively.
