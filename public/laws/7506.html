Republic Act No. 7506
May 18, 1992
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABAGAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE DELFIN ALBANO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled

SECTION. 1. There shall be established a high school in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, to be known as the Delfin Albano High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 18, 1992.
