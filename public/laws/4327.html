Republic Act No. 4327
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GOVANTES DIKE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIGAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO ISABELO DE LOS REYES BIKE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Govantes Dike in the Municipality of Vigan, Province of Ilocos Sur, is changed to Isabelo de los Reyes Dike.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
