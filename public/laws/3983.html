Republic Act No. 3983
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CREATING THE POSITION OF ONE ASSISTANT CITY FISCAL FOR CAVITE CITY AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION TWENTY-FOUR OF THE CHARTER OF SAID CITY.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The first paragraph of Section twenty-four of Commonwealth Act Numbered Five hundred forty-seven, otherwise known as the Charter of the City of Cavite, as amended, is further amended to read as follows:

"Sec. 24. The City fiscal—His assistant—His powers and duties.&mdashThe Law Department shall consist of the city fiscal who shall be the chief adviser of the city and one assistant city fiscal. The city fiscal shall receive a salary of not exceeding five thousand seven hundred pesos per annum and the assistant city fiscal, not exceeding five thousand one hundred pesos per annum. SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
