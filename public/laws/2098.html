Republic Act No. 2098
March 28, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF BAGONG SILANG, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF BAGONG SILANG.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Bagong Silang, Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bulacan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Bagong Silang.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, March 28, 1959.
