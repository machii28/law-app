Republic Act No. 1935
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE CENTRAL ANTIQUE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUGASONG, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, INTO A VOCATIONAL SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE ANTIQUE VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Central High School in the Municipality of Bugasong, Province of Antique, is hereby converted into a vocational high school to be known as the Antique Vocational High School. The said school shall be under the supervision of the Bureau of Public Schools and subject to the provisions of the School Law. Courses of secondary level shall be offered in the said school.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the expenses of the said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred fifty-eight. The necessary funds for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
