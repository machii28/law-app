Republic Act No. 1177
June 18, 1954
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, FROM ITS PRESENT SITE TO THE SITE OF THE BARRIO OF AUITAN IN THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The site of the seat of government in the municipality of San Pablo, Province of Isabela, is transferred to the site of the barrio of Auitan in the same municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1954.
