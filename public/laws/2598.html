Republic Act No. 2598
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF KAGA-OLAS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MATA-NAO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO SWERTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Kaga-olas in the Municipality of Matanao, Province of Davao, is changed to Swerte.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
