Republic Act No. 4343
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF VENTURANZA BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF VENTURANZA, MUNICIPALITY OF BAKGA, PROVINCE OF AKLAN, TO DON EDECIO S. VENTURANZA MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Venturanza Barrio School in the barrio of Venturanza, Municipality of Banga, Province of Aldan, is hereby changed to Don Edecio S. Venturanza Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
