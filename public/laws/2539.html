Republic Act No. 2539
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BOGTONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUSUANGA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Bogtong in the Municipality of Busuanga, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bogtong.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
