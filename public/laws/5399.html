Republic Act No. 5399
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO DINOKOT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINDANGAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, TO BARRIO RICARDO G. MACIAS.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Dinokot in the Municipality of Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, hereby changed to Barrio Ricardo G. Macias.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
