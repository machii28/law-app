Republic Act No. 5033
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MANALPAC SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOLSONA, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO MANALPAC-MARIQUET PRIMARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Manalpac School in the Municipality of Solsona, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Manalpac-Mariquet Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
