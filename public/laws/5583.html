Republic Act No. 5583
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO AGLIPAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF BOHOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sitio Aglipay in the Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bohol, is hereby created and constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Aglipay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
