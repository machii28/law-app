Republic Act No. 3356
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN ANTONIO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PUERTO GALERA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of San Antonio in the Municipality of Puerto Galera, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Antonio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
