Republic Act No. 2795
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Tuao, Province of Cagayan, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to wit:
  1. Sitio Pata to Barrio Pata;
  2. Sitio Dagupan to Barrio Dagupan;
  3. Sitio Palca to Barrio Palca;
  4. Sitio Naruangan to Barrio Naruangan;
  5. Sitio Bugnay to Barrio Bugnay;
  6. Sitio San Juan to Barrio San Juan;
  7. Sitio Moleg to Barrio Moleg;
  8. Sitio Taribugu to Barrio Taribugu;
  9. Sitio Culung to Barrio Culung;
  10. Sitio Bicok to Barrio Bicok;
  11. Sitio Talia to Barrio Talia;
  12. Sitio Malummin to Barrio Malummin;
  13. Sitio Gurengad to Barrio Gurengad;
  14. Sitio Angang to Barrio Angang; and
  15. Sitio Lalambini to Barrio Lalambini.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
