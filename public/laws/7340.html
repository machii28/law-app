Republic Act No. 7340
April 1, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE JOSE ABAD SANTOS HIGH SCHOOL TO PAMPANGA HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Jose Abad Santos High School in San Fernando, Pampanga, shall hereafter be known and referred to as the Pampanga High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 4, 1991 and January 23, 1992, respectively.
