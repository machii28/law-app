Republic Act No. 1491
June 16, 1956
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM NINETY, GROUP PARAGRAPH "m," FOR THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item ninety, group II, paragraph "m" for the Province of Camarines Sur, section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"90. San Jose Home Economics Building, San Jose—
Completion P2,500.00."


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
