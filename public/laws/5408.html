Republic Act No. 5408
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANCING THE NAME OF BARRIO SAN MAGRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARCOS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO SANTIAGO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio San Magro in the Municipality of Marcos, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Santiago.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
