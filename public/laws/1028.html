Republic Act No. 1028
June 12, 1954
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF CARAYAT, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRIETO DIAZ, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, INTO A BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Carayat, in the municipality of Prieto Diaz, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby converted into a barrio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
