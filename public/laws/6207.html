Republic Act No. 6207
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CATUBIG RURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATUBIG, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR, TO PEDRO REBADULLA MEMORIAL AGRICULTURAL HIGH SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Catubig Rural High School in the Municipality of Catubig, Province of Northern Samar, is hereby changed to Pedro Rebadulla Memorial Agricultural High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
