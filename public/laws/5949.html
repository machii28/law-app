Republic Act No. 5949
June 21, 1969
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NATIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAFT, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, TO BE KNOWN AS SAMAR NATIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Director of Vocational Education, a national school of arts and trades in the Municipality of Taft, Province of Samar, to be known as Samar National School of Arts and Trades. Courses on the secondary level shall be offered in this school effective upon availability of funds.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation, and maintenance of said school during the first year of its operation. Such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
