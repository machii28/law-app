Republic Act No. 2134
April 7, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF MANGGAHAN IN THE BARRIO OF SAN MARTIN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN LUIS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, INTO A BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Manggahan in the barrio of San Martin, Municipality of San Luis, Province of Batangas, is hereby separated from the said barrio and converted into a new barrio to be known as the barrio of Manggahan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
