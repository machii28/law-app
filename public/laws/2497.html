Republic Act No. 2497
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF LUCBAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ABULUG, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Lucban in the Municipality of Abulug, Province of Cagayan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Lucban.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
