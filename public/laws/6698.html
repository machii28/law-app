Republic Act No. 6698
February 10, 1989
AN ACT RENAMING THE BATITI PRIMARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BATITI, MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE BORRES-POSADAS PRIMARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Batiti Primary School in Barangay Batiti, Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Borres-Posadas Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,
