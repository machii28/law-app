Republic Act No. 2555
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PULANTUBIG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AT DUMAGUETE CITY TO RAMON MAGSAYSAY MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Pulantubig Elementary School at Dumaguete City is changed to Ramon Magsaysay Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
