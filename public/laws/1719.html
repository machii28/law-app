Republic Act No. 1719
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TABAÑGO, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Gibacungan, Catmon and Manlawa-an, Municipality of Tabañgo, Province of Leyte, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Gibacungan, Catmon and Manlawa-an, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
