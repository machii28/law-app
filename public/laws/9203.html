Republic Act No. 9203
April 20, 2003
AN ACT DECLARING THE FIFTEENTH (15TH) DAY OF AUGUST OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN MUNICIPALITY OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS "CATARMAN DAY"
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. August Fifteenth (15th) every year is hereby declared as special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Catarman, Province of Camiguin, to commemorate its foundation day anniversary.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON (Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR.
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 3, 2003 and January 22, 2003, respectively.

Lapsed into law on APR 20 2003, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
