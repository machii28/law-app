Republic Act No. 5686
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO SAMPAGUITA IN LIPA CITY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Sarapalocan in Barrio Cumba, Lipa City, is hereby separated from said barrio, and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said city, to be known as Barrio Sampaguita.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
