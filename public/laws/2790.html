Republic Act No. 2790
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIRAC, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following places in the Municipality of Virac, Province of Catanduanes, are declared as barrios of said municipality, to wit:

  1. Pajo as Barrio Bagui, Pajo;
  2. Bigaa as Barrio Bigaa;
  3. Calampong as Barrio Calampong;
  4. Buenavista as Barrio Buenavista;
  5. Capilihan as Barrio Capilihan;
  6. Dugui (TO-O) as Barrio Dugui (TO-O);
  7. Hawan (Ilaya) as Barrio Hawan (Ilaya);
  8. Igang as Barrio Igang;
  9. Palta (Small) as Barrio Palta (Small);
  10. Sto. Cristo (Cabijian) as Barrio Sto. Cristo (Cabijian) ;
  11. San Isidro (Dugui Wala) as Barrio San Isidro (Dugui Wala);
  12. San Vicente (Dugui Wala) as Barrio San Vicente (Dugui Wala);
  13. San Isidro, Pajo, as Barrio San Isidro, Pajo; and
  14. Valencia as Barrio Valencia.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
