Republic Act No. 1109
June 15, 1954
AN ACT TO CREATE THE BARRIO OF LIBUTON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANUCAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Libuton in the municipality of Manucan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Libuton.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1954.
