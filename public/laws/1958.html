Republic Act No. 1958
June 22, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM EIGHTEEN FOR THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, SUBSECTION (a), TITLE A, SECTION ONE, REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIX HUNDRED AND SEVENTY, ENTITLED "AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item eighteen for the Province of Negros Oriental, subsection (a), title A, section one, Republic Act Numbered Six hundred and seventy, page eighty-five, is hereby amended to read as follows:
"18. For the purchase of the Mantuyop,
Siaton, primary school site and
improvement thereof P2,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
