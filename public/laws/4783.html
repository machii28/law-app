Republic Act No. 4783
June 18, 1966
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO SCHOOL IN MALIGAYA, MUNICIPAUTY OF TARLAC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC, FROM BUENAVISTA-TETE-UNGOT PRIMARY SCHOOL TO MALIGAYA PRIMARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio school in Maligaya, Municipality of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac, is hereby changed from Buenavista-Tete-Ungot Primary School to Maligaya Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
