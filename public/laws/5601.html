Republic Act No. 5601
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TANZA PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF ROXAS TO JUDGE MAMERTO ROXAS MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Tanza Elementary School in the City of Roxas is hereby changed to Judge Mamerto Roxas Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
