Republic Act No. 2115
April 5, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BETON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Island of Icaduang Banua in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, is constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Beton.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 5, 1959.
