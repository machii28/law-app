Republic Act No. 2779
June 19, 1960
AN ACT FIXING THE BOUNDARIES OF THE BARRIOS OF BINAGBAG AND SALVACION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGDANGAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of Binagbag in the Municipality of Agdangan, Province of Quezon, shall be bounded on the North by the Manila Railroad Line; on the East by Lots Numbers 387, 386, 385, 382, and Bolo River; on the West by Lots Numbers 426, 425, 424, 423, 421, 3175, 420, and 418, and on the South by Tayabas Bay.

The barrio of Salvacion in the Municipality of Agdangan, Province of Quezon, shall be bounded on the North by the Manila Railroad Line; on the East by Lots Numbers 422, 323, 413, 414, 415, and 417; on the West by Lots Numbers 816 1125, 818, 456, 452, and 453, and on the South by Tayabas Bay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
