Republic Act No. 2161
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIOS OF TUROD, PACO, CALAMAGUI AND K1M-MALABASA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGSAYSAY, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, INTO BARRIOS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Turod, Paco, Calamagui Kim-malabasa in the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province of Isabela, are hereby converted into barrios.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
