Republic Act No. 4899
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF JUBAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JUBAN, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, TO JOSE G. ALINDOGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Juban Elementary School in the Municipality of Juban, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby changed to Jose G. Alindogan Elementary School.
Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
