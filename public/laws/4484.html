Republic Act No. 4484
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO CALUNGBUYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO AGLIPAY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Calungbuyan, Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to be known as Barrio Aglipay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
