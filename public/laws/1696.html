Republic Act No. 1696
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF CURBA, MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA FE PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BARRIO SAN ROQUE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Curba, Municipality of Santa Fe, Province of Leyte, is changed to barrio San Rogue.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
