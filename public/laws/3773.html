Republic Act No. 3773
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CAMAMBUGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF CAMAMBUGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF DAET, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO FRANCISCA BALDOVINO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Camambugan Elementary School in the barrio of Camambugan, Municipality of Daet, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Francisca Baldovino Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
