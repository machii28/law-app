Republic Act No. 9330
August 8, 2004
AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SAN ANTONIO AIRPORT IN THE ISLAND-MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ANTONIO, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. To enhance tourism in the Island-Municipality of San Antonio, Province of Northern Samar, which is endowed with exotic white sand beaches, there is hereby established in said, municipality an airport to be known as the San Antonio Airport.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation and Communications shall include in its program the establishment and operationalization of the San Antonio Airport, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,
