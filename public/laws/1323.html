Republic Act No. 1323
June 16, 1955
AN ACT TO CREATE THE BARRIOS OF TALISAY, LINUNGGANAN, SUKAILANG, AND BIYABID, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SURIGAO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Talisay, Linungganan, Suka-ilang, and Biyabid, in the Municipality of Surigao, Province of Surigao, are hereby constituted into barrios in the same municipality, to be known as Talisay, Linungganan, Sukailang and Biyabid, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
