Republic Act No. 2150
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN VICENTE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is created a barrio in the Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, to be known as the barrio of San Vicente, and which shall be bounded on the north by the barrio of San Diego, on the east by the barrio Alagao, on the south by the barrio of Baguilaua, and on the West by the school building of the barrio of Manalupang and a line running northward and southward from said building.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
