Republic Act No. 1331
June 16, 1955
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF SAN VICENTE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IVANA, PROVINCE OF BATANES, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF SAN VICENTE OF SAID MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and Bouse of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of San Vicente in the Municipality of Ivana, Province of Batanes, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of San Vicente of said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
