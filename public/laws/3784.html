Republic Act No. 3784
June 22, 1963
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE ADDITIONAL SUM OF FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE PRINTING AND PUBLICATION IN BOOK FORM THE SPEECHES, MESSAGES, AND OTHER WRITINGS OF THE LATE PRESIDENT MANUEL LUIS QUEZON.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the printing and publication in book form the speeches, messages, and other writings of the late President Manuel Luis Quezon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
