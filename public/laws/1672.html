Republic Act No. 1672
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF BUGANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIO V. CORPUS, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO BUGANG.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Bugang in the Municipality of Pio V. Corpus, Province of Masbate, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as barrio Bugang.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
