Republic Act No. 1715
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF PINANA-AN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARABUT, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, INTO A REGULAR BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Pinana-an in the Municipality of Marabut, Province of Samar, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known henceforth as the barrio of Pinalangga.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
