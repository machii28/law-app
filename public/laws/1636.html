Republic Act No. 1636
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAAYON BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF MAAYON, MUNICIPALITY OF PONTEVEDRA, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO ARTURO CANTILLER MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Maayon Barrio School in the barrio of Maayon, Municipality of Pontevedra, Province of Capiz, is hereby changed to Arturo Cantiller Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
