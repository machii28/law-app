Republic Act No. 1492
June 16, 1956

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Items (g) and (h), Group II, subparagraph thirty-six, paragraph (a), Title E, section one, Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred and twenty are hereby amended to read as follows:

"(g) Dauin waterworks—

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
