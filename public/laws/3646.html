Republic Act No. 3646
June 22, 1963
AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF ONE EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INITAO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, AND TO AUTHORIZE THE APPRORIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Director of Medical Services, one emergency hospital in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis Oriental, to be known as the Initao Emergency Hospital.

SEC. 2. The sum of four hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for establishment, operation and maintenance of said hospital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-four. Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for the operation and maintenance of said hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
