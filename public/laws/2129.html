Republic Act No. 2129
April 7, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF NAGANACAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO NAGANACAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and Home of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Naganacan in the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Province of Isabela, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as barrio Naganacan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
