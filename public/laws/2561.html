Republic Act No. 2561
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING BARRIOS IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Nasugbu, Province of Batangas, are hereby converted into barrios of the said municipality, as follows:
Sitio Lumbañgan to Barrio Lumbañgan;
Sitio Balaytigui to Barrio Balaytigui;
Sitio Malapad na Bato to Barrio Malapad na Bato;
Sitio Aga to Barrio Aga;
Sitio Kayrilao to Barrio Kayrilao; and
Sitio Mataas na Pulo to Barrio Mataas na Pulo.
SEC. 2. The sitio of Sambal in the Municipality of Lemery, Province of Batangas, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Sambal.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
