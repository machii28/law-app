Republic Act No. 3729
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF EL-VITA TARITIEN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ABORLAN, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of El-Vita Taritien in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of El-Vita Taritien.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
