SEC. 2. The provisions of any law, rule or regulation to the contrary notwithstanding, the incumbents to the positions mentioned in Section one hereof shall continue to occupy their respective positions and receive the salary rates as provided in this Act without the necessity of issuing new appointments to them, except for those whose designations were changed under the said section, who shall automatically be issued appointments corresponding to their respective new designations at rates herein respectively provided for them.

SEC. 3. Such sums as may be necessary to cover the salary differentials of the personnel provided in Section one are hereby appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated. Henceforth, appropriations to cover their salaries, as herein fixed, shall be carried in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. All Acts, executive orders, administrative orders, rules and regulations or parts thereof, inconsistent with any of the provisions of this Act, are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

SEC. 5. Effective Date.—This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, Aug. 4, 1969.
