Republic Act No. 2580
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SITIO OF BURAL IN THE BARRIO OF ZINUNDUNGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO SAN JUAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the sitio of Bural in the barrio of Zinundungan, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Cagayan, is changed to San Juan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
