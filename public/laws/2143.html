Republic Act No. 2143
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BA&RIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OP BULAN, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Cabogaan, San Juan Daan, Cadandanan, Tanga, Togbogñon and Bagnog in the Municipality of Bulan, Province of Sorsogon, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Cabogaan, San Juan Daan, Cadandanan, Tanga, San Rafael and Magsaysay, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
