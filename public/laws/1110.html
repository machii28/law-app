Republic Act No. 1110
June 15, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BUKAW IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IGBARAS, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO THAT OF ALAMEDA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION l. The name of the barrio of Bukaw in the Municipality of Igbaras, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to that of Alameda.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1954.
