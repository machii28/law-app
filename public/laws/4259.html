Republic Act No. 4259
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PANALAWAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BUBONG, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, TO MARIGA ALONTO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Panalawan Elementary School in the Municipal District of Bubong, Province of Lanao del Sur, is changed to Mariga Alonto Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
