Republic Act No. 995
June 8, 1954
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF TWO HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS, OUT OF ANY FUNDS IN THE NATIONAL TREASURY NOT OTHERWISE APPROPRIATED, AS AID TO THE CITY OF TAGAYTAY TO HELP SAID CITY FINANCE THE RECONSTRUCTION OF ITS URGENT PUBLIC IMPROVEMENTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In order to help the City of Tagaytay fi- nance reconstruction and repair of its urgent public improvements there is hereby appropriated the sum of two hundred fifty thousand pesos as aid to said city.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 8, 1954.
