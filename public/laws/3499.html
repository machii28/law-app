Republic Act No. 3499
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TIAONG CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIAONG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO CLARO M. RECTO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Tiaong Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Claro M. Recto Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
