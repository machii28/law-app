Republic Act No. 4044
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LUBUAGAN HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUBUAGAN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, TO KALINGA HOSPITAL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Lubuagan Hospital in the Municipality of Lubuagan, Mountain Province, is hereby changed to Kalinga Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
