Republic Act No. 1641
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PUERTO RIVAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF PUERTO RIVAS, MUNICIPALITY OF BALANGA, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, TO MAXIMINO DE LOS REYES MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Puerto Rivas Elementary School in the barrio of Puerto Rivas, Municipality of Balanga, Province of Bataan, is hereby changed to Maximino de los Reyes Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
