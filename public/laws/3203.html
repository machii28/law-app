Republic Act No. 3203
June 17, 1961
AN ACT TO AMEND REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED EIGHTEEN HUNDRED FORTY-FIVE, ENTITLED "AN ACT GRANTING THE TAGUM AGRICUL-TURAL DEVELOPMENT COMPANY, INC., A TEMPORARY PERMIT TO CONSTRUCT, MAINTAIN AND OPERATE PRIVATE FIXED POINT-TO-POINT AND LAND-BASED RADIO STATIONS FOR THE RECEPTION AND TRANSMISSION OF RADIO COMMUNICATIONS WITHIN THE PHILIPPINES."


Be it enacted by the Senate and, liouse of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section six of Republic Act Numbered Eighteen hundred forty-five is hereby repealed.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1961.
