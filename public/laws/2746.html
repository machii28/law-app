Republic Act No. 2746
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING BARRIOS IN THE THIRD DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF SAMAR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Libertad in the Municipality of Dolores, Province of Samar, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Libertad.

SEC. 2. The sitios of Camada, Guindalitan and Tagaslian in the Municipality of Maydolong, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Camada, Guindalitan and Tagaslian, respectively.

SEC. 3. The sitio of Cambilla in the Municipality of Quinapundan, Province of Samar, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Cambilla.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
