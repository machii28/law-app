Republic Act No. 10104
May 14, 2010
AN ACT CONVERTING THE INTERIOR CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD OF LANAO DEL NORTE, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The interior circumferential road which starts from the Iligan — Aurora National Highway at Iligan and traverses Tagoloan, Baloi, Pantao — Ragat, Munai, Tangkal, Magsaysay, Tubod, Salvador, Sapad, Nunungan and ends at Malabang — Tukuran National Highway at Sultan Naga Dimaporo, Province of Lanao del Norte is hereby converted into a national road.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
