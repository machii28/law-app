Republic Act No. 1442
June 14, 1956
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO SOLEDAD IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINALBAGAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL TO BARRIO PAYAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Soledad in the Municipality of Binalbagan, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Barrio Payao.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1956.
