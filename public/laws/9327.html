Republic Act No. 9327
August 8, 2004
AN ACT CONVERTING THE RIZAL PROVINCIAL ROAD IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF KALINGA INTO A NATIONAL ROAD AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Rizal Provincial Road in the Municipality of Rizal, Province of Kalinga is hereby converted into a national road under the supervision of the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH).

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the DPWH shall include in its program the improvement, maintenance and repair of the said road, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,
