Republic Act No. 10671
August 19, 2015
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GUBAT DISTRICT HOSPITAL TO SALVADOR R. ENCINAS DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN GUBAT, SORSOGON
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Change of Hospital Name. – The name of Gubat District Hospital in the Municipality of Gubat, Province of Sorsogon is hereby changed to Salvador R. Encinas District Hospital.

SEC. 2. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in any newspaper of general circulation.
