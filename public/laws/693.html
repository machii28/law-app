Republic Act No. 693
May 9, 1952
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SITIO VICTORY, MUNICIPALITY OF CABADBARAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN, TO DOÑA ROSARIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of sitio Victory, Municipality of Cabadbaran, Province of Agusan, is hereby changed to Doña Rosario.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
