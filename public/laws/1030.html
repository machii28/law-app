Republic Act No. 1030
June 12, 1954
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE NAGCARLAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN NAGCARLAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO CRISANTO GUYSAYKO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the only son of Nagcarlan, Province of Laguna, who became three times Congressman from the second district of the Province of Laguna, the name of Nagcarlan Elementary School in Nagcarlan, Province of Laguna, is hereby changed to Crisanto Guysayko Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
