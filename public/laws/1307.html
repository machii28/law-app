Republic Act No. 1307
June 16, 1955
AN ACT CONVERTING CERTAIN SITIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BABAK, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, INTO BARRIOS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Mambago, San Isidro, Sto. Nino, San Antonio, San Agustin, Dangcaan, Balet, Tambo, Camudmud, and Cogo, in the Municipality of Babak, Province of Davao, are converted into barrios.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
