Republic Act No. 2203
May 7, 1959
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE DULAO PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF DULAO, MUNICIPALITY OF BAGO, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO VIDAL CAUNTOY PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Dulao Primary School in the barrio of Dulao, Municipality of Bago, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Vidal Cauntoy Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
