Republic Act No. 2136
April 7, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OP MALIBAYO, MUNICIPALITY OF BATANGAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF MALIBAYO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Malibayo, barrio of Simlong, Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Malibayo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
