Republic Act No. 2860
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE APPROPRIATION OF FOUR THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE CAMBIJANG BARRIO SCHOOL, CAJIDIOCAN, ROMBLON, TO LUMBANG COMMUNITY SCHOOL OF THE SAME MUNCIPALITY, BY AMENDING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY HUNDRED NINETY-THREE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (n) under (46) Romblon, paragraph f, Title M, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty hundred ninety-three, is amended to read as follows:

"(n) Lumbang Community School, Cajidiocan—
Construction of one room ......... P4,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
