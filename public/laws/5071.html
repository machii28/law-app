Republic Act No. 5071
June 17, 1967
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OP TWO HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF CAGAYAN VALLEY AGRICULTURAL COLLEGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LALLO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of Cagayan Valley Agricultural College in the Municipality of Lallo, Province of Cagayan. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
