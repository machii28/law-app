Republic Act No. 2461
June 21, 1959
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM (g), PAGE 258, PARAGRAPH f, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (g), for the Province of Cebu, page 258, paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
"(g)
Guadalupe Elementary School, Carcar, Construction of one room standard shop building, Noel type—size: 6.90 X 8-55 M
P5.000.00."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
