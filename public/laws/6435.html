Republic Act No. 6435
June 17, 1972
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN MARCELINO EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MARCELINO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, TO MAYOR LEOPOLDO F. RABANES MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION .1. The name of San Marcelino Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of San Marcelino, Province of Zambales, is hereby changed to Mayor Leopoldo F. Rabanes Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1972.
