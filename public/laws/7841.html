Republic Act No. 7841
December 16, 1994
AN ACT CREATING THE PARAÑAQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PARAÑAQUE, METRO MANILA, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be a Parañaque National High School in the Municipality of Parañaque, Metro Manila, under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

Approved, December 16, 1994.
