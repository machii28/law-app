Republic Act No. 4342
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CONVERTING DUEÑAS HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUEÑAS, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS DUEÑAS GENERAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Dueñas High School is hereby converted into a national high school, to be known as Dueñas General Comprehensive High School. Comprehensive curriculum which includes both academic and industrial arts and handicraft courses shall be offered. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize the said school accordingly.

SEC. 2. Upon organization of Dueñas General Comprehensive High School, the assets, liabilities, personnel, equipment and records of Dueñas High School shall stand transferred to said Dueñas General Comprehensive High School.

SEC. 3. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said school in addition to its matriculation and tuition fees, and other revenues.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
