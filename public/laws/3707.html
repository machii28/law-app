Republic Act No. 3707
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SULU TRADE SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JOLO, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO HADJI BUTU SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Sulu Trade School in the Municipality of Jolo, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed to Hadji Butu School of Arts and Trades.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
