Republic Act No. 5633
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF KAMUNING ROAD, KAMUNING DISTRICT, QUEZON CITY, TO BENEDICTO PADILLA AVENUE IN HONOR OF THE LATE CONGRESSMAN BENEDICTO PADILLA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Hereafter, Kamuning Road in Kamuning District, Quezon City, shall be known as Benedicto Padilla Avenue in honor of the late Congressman Benedicto Pa­dilla of the First District of the Province of Rizal.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
