Republic Act No. 4817
June 18, 1966
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF SOUTHERN LEYTE GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and Home of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of Southern Leyte General Hospital in the Province of Southern Leyte is hereby increased from twenty-live beds to seventy-five beds.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be
appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise
appropriated, to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
