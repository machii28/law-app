Republic Act No. 2530
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE IROSIN HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IROSIN, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, TO GALLANOSA HIGH SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Irosin High School in the Municipality of Irosin, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby changed to Gallanosa High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
