Republic Act No. 2520
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIBOG, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, TO MUNICIPALITY OF STO. DOMINGO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipality of Libog, Province of Albay, shall hereafter be known as the Municipality of Sto. Domingo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
