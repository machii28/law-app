Republic Act No. 1181
June 20, 1954
AN ACT TO TRANSFER THE MUNICIPAL SITE OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUPAX, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, FROM ITS PRESENT LOCATION TO THE BARRIO OF MALASIN OF THE SAID MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The municipal site of the municipality of Dupax, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, is transferred from its present location to the barrio of Malasin of the said municipality

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 20, 1954.
