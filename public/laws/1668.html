Republic Act No. 1668
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF DAMPIGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA RITA, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, INTO A BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Dampigan in the Municipality of Santa Rita, Province of Samar, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Dampigan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
