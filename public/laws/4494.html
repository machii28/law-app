Republic Act No. 4494
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO ABAROAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sitios Baututo and Egruroan in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, are hereby constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Abaroan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
