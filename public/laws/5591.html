Republic Act No. 5591
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO PASONG INCH1K IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN RAFAEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO BARRIO SAN ISIDRO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio1 Pasong Inchik in the Municipality of San Rafael, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to Barrio San Isidro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
