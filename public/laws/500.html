Republic Act No. 500
June 12, 1950
AN ACT TO TRANSFER CERTAIN BARRIOS FROM THE TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION OF THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BONTOC TO THAT OF THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF SADANGA, SUB-PROVINCE OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrios of Betwagan and Anabel, in the municipal district of Bontoc, are transferred from the said municipal district to that of the municipal district of Sadanga, Sub-province of Bontoc, Mountain Province.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1950.
