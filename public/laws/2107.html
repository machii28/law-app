Republic Act No. 2107
April 3, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF CAPATAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Capatan in the barrio of Libag, Municipality of Tuguegarao, Province of Cagayan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Capatan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 3, 1959.
