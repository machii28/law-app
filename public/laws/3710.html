Republic Act No. 3710
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PARANG CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PARANG, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO AKUK SANGKULA CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Parang Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Parang, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed to Akuk Sangkula Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
