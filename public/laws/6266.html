Republic Act No. 6266
June 19, 1971
AN ACT DECLARING MALINDANG MOUNTAIN IN THE ISLAND OF MINDANAO A NATIONAL PARK AND WATERSHED AREA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Malindang Mountain in the Island of Mindanao is hereby declared a national park and watershed area, without prejudice to rights already acquired and held by private persons in that area.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
