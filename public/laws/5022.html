Republic Act No. 5022
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BAGOÑGON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO NARCISO CENTENO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives-of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Bagoñgon Elementary School in the Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Narciso Centeno Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
