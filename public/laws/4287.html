Republic Act No. 4287
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TAGUMPAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Buayan in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Tagumpay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
