Republic Act No. 2063
June 13, 1958
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF FIVE MILLION SIX HUNDRED NINETY-FOUR THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED FIFTY-FOUR PESOS FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED THIRTY-ONE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of five million six hundred ninety-four thousand eight hundred fifty-four pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to implement the provisions of Republic Act Numbered One thousand eight hundred thirty-one from the date of its effectivity.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 13, 1958.
