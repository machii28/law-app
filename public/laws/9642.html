Republic Act No. 9642
June 11, 2009
AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 16 OF EVERY YEAR AS ROMBLON FOUNDATION DAY AND A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ROMBLON


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:


SECTION 1. March 16 of every year is hereby declared as Romblon Foundation Day a special nonworking public holiday in the Province of Romblon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days following its publication in two (2) national newspaper of general circulation.

Approved,
