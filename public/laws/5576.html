Republic Act No. 5576
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE'NAME OF BARRIO LANG-ANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GLORIA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO BARRIO SANTA THERESA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, The name of the barrio of Langang in the Municipality of Gloria, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Barrio Santa Theresa.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
