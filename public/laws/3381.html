Republic Act No. 3381
June 18, 1961
AN ACT DECLARING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ORAS, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, AS DULY CONSTITUTED BARRIOS THEREOF.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrios of Pangodtan, Sabang, Gamot, Cabagakyan and Himamayo in the Municipality of Oras, Province of Samar, are hereby declared as duly constituted barrios of said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
