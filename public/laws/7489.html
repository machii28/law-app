Republic Act No. 7489
May 13, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BALIWAG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALLANES, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO PACIFICO VILLACARLOS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Baliwag elementary School in the Municipality of Magallanes, Provinc of Cavite, is hereby changed to Pacifico Villacarlos Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 13, 1992.
