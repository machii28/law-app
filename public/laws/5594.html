Republic Act No. 5594
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO PANGI IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MACO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, TO BARRIO GAUDENCIO ANTONINO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Pangi in the Municipality of Maco, Province of Davao del Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Gaudencio Antonino.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
