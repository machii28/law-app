Republic Act No. 6694
February 10, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NAVALAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY NAVALAN, MUNICIPALITY OF TUKURAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA, DEL SUR, TO MARGARITO V. LAGARE MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Navalan Elementary School in Barangay Navalan, Municipality of Tukuran, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is hereby changed to Margarito V. Lagare Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA
 (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 22, 1988 and December 2, 1988, respectively.
