Republic Act No. 2559
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING BARRIOS IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Labrador, Province of Pangasinan, are constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, as follows:
Sitios of Tokoc, Polowen and the adjoining part of Barrio Bolo bordering Silag to Barrio Magsaysay.
SEC. 2. The following sitios in the Municipality of Mabini, Province of Pangasinan, are constituted into a barrio of the said municipality, as follows:

Sitios Loboc, Lalasoran, Libsong and Biga to Barrio Kaabiañgan.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
