Republic Act No. 5690
June 21, 1969
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF LIPA CITY HOSPITAL AND CONVERTING THE SAME INTO A NATIONAL HOSPITAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and Souse of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Lipa City Hospital in Lipa City is hereby converted into a national hospital with an authorized bed capacity of fifty beds.

SEC. 2. The additional sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated in the next General Appropriations Act for its maintenance and operation.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
