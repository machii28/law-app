Republic Act No. 10281
November 15, 2012
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BIGNAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BIGNAY, CITY OF VALENZUELA TO ROBERTA DE JESUS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of an Elementary School. – The name of Bignay Elementary School in Barangay Bignay, City of Valenzuela is hereby changed to Roberta de Jesus Elementary School.

SEC. 2. Location of the Main Campus. – The main campus of Roberta de Jesus Elementary School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Bignay, City of Valenzuela.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
