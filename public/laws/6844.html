Republic Act No. 6844
January 18, 1990
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MARCOS BRIDGE IN BARANGAY LAUIS, MUNICIPALITY OF CANDELARIA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, TO LAUIS BRIDGE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Marcos Bridge in Barangay Lauis, Municipality of Candelaria, Province of Zambales, is hereby changed to Lauis Bridge.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 6, 1988 and September 28, 1989, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDWIN P. ACOBA (Sgd.) QUIRINO D. ABAD SANTOS, JR.
Secretary of the Senate Secretary of the House of Representatives

Approved: (Lapsed into law on January 18, 1990 without the President's signature in accordance with Article VI, Sec. 27(1), of the Constitution)

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO
President of the Philippines
