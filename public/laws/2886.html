Republic Act No. 2886
June 19, 1960
AN ACT REAPPROPRIAT1NG THE SUM OF TWO THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED PESOS APPROPRIATED UNDER ITEM (ak), PAGE 232, GROUP II, SUBPARAGRAPH 28, FOR THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE, PARAGRAPH f, TITLE M, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWO THOUSAND NINETY-THREE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of two thousand live hundred pesos appropriated under item (ak), page 232, group II, subparagraph 28, for the Province of Leyte, paragraph f, Title M, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Two thousand ninety-three, to wit:

"(ak) Victory Barrio School, Matalom
Construction and/or improvement ..........P2,500.00"

is hereby re-appropriated for the construction of a school building in the barrio of Taylan, Municipality of Matalom, Province of Leyte.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
