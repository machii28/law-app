Republic Act No. 4588
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TALIWA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALITBOG, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The remaining portion of the poblacion of the Municipality of Malitbog, Province of Southern Leyte, bounded on the north by Lopez Jaena Street, on the east by Rizal Avenue, on the south by Jose P. Burgos Street, and on the west by Marcelo H. del Pilar Street which was not created into a barrio under Republic Act Numbered Twenty-six hundred is hereby constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Taliwa.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
