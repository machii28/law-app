Republic Act No. 2138
April 7, 1959
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIOS OF MALAGUINING AND CANCORVENUS, BOTH IN THE MUNICIPALITY OP TALALORA, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, TO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO SAN FRANCISCO OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Malaguining and Cancorvenus, both in the Municipality of Talalora, Province of Samar, are hereby converted to a barrio to be known as Barrio San Francisco of the same municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
