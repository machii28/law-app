Republic Act No. 2788
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIARGAO IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR TO RAMON MAGSAYSAY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Liargao in the Province of Zamboanga del Sur is changed to Ramon Magsaysay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
