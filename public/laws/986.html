Republic Act No. 986
June 2, 1954
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF PAGSANJAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN PAGSANJAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO FRANCISCO BENITEZ MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the educational contribution and eminent services rendered to the country by the late Francisco Benitez, the name of Pagsanjan Elementary School in Pagsanjan, Province of Laguna, is hereby changed to Francisco Benitez Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 2, 1954.
