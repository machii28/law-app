Republic Act No. 2884
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (d), SUBPARAGRAPH 53, FOR THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, PAGE 113, PARAGRAPH a, TITLE B, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (d), subparagraph 53, for the Province of Zamboanga, del Norte, page 113, paragraph a, Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is hereby amended to read as follows:

"(d) Rizal Damasing-Nasipang Road
Construction .................60,000.00."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
