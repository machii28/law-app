Republic Act No. 9447
May 15, 2007
AN ACT RENAMING FLORIDA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SITUATED IN BARANGAY FLORIDA, BUTUAN CITY, INTO MARIANA L. PINEDA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of School. — Florida Elementary School, a public elementary school situated in Barangay Florida, Butuan City, is hereby renamed and henceforth shall be known as Mariana L. Pineda Memoraial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. Repealing Clause. — All laws, decrees, executive orders and other administrative issuances and part thereof which are inconsistent with the provision of this Act are hereby modified or suspended accordingly.

SEC. 3. Separability Clause. — If any clause, provision, paragraph or part hereof shall be declared unconstitutional or invalid, it shall not affect the remaining provisions of this Act.

SEC. 4. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,
