Republic Act No. 1334
June 16, 1955
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIOS OF CORAL, HAGO, TUVA, DIOJANGO, CANALAAN, HONAAN AND DIMNALAMAY, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF SAN JOAQUIN, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BASCO, PROVINCE OF BATANES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Coral, Hago, Tuva, Canalaan, Honaan and Dimnalamay are hereby converte into a barrio, to be known as barrio San Joaquin, in t Municipality of Basco, Province of Batanes.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
