Republic Act No. 3576
June 21, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE WELFAREVILLE SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANDALUYONG, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO JOSE FABELLA MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Welfareville School in the Municipality of Mandaluyong, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Jose Fabella Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1963.
