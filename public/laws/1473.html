Republic Act No. 1473
June 14, 1956
AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION TWO, ap. QUEZON, ITEM THIRTY-THREE, PAGE FIVE HUNDRED SEVENTY-EIGHT, OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section two, ap. Quezon, Item. thirty-three, page five hundred seventy-eight, of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred is amended to read as follows:

"33. Construction of additional building, Bignay Barrio School, Sariaya……….P6,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
