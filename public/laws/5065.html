Republic Act No. 5065
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CREATING FOUR ADDITIONAL POSITIONS OF ASSISTANT PROVINCIAL FISCAL IN THE PROVINCE OF SAMAR, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION SIXTEEN HUNDRED SEVENTY-FOUR OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The provision of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Samar, is further amended to read as follows:
"Samar, fourteen assistant provincial fiscals;"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
