Republic Act No. 7573
May 26, 1992
AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE STA. CRUZ HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The rural health unit in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Sta. Cruz Health Infirmary.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the Year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 26, 1992.
