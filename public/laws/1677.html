Republic Act No. 1677
June 20, 1957
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF DURADO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAKIL, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF DURADO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Durado in the Municipality of Pakil, Province of Laguna, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of Durado of said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
