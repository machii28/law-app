Republic Act No. 2224
May 17, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BAGONG POOK IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Pulo in the barrio of Tugtog, Municipality of San Jose, Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bagong Pook.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 17, 1959.
