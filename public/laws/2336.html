Republic Act No. 2336
June 19, 1959
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SUMMER RESORT IN SAMPALOC, MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a summer resort in Sampaloc, Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal. The Commission on Parks and Wildlife shall issue such rules and regulations for the establishment of such resort.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1959.
