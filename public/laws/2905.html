Republic Act No. 2905
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-THREE HUNDRED ONE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (q), page 377 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred one, is amended to read as follows:

" (q) Lipata Elementary School shop building, Minglanilla—
Construction .................P5,000.00."

SEC. 2. Item 15(a), page 528 of the same Act, is amended to read as follows:

"15. Carcar:
(a) Valencia public market............P15,000.00."

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
