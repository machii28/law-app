Republic Act No. 1931
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE NEW WASHINGTON HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NEW WASHINGTON, PROVINCE OP AKLAN, INTO A NATIONAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES TO BE KNOWN AS THE AKLAN NATIONAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES AND APPROPRIATING THE NECESSARY FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The New Washington High School in the Municipality of New Washington, Province of Aklan, is hereby converted into a national school of fisheries to be known as the Aldan National School of Fisheries. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize said school accordingly.

SEC. 2. The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the Aklan National School of Fisheries for the fiscal year nineteen hundred fifty-eight. Such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
