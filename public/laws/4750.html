Republic Act No. 4750
June 18, 1966
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF THREE MILLION PESOS AS SUBSIDY TO THE REHABILITATION OF THE PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE AND ITS MUNICIPALITIES WHICH WERE DEVASTATED BY TYPHOON LOUISE LAST NOVEMBER 19, 1964.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of three million pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, as a subsidy to the rehabilitation of the Province of Surigao del Norte and its municipalities which were devastated by Typhoon Louise last November 19, 1964, graduated as follows:
  1. For the Province of Surigao del Norte:

    1. For the fiscal year 1965-66
    		 P500,000
    2. For the fiscal year 1966-67
    		400,000
    3. For the fiscal year 1967-68
    		300,000
    4. For the fiscal year 1968-69
    		200,000
    5. For the fiscal year 1969-70
    		100,000
    P1,500,000

  2. For the different municipalities of Surigao del Norte:

    1. For the Municipality of Surigao:
    a. For the fiscal year 1965-66 P50,000
    b. For the fiscal year 1966-67 40,000
    c. For the fiscal year 1967-68 30,000
    d. For the fiscal year 1968-69 20,000
    e. For the fiscal year 1969-70 10,000
    P150,000
    2. For three fifth class municipalities:
    a. For the fiscal year 1965-66, P25,000 each P75,000
    b. For the fiscal year 1966-67, P20,000 each 60,000
    c. For the fiscal year 1967-68, P15,000 each 45,000
    d. For the fiscal year 1968-69, P10,000 each 30,000
    e. For the fiscal year 1969-70, P5.000 each 15,000
    P225,000
    3. For nine sixth-class municipalities:
    a. For the fiscal year 1965-66, P20,000 each P180,000
    b. For the fiscal year 1966-67, P16.000 each 144,000
    c. For the fiscal year 1967-68, P12,000 each 108,000
    d. For the fiscal year 1968-69, P8,000 each 72,000
    e. For the fiscal year 1969-70, P4,000 each 36,000
    P540.000
    4. For thirteen seventh-class municipalities:
    a. For the fiscal year 1965-66, P15,000 each P195,000
    b. For the fiscal year 1966-67, P12,000 each 156,000
    c. For the fiscal year 1967-68, P9,000 each 117,000
    d. For the fiscal year 1968-69, P6,000 each 78,000
    e. For the fiscal year 1969-70, P3,000 each 39,000
    P585,000
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
