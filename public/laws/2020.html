Republic Act No. 2020
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LOAKAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO TO MALVAR ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Loakan Elementary School in the City of Baguio is hereby changed to Malvar Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
