Republic Act No. 5311
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF CERTAIN SCHOOLS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGLE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Licu-an Elementary School in Municipality of Dingle, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to San Matias Elementary School.

SEC. 2. The name of Agtatacay Elementary School also in the Municipality of Dingle, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Agtatacay-Gutao Elementary School.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
