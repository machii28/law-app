Republic Act No. 3503
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TUBURAN NATIONAL VOCATIONAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBURAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU, TO TUBURAN NATIONAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Tuburan National Vocational School in the Municipality of Tuburan, Province of Cebu, is hereby changed to Tuburan National School. General courses as well as vocational courses shall be offered in said school.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
