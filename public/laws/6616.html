Republic Act No. 6616
October 23, 1972
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF AN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN BARRIO CALANTAC, MUNICIPALITY OF ALCALA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the Bureau of Medical Services, a twenty-five-bed emergency hospital in Barrio Calantac, Municipality of Alcala, Province of Cagayan, to be known as the Central Cagayan Emergency Hospital.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment and construction of said hospital as well as the purchase of equipment and facilities thereof. The annual maintenance of said hospital shall be in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-nine.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

GIL J. PUYAT CORNELIO T. VILLAREAL
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives
Finally passed by the Senate on September 11, 1972. This Act, which originated in the House of Representatives, was finally passed by the same on April 7, 1972.

ELISEO M. TENZA INOCENCIO B. PAREJA
