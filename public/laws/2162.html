Republic Act No. 2162
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The names of the following barrios in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Leyte, are changed as follows:
  1. Barrio of Santo Rosario to Barrio Catmon;
  2. Barrio La Purisima Concepcion to Barrio Agpangi;
  3. Barrio San Vicente to Barrio Lico;
  4. Barrio Santa Fe to Barrio Talostusan;
  5. Barrio San Isidro to Barrio Locso-on; and
  6. Barrio Sulphur Valley to Barrio Villa Caneja.
SEC. 2. Republic Act Numbered One thousand three hundred thirty-three is repealed.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
