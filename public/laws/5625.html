Republic Act No. 5625
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SUBA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF DANAO, TO SEVERO DUTERTE MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Suba Elementary School in the City of Danao, is hereby changed to Severo Duterte Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
