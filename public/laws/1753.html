Republic Act No. 1753
June 21, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE B OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED ELEVEN,—HIGHWAYS, FEEDER AND SECONDARY ROADS—MARINDUQUE, PROJECT B-028.17.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Project B-028.17, title B of Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"Project B-028.17—Mogpog-Hightown-Danao Road Improvement ..................... P20.000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
