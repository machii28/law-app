Republic Act No. 6755
August 11, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MALIBAGO BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BABATNGON, LEYTE, TO MALIBAGO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Malibago Barangay High School in Babatngon, Leyte, is hereby changed to Malibago National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved: August 11, 1989.
