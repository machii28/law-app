June 21, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS OF REPUBLIC ACTS NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED, FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN AND SIXTEEN HUNDRED THIRTEEN.

 P10,000"
SEC. 5. Item (3), page two hundred, eighty-eight of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:
"(3)-a Construction or improvement of San Joaquin-Calawaan road Pasig, Rizal ...........
 P10,000"
"(3)-b Asphalt paving of A. Mabini street, Marikina, Rizal ..........................................
