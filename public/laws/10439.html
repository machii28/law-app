Republic Act No. 10439
April 8, 2013
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CANUMAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CANUMAY, CITY OF VALENZUELA TO CANUMAY WEST NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of a National High School. – The name of Canumay National High School in Barangay Canumay, City of Valenzuela is hereby changed to Canumay West National High School.

SEC. 2. Location of the Main Campus. – The main campus of the Canumay West National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Canumay, City of Valenzuela.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
