Republic Act No. 8780
April 27, 2000
AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE CALINOG NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALINOG, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CALINOG NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established the Calinog National Comprehensive High School to be located in the Municipality of Calinog, Iloilo.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby directed to take such measures as are necessary to establish the Calinog National Comprehensive High School.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 27,2000.
