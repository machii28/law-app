Republic Act No. 2885
June 19, 1960
AN ACT REAPPROPRIATING THE SUM OF TWO THOUSAND PESOS APPROPRIATED UNDER ITEM (128), PAGE 708, SUBPARAGRAPH (ab), FOR THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE, PARAGRAPH b, TITLE A, SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Semite and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of two thousand pesos appropriated under item (128), page 708, subparagraph (ab), for the Province of Leyte, paragraph b, Title A, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, to wit:

"(128) Construction, Sitio Panyi Templanza
Barrio School, Matalom........................... P2, 000.00"

is hereby re-appropriated for the construction of a school building in the barrio of San Juan, Municipality of Matalom, Province of Leyte.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
