Republic Act No. 5110
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LA CASTELLANA NORTH ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA CASTELLANA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO DON FELIX ROBLES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House, of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of La Castellana North Elementary School in the Municipality of La Castellana, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Don Felix Robles Elementary School in honor of Don Felix Robles, donor of the townsite of said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
