Republic Act No. 4002
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO TILTILAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TINAMBAC, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO SAN JOSE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Tiltilan in the Municipality of Tinambac, Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby changed to San Jose.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
