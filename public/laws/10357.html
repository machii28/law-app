Republic Act No. 10357
January 9, 2013
AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 5 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF NAGA PROVINCE OF CEBU TO BE KNOWN AS THE “CHARTER DAY OF THE CITY OF NAGA”
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 5 of every year is hereby declared to be a special working public holiday in the City of Naga, Province of Cebu to commemorate its founding day and to be known as the “Charter Day of the City of Naga”.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
