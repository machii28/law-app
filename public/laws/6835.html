Republic Act No. 6835
January 5, 1990
AN ACT TO NAME THE ROAD FROM CANAYAON JUNCTION TO ROXAS PARK IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GARCIA HERNANDEZ, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, AS THE HONORABLE CRUZ P. RANARIO AVENUE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The road from Canayaon Junction to Roxas Park in the Municipality of Garcia Hernandez, Province of Bohol, is hereby named as the Honorable Cruz P. Ranario Avenue.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 6, 1988 and September 28, 1989, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDWIN P. ACOBA (Sgd.) QUIRINO D. ABAD SANTOS, JR.
