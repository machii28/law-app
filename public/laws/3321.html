Republic Act No. 3321
June 18, 1961
AN ACT FIXING THE BOUNDARIES OF THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF CARASI, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The area comprising the Municipal District of Carasi, Province of Ilocos Norte, shall be bounded as follows:

Beginning at Corner 1, which is N 60° E, 6,000 meters from B.L.L.M. No. 1 of the Municipality of Solsona, Province of Ilocos Norte; thence N 20° W, 8,000 meters to Corner 2; thence N 75° E, 13,850 meters to Corner 3; thence S 42° E, 3,500 meters to Corner 4; thence S 67° E, 2,300 meters to Corner 5; thence S 70° W, 2,050 meters to Corner 6; thence S 80° W, 2,000 meters to Corner 7; thence S 74° W, 6,150 meters to Corner 8; thence S 46° W, 6,750 meters to the point of beginning, containing an area of 8,296.7 hectares, more or less.


SEC. 2. The sum of ten thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the boundary survey thereof.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
