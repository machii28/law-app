Republic Act No. 1681
June 20, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (37), on page 18, paragraph 2a, title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(37) Negros Occidental Provincial Hospital, Bacolod A-000.2a (76)

(a) Construction of Kitchen for Contagious Diseases Pavilion
P15,000.00
(b) Construction of Laboratory Building
15,000.00"

SEC. 2. The following item is hereby inserted on page 22, after item (62) (d), paragraph 2a, title A, section one of same Act:

"(63) Negros Occidental Emergency Hospital Himamaylan A-000.2a (80) Construction
P25,000.00"

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
