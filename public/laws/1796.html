Republic Act No. 1796
June 21, 1957
AN ACT PROVIDING THAT THE SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBIGON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, AT PRESENT KNOWN AS PANADTARAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, SHALL HEREAFTER BE KNOWN AS MAYOR AVELINO CHAGAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The school in the Municipality of Tubigon Province of Bohol, at present known as Panadtaran Elementary School, shall hereafter be known as Mayor Avelino Chagas Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
