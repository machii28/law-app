Republic Act No. 5678
June 21, 1969
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM 34, PAGE 95, SUBPARAGRAPH 24, FOR THE PROVINCE OF ISABELA, PARAGRAPH "A", TITLE B, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTY-ONE HUNDRED ONE (RE PUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS FOR THE PROVINCE OF ISABELA).
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 34, page 95, subparagraph 24, for the Province of Isabela, paragraph "a", Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-one hundred one is hereby amended to read as follows:

"34. Masigun Bridge, (Masigun Creek), Burgos cons­truction 40,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
