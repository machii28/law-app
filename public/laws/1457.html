Republic Act No. 1457
June 14, 1956
AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED ELEVEN TO PROVIDE FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF A SHOP BUILDING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF QUEZON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item fifty-one, roman numbered two, subsection aq. Quezon, section three of Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred eleven, is hereby amended to read as follows:
"51. Quezon Central School Shop Building—Construction ........................ P3,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1956.
