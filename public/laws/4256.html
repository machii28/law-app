Republic Act No. 4256
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF NEW CANIPO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Capalpalan in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, is hereby converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as Barrio New Canipo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
