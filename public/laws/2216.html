Republic Act No. 2216
May 16, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF TAMBON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALICIA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, TO BARRIO TANDIONG MUSLIM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Tambon in the Municipality of Alicia, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is changed to barrio Tandiong Muslim.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 16, 1959.
