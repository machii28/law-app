Republic Act No. 11065
August 23, 2018
AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) LICENSING CENTER IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT , PROVINCE OF CAVITE INTO A REGULAR LICENSING CENTER TO BE KNOWN AS THE LTO CAVITE LICENSING CENTER OF KAWIT, CAVITE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Land Transportation Office (LTO) Licensing Center in the Municipality of Kawit, Cavite is hereby converted into a Regular Licensing Center to be known as the LTO Cavite Licensing Center.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately include in the Department's programs of the operationalization of the LTO Cavite Licensing Center, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year's appropriations of the LTO Cavite Licensing Center. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the continued implementation of this Act shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in two (2) national newspapers of general circulation.
