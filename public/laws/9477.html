Republic Act No. 9477
May 22, 2007
AN ACT RENAMING THE GAPAN — SAN FERNANDO — OLONGAPO ROAD (GSO ROAD) AS JOSE ABAD SANTOS AVENUE (JASA)
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Gapan — San Fernando — Olongapo Road (GSO Road) is hereby renamed as Jose Abad Santos Avenue (JASA).

SEC. 2. All laws, decrees, orders and issuances or parts thereof inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate


This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 29, 2005 and February 19, 2007, respectively.
