Republic Act No. 2805
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BINUANGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALICIA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, TO BELLA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress asssembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Binuañgan in the Municipality of Alicia, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is changed to Bella.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
