Republic Act No. 2133
April 7, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF CUBAY SUR IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUGASONG, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Cubay Sur in the Municipality Bugasong, Province of Antique, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Cubay Sur.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
