Republic Act No. 5122
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PALONGPONG SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BENIGNO MACADAEG MEMORIAL COMMUNITY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Palongpong School in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Benigno Macadaeg Memorial Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
