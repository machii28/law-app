Republic Act No. 1424
June 12, 1956
AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING SECTION SEVEN OF COMMONWEALTH ACT NUMBERED THREE HUNDRED TWENTY-SIX, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS "THE CHARTER OF THE CITY OF BACOLOD."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Paragraph one of section seven of Commonwealth Act Numbered Three hundred twenty-six, as amended by Republic Act Numbered Three hundred, is further amended to read as follows:
"SEC. 7. Relations between the City of Bacolod and the Provincial Government of Negros Occidental.—For election purposes, the City of Bacolod unless otherwise provided shall continue as part of the Second Congressional District of the Province of Negros Occidental. The voters of the City of Bacolod shall take part in the election of provincial officers of Negros Occidental, but the latter shall have no jurisdiction over the City of Bacolod and the officers of the same."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1956.
