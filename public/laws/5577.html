Republic Act No. 5577
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE N^ME OF BARRIO DALAGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GLORIA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO BARRIO SAN ANTONIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Dalagan in the Municipality of Gloria, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Barrio San Antonio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
