Republic Act No. 3138
June 17, 1961
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF AN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTIAGO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SOUTHERN ISABELA EMERGENCY HOSPITAL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Director of Medical Services, an emergency hospital in the Municipality of Santiago, Province of Isabela, to be known as the Southern Isabela Emergency Hospital. The site for the said hospital shall be provided by the Municipal Council of the Municipality of Santiago, in consultation with the Director of Medical Services.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Health shall promulgate such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry into effect the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The sum of ninety-one thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said hospital.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1961.
