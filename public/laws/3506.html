Republic Act No. 3506
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LA LOMA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN QUEZON CITY TO MAGSAYSAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the La Loma Elementary School in Quezon City is changed to Magsaysay Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
