Republic Act No. 9475
May 22, 2007
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LASAM NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LASAM, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN TO LASAM INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of School. — The name of Lasam National Agricultural School in the Municipality of Lasam, Province of Cagayan is hereby changed to Lasam Institute of Technology.

SEC. 2. Repealing Clause. — All laws, presidential decrees, executive orders, rules and regulations contrary to or inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect upon its approval and after its publication in at least two newspapers of general circulation.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
