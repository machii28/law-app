Republic Act No. 5074
June 17, 1967
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF TWO HUNDRED NINETY-SIX THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED TWENTY-ONE PESOS FOR THE OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF CAGAYAN PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of two hundred ninety-six thousand seven hundred twenty-one pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of Cagayan Provincial Hospital in the Municipality of Tuguegarao, Province of Cagayan. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
