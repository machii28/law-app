Republic Act No. 2536
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF FRATERNIDAD STREET, DISTRICT OF PANDACAN, CITY OF MANILA, TO TEODORA SAN LUIS STREET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Fraternidad Street in the District of Pandacan, City of Manila, is changed to Teodora San Luis Street.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
