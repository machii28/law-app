Republic Act No. 6154
November 9, 1970
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TAGBILARAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO DOÑA REGINA, MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA MARIA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO DOÑA REGINA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Tagbi1aran Elementary School in Barrio Doña Regina, Municipality of Santa Maria, Province of Davao del Sur, is hereby changed to Doña Regina Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, November 9, 1970.
