Republic Act No. 695
May 9, 1952
AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE APPROPRIATION OF TEN THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE INTENSIVE PRODUCTION OF COTTON SEEDS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of ten thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, which shall be at the disposal of the Director of Plant Industry for the intensive production of cotton seeds.

A report of the work accomplished under this Act should be made to both Houses of the Congress of the Philippines.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
