Republic Act No. 1640
June 20, 1957
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF CANAWAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALILIPOT, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, INTO A BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION. 1. The sitio of Canaway in the Municipality of Malilipot, Province of Albay, is hereby converted into a barrio of the said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
