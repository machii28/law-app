Republic Act No. 3310
June 18, 1961
AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-SEVEN HUNDRED ONE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 20, page 119, subparagraph 54, for the Province of Tarlac, paragraph c, Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred one, is hereby amended to read as follows:
"20. San Felipe-Mañgandingay Road, San Manuel, Tarlac
P5,000.00"

SEC. 2. Item (db), page 663, Group I, subparagraph 53, for the Province of Tarlac, paragraph c, Section two of the same Act, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"(db) Camposanto 2nd Barrio School, Moncada, Tarlac Construction, repair and/or improvement
P5,000.00"

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
