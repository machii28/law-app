Republic Act No. 7262
March 20, 1992
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE ORMOC DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN ORMOC CITY, FROM ONE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE (125) TO ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Ormoc District Hospital in Ormoc City is hereby increased from one hundred twenty-five (125) to one hundred fifty (150) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be charged against the lump-sum appropriations authorized for the purpose under the current General Appropriations Act. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 16, 1992 and December 17, 1991, respectively.
