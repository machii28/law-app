Republic Act No. 10559
May 15, 2013
AN ACT NAMING THE PALAWAN POLICE PROVINCIAL OFFICE (PALAWAN PPO) TO CAMP HIGINO ACOSTA MENDOZA SR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Palawan Police Provincial Office (Palawan PPO) in the City of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan is hereby named as Camp Higino Acosta Mendoza Sr.

SEC. 2. The Philippine National Police shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in a national newspaper of general circulation.
