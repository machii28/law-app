Republic Act No. 5130
June 17, 1967
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR TWO ADDITIONAL ASSISTANT PROVINCIAL FISCALS FOR THE PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION SIXTEEN HUNDRED SEVENTY-FOUR OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE, AS AMENDED.


Be it enacted by the Semite and, House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The provision of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Surigao del Sur, is further amended to read as follows:
"Surigao del Sur, four assistant provincial fiscals."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
