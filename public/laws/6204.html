Republic Act No. 6204
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ALAG COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN BARRIO ALAG, MUNICIPALITY OF BACO, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO FELIX HERNANDEZ COMMUNITY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Alag Community School in Barrio Alag, Municipality of Baco, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Felix Hernandez Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
