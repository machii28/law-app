Republic Act No. 1729
June 21, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND A CERTAIN ITEM IN SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item one, Group V, under q. Cebu, page three hundred seventy-four, section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, is amended to read as follows:

"1. Tumalog Barrio School Building, Oslob Construction
P5,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
