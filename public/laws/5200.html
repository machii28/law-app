Republic Act No. 5200
April 27, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LIPAY ELE­MENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOLSONA, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO NALIPSAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Semite and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Lipay Elementary School in the Municipality of Solsona, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby change to Nalipsan Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 27, 1968.
