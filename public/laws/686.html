Republic Act No. 686
May 9, 1952
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF "AGBOBOLO" IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO "MANOLING".
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of "Agbobolo" in the Municipality of President Roxas, Province of Capiz, shall hereafter be known as "Manoling."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
