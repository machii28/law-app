Republic Act No. 2918
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BURGOS, PROVINCE OF ILOCCS SUR, TO THE BARRIO OF LUNA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The seat of government of the Municipality of Burgos, Province of IIocos Sur, is transferred from its present site to the barrio of Luna.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
