Republic Act No. 221
June 5, 1948
AN ACT TO TRANSFER THE BARRIO OF TUBURAN-SOLBUD FROM THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA BARBARA TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF ZARRAGA, BOTH OF THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of Tuburan-Solbud, presently under the jurisdiction of the municipality of Santa Barbara, is hereby transferred to the municipality of Zarraga, Province of Iloilo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 5, 1948.
