Republic Act No. 3285
June 18, 1961
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM FIFTY-ONE, PAGE SEVEN HUNDRED EIGHTY-SIX, SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item fifty-one, page seven hundred eighty-six, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, is hereby amended to read as follows:

"51. Construction and/or repair of Home Economics building, Lalig Barrio School, Tiaong P 5,000.00."


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
