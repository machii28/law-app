Republic Act No. 5606
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CAPICHO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN ROXAS CITY TO GOVERNOR RAFAEL ACUNA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Capicho Elementary School in Roxas City is hereby changed to Governor Rafael Acuna Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
