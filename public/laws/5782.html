Republic Act No. 5782
June 21, 1969
AN ACT NAMING THE PUBLIC HIGHWAY FROM THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANZA TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAIC IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE ANTEKO SORIANO AVENUE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The public highway from the Municipality of Tanza to the Municipality of Naic in the Province of Cavite is hereby named Antero Soriano Avenue.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
