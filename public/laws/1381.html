Republic Act No. 1381
June 18, 1955
AN ACT CONVERTING THE ODIOÑGAN HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ODIOÑGAN, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON, INTO A NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL WHICH SHALL BE KNOWN AS THE ODIOÑGAN NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Odioñgan High School in the Municipality of Odioñgan, Province of Romblon, is hereby converted into a national agricultural school which shall be known as the Odioñgan National Agricultural School. The said shall be under the supervision of the Bureau of public Schools.

SEC. 2. The sum of three hundred thousand pesos is authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the expenses of the said school during the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, including the purchase or expropriation of additional land for the use of the said school, and of equipment and supplies. Thereafter, the necessary sum for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1955.
