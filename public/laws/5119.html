Republic Act No. 5119
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO BANTUG COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN BARRIO BANTUG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF UMINGAN. PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO DOÑA S. BELISARIO-ADAMOS COMMUNITY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Bantug Community School in Barrio Bantug in the Municipality of Umingan, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby changed to Doña S. Belisario-Adamos Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
