Republic Act No. 10955
October 30, 2017
AN ACT DIVIDING BARANGAY DUMALNEG IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE INTO THREE (3) DISTINCT AND INDEPENDENT BARANGAYS TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY KALAW, BARANGAY CABARITAN AND BARANGAY QUIBEL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Division of Barangay Dumalneg. Barangay Dumalneg in the Province of IIocos Norte is hereby divided into three (3) distinct and independent barangays to be known as Barangay Kalaw, Barangay Cabaritan and Barangay Quibel.

SEC. 2. Composition. - The existing puroks and sitios shall comprise the new barangays herein created as follows:

(a) Barangay Kalaw.- Barangay Kalaw shall be composed of Purok Crisalita, Purok Saniata., Sitio Pacac, Sitio Banbanag, Sitio Sta. Maria, Sitio Siraong, Sitio As-asaan, Sitio Nasduan, Sitio Nappud, Sitio Nanguratao and Sitio Sisilungen.

(b) Barangay Cabaritan. - Barangay Cabaritan shall be composed of Purok Timpuyog, Poblacion, Sitio Alilem, Sitio Cabaritan, Sitio Capcapitan, Sitio Tapauan, Sitio Immalsung and Sitio Mangadamay.

(c) Barangay Quibel. - Barangay Quibel shall be composed, of Purok Namnama, Purok Danggayan, Purok Regta, Sitio Namoc, Sitio Arapang, Sitio Nalames, Sitio Baracbac, Sitio Bobog and Sitio Baset.

SEC. 4. Seat of the Municipal Government.- The seat of the municipal government shall be at Poblacion, Barangay Cabaritan.

SEC. 5. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite.- The Commission on Elections (COMELEC) shall conduct and supervise the plebiscite to be held in the entire Municipality of Dumalneg, Province of Ilocos Norte not later than one hundred twenty (120) days from the effectivity of this Act.

SEC. 6. Barangay Officials.- The first set of officials of the barangays shal be appointed by the provincial governor, in consultation with the Representative of the District, and shall hold office until such time that their successors have been duly elected and qualified.

SEC. 7. Public Infrastructure and Facilities.- All public infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new barangays.

SEC. 8. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares.- The Municipality of Dumalneg and all the barangays herein created shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the "Local Government Code of 1991"

SEC. 9. Effectivity.- This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
