Republic Act No. 1026
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF CASINSIN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAKIL, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO A BARRIO TO BK KNOWN AS BARRIO CASINSIN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Casinsin in the municipality of Pakil, Province of Laguna, is hereby converted to a barrio to be known as barrio Casinsin.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
