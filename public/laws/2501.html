Republic Act No. 2501
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF THE CAPARAN PRIMARY SCHOOL AND DALAÑGIN PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, TO TIAYON PRIMARY SCHOOL AND DAROL MOSLEM PRIMARY SCHOOL, RESPECTIVELY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The names of Caparan Primary School and Dalañgin Primary School, both in the Municipality of Ipil, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, are changed to Tiayon Primary School and Darol Moslem Primary School, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
