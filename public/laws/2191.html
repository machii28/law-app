Republic Act No. 2191
May 7, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIOS OF LUYA, STA. ANA AND BAGONG SILANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, INTO BARRIOS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Luya, Sta. Ana and Bagong Silang in the Municipality of Calatagan, Province of Batangas, are hereby converted into barrios to be known as the barrios of Luya, Sta. Ana and Bagong Silang, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
