Republic Act No. 5396
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MASBATE SOUTH ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, TO JOSE ZURBITO, SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL. AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MASBATE SOUTH ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, TO JOSE ZURBITO, SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Masbate South Elementary School in the Municipality of Masbate, Province of Masbate is hereby changed to Jose Zurbito, Sr. Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
