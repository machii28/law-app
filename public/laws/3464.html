Republic Act No. 3464
June 16, 1962
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS TO BE DISBURSED BY THE SECRETARY OF LABOR FOR THE EXPENSES OF THE PHILIPPINE DELEGATION TO THE INTERNATIONAL LABOUR CONFERENCE IN GENEVA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any sum in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, in addition to the sum of eighty-four thousand seven hundred pesos already appropriated under Republic Act Numbered Thirty-one hundred, to be disbursed by the Secretary of Labor for the expenses of the Philippine Delegation to the International Labour Conference in Geneva.

SEC. 2. The amount appropriated herein shall be carried in the annual budget of the Department of Labor beginning the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-two-nine-teen hundred sixty-three as an addition to the sum of eighty-four thousand seven hundred pesos appropriated by Republic Act Numbered Thirty-one hundred.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
