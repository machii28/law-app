Republic Act No. 3727
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE NORTHERN NEGROS EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN BALINTAWAK, ESCALANTE, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO CONGRESSMAN VICENTE F. GUSTILO MEMORIAL HOSPITAL


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Northern Negros Emergency Hospital in Balintawak Escalante, Negros Occidental, is changed to Congressman Vicente F. Gustilo Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
