Republic Act No. 5123
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BALIAN BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANGIL, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO VICTOR OTEYZA DE GUIA, JR. MEMORIAL BARRIO SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Balian Barrio School in the Municipality of Pangil, Province of Laguna, is hereby changed to Victor Oteyza de Guia, Jr. Memorial Barrio School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
