Republic Act No. 7501
May 18, 1992
AN ACT RENAMING THE MEDINA-BANGAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGRAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO MEDINA-PARADO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Medina-Bangay Elementary School located in the Municipality of Dingras, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby renamed as the Medina-Parado Elementary School.

SEC. 2. All allocations provided for the operation and maintenance of the Medina-Bangay Elementary School shall be implemented to the Medina-Parado Elementary School.

SEC. 3. The complete organizational staff of the Medina-Bangay Elementary School is deemed adopted by the Medina-Parado Elementary School.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 23, 1991 and February 6, 1992, respectively.
