Republic Act No. 5028
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CENTRAL SCHOOL ANNEX IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF OROQUIETA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL, TO LAYAWAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Central School Annex in the Municipality of Oroquieta, Province of Misamis Occidental, is hereby changed to and shall hereafter be known as Layawan Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
