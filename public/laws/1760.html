Republic Act No. 1760
June 21, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING ITEM EIGHTEEN, UNDER "S. DAVAO," SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate mid House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item eighteen, under "S. Davao," section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:
"18. Blocon Primary School Building, Bansalan, repair and improvement ...... P1,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
