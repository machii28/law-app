Republic Act No. 5373
June 15, 1968
AN ACT APPROPRIATING FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE EXPENSES OF THE CONFERENCE OF THE ASIAN PARLIAMENTARIANS’ UNION TO BE HELD IN THE CITY OF MANILA IN NINETEEN HUNDRED SIXTY-EIGHT.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of five hundred thousand pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the expenses of the conference of the Asians Parliamentarians’ Union which will be held in the City of Manila, in nineteen hundred sixty-eight.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1968.
