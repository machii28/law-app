Republic Act No. 1107
June 15, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUAYAN, IN THE PROVINCE OF COTABATO, TO GENERAL SANTOS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the municipality of Buayan, in the Province of Cotabato, is changed to General Santos.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1954.
