Republic Act No. 6090
August 4, 1969
AN ACT FIXING THE MAXIMUM COMPENSATION OF MUNICIPAL VICE-MAYORS AND COUNCILORS.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In lieu of per diems, the municipal council may grant to each Vice-Mayor and councilor an annual salary not to exceed the following:

Third Class

2,400.00


SEC. 2. Municipalities of the fourth, fifth, sixth and seventh classes shall not be covered by the provisions of this Act: Provided, however, That the Municipal Councils of the municipalities belonging to these classes may grant to each Vice-Mayor and Councilor a per diem not exceeding fifteen pesos for every day of regular or special session of the Council actually attended by them: Provided, finally, That no per diem may be granted for more than two special sessions a month.

SEC. 3. All Acts, executive orders and administrative orders, or parts thereof, which are inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, August 4, 1969.
