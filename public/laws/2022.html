Republic Act No. 2022
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BALABAC, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, INTO A REGULAR MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipal District of Balabac, Province of Palawan, is converted into a regular municipality of said province, to be known as the Municipality of Balabac.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
