Republic Act No. 1693
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BAGONG POOK IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LEMERY, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Bagong Pook is separated from the barrio of Arumahan, Municipality of Lemery, Province of Batangas, and constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bagong Pook.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
