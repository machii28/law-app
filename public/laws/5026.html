Republic Act No. 5026
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SALANGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, INTO MABABANG PAARALANG PANGALAALA KAY DR. JUAN PASCUAL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Salangan Elementary School in Barrio Salangan, Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed into Mababang Paaralang Pang-alaala kay Dr. Juan Pascual.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
