Republic Act No. 2537
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF CALANSAYAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATANGAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO BARRIO CONCEPCION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Calansayan in the Municipality of Batangas, Province of Batangas, is changed to Barrio Concepcion.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
