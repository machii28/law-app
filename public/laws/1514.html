Republic Act No. 1514
June 16, 1956
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BAYAN-BAYANAN, MUNICIPALITY OF MARIKINA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO CONCEPCION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Bayan-Bayanan, Municipality of Marikina, Province of Rizal, is changed to Concepcion.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
