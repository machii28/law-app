Republic Act No. 2196
May 7, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO KIUG-ANG, MUNICIPALITY OF JASAAN, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, TO LUZ.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Kiug-ang in the Municipality of Jasaan, Province of Misamis Oriental, is changed to Luz, to wit: Barrio Luz, Municipality of Jasaan, province of Misamis Oriental.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
