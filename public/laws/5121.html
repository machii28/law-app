Republic Act No. 5121
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF NINTH AVENUE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, MARIA CLARA STREET, CALOOCAN CITY, TO MARIA CLARA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Ninth Avenue Elementary School, Maria Clara Street, Caloocan City, is hereby changed to Maria Clara Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
