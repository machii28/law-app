Republic Act No. 5570
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO BULBUGAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GLORIA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL M1ND0R0, TO BARRIO STA. MARIA.
Be it enacted by the Senate) and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Bulbugan in the Municipality of Gloria, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is here­by changed to Barrio Sta. Maria.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
