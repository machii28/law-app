Republic Act No. 2898
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF LACAREN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUYO, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Lacaren in the Municipality of Cuyo, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Lacaren.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
