Republic Act No. 2152
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MALIDONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GAMAY, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Candayong, Malidong and Malaspagas in the Municipality of Gamay, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Malidong.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959
