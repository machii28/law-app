Republic Act No. 2508
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SANTO DOMINGO HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTO DOMINGO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, TO JULIA ORTIZ LUIS HIGH SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Santo Domingo High School in the Municipality of Santo Domingo, Province of Nueva Ecija, is changed to Julia Ortiz Luis High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
