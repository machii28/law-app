Republic Act No. 2722
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (73), PAGE 737, SUB-PARAGRAPH aj, FOR MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, PARAGRAPH b, TITLE A, SECTION THREE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (73), page 737, subparagraph aj, for Mountain Province, paragraph b, Title A, Section three of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(73) Construction and completion of 3 rooms, Mompolia Barrio School, Burnay
P11,000.00."


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
