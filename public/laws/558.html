Republic Act No. 558
June 17, 1950
AN ACT TO TRANSFER A CERTAIN BARRIO FROM THE TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAD, PROVINCE OF ILOILO TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALASAN, SAME PROVINCE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of Zarragoza, in the municipality of Batad, Province of Iloilo, is transferred from said municipality to that of the municipality of Balasan, Province of Iloilo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1950.
