Republic Act No. 5024
June 17, 1967
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE IMPROVENMENT OF ARGAO BREEDING STATION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ARGAO, PROVINCE OF CEBU.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the improvement of Argao Breeding Station in the Municipality of Argao, Province of Cebu.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
