Republic Act No. 1419
June 10, 1956
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF WATU, PROVINCE OF LANAO, TO MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BALINDONG.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipal District of Watu Province of Lanao, is changed to Municipal District of Balindong.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1956.
