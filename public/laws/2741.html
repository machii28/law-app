Republic Act No. 2741
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEMS 50 AND 51, FOR THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, PAGE 230, PARAGRAPH g, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Items 50 and 51, for the Province of Pangasinan, page 230, paragraph g, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven are amended to read as follows:

"50. Polong Barrio School Building, Malasiqui P4,500.00
"51. Apaya Barrio School Building, Malasiqui 4,500.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
