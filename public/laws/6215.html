Republic Act No. 6215
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GOVERNOR FORBES STREET IN THE THIRD DISTRICT OF MANILA TO MAYOR ARSENIO H. LACSON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Governor Forbes Street in the Third District of Manila is hereby changed to Mayor Arsenio H. Lacson.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
