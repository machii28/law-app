Republic Act No. 3371
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS CABATUAN AND ATLAS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO TALISAY.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sitios Cabatuan and Atlas in the Municipality of Bontoc, Province of Leyte, are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Talisay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
