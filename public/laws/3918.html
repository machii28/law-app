Republic Act No. 3918
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SEIT CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANAMAO, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO PANGLIMA AGGA CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL,


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Seit Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Panamao, Province of Sulu, is changed to Panglima Agga Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
