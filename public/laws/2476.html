Republic Act No. 2476
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF MANLUNGAY OF THE BARRIO OF CANAAN, MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, INTO A BARRIO OF THE SAID MUNICIPALITY TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO VILLA PARAISO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Manlungay of the barrio of Canaan, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Nueva Ecija, is separated from said barrio of Canaan and constitute into a barrio of the, said municipality to be known as Barrio Villa Paraiso.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
