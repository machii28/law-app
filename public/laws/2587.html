Republic Act No. 2587
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAMBAYONG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, TO SULTAN SA BARONGIS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Lambayong in the Province of Cotabato is changed to Sultan Sa Barongis.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
