Republic Act No. 1632
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO ZIMI-GUI-ZIUANAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO MASI.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Zimigui-Ziuanan of Municipality of Pamplona, Province of Cagayan, is hereby changed to Masi.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
