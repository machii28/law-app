Republic Act No. 2503
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN MUNICIPALITY OF ABORLAN, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Tinagong-dagat, Apoaporawan and Malatgao in the Municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Tinagong-dagat, Sandoval and Maiatgao, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
