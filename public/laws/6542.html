SEC. 2. Other existing positions in the Veterans Memorial Hospital not mentioned in Section One of this Act, except casual and emergency positions shall be adjusted to the same range as the other positions of equal WAPCO range as of June 30, 1970, have been adjusted under this Act.

SEC. 3. The incumbents of the positions at the time of approval of this Act, whose salaries are herein standardized shall be automatically be entitled to the benefits hereof without the need of another appointment.

SEC. 4. In the implementation of the salary adjustment under this Act, the salary of an employee shall be place in the same step it had in its range as of June 30, 1970.

SEC. 5. All funds needed for the implementation of the salary adjustments provided for in this Act shall be taken from the savings of Veterans Memorial Hospital appropriation. Thereafter, the sums shall be included in the annual general appropriation Acts.

SEC. 6. Republic Act Numbered Five thousand two hundred five, as amended by Republic Act Six thousand one hundred thirty-three is hereby repealed.

SEC. 7. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, August 26, 1972.
