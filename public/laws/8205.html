Republic Act No. 8205
September 5, 1996
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SIASI SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JOLO, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO JOLO SCHOOL OF FISHERIES
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Siasi School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Jolo, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed to Jolo School of Fisheries.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, Lapsed into law on September 5, 1996, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
