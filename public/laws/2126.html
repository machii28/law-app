Republic Act No. 2126
April 7, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TALLAG BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABAGAN, PROVINCE OF 1SABELA, TO BIUAG COMMUNITY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives} of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Tallag Barrio School in the Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to Biuag Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
