Republic Act No. 2120
April 5, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN ISIDRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of San Isidro in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Marinduque, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Isidro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 5, 1959.
