Republic Act No. 3383
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN MIGUEL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOBO, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Viga in the barrio of Tayuman, Municipality of Lobo, Province of Batangas, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of San Miguel.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
