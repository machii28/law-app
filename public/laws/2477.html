Republic Act No. 2477
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN ISIDR0 IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALOLBON, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of San Isidro in the Municipality of Calolbon, Province of Catanduanes, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Isidro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
