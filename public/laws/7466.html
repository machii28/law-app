Republic Act No. 7466
April 28, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN ROQUE BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN TANAUAN, LEYTE, TO TANAUAN HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the San Roque Barangay High School in Tanauan, Leyte, is hereby changed to Tanauan High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 29, 1992 and January 27, 1992, respectively.
