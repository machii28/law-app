Republic Act No. 8830
August 22, 2000
AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXISTING BAYANIHAN HIGH SCHOOL IN SITIO SICUD, BARANGAY CANDAWAGA, MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE CANDAWAGA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Bayanihan High School in Sitio Sicud, Barangay Candawaga, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Palawan, is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Candawaga National High School.

SEC. 2. All personnel, records, assets and properties including buildings, sites and improvements thereof, obligations, funds and appropriations of the Sicud Bayanihan High School are hereby transferred to the Candawaga National High School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to carry out the provisions of this Act.

SEC. 4. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
