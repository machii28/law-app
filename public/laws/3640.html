Republic Act No. 3640
June 22, 1963
AN ACT AMENDING REPUBLIC ACTS NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED, TWENTY-THREE HUNDRED ONE, TWENTY-SEVEN HUNDRED ONE, AND THIRTY-ONE HUNDRED ONE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION. 1. Item (34), page 5, paragraph a, Title A, Section one of Republic, Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, is amended to read as follows:

"(34) Jose Rizal National Shrine,
Calamba, Laguna P50,000.00"


SEC. 2. Item (36), page 3, paragraph a, Title A, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred one, Item (ap), page 406, Group I, subparagraph 26, for the Province of Laguna, paragraph d, Section two of the same Act, are amended, to read as follows:

"(36) Jose Rizal National Shrine,
Calamba, Laguna P10,000.00


"(ap) Jose Rizal National Shrine,
Calamba, Improvement P5,000.00"


SEC. 3. Item (cf), page 433, Group I, .subparagraph 26, for the Province of Laguna, paragraph c, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred one, is amended to read as follows:

"(cf) Jose Rizal Shrine, Calamba,
Improvement _ P10,000.00"


SEC. 4. Item 48, page 6, paragraph a, Title A, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-one hundred one, is amended to read as follows:

"48. Dr. Jose Rizial National Shrine,
Calamba, Laguna P200,000.00"


SEC. 5. The President of the Philippines is hereby authorized to determine and fix the site of the Jose Rizal National Shrine.

SEC. 6. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
