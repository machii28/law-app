Republic Act No. 3639
June 22, 1963
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF EMERGENCY HOSPITALS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGELES AND IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MACABEBE, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the direct supervision of the Department of Health, one emergency hospital in each of the municipalities of Angeles and Macabebe in the Province of Pampanga.

SEC. 2. The emergency hospitals established under this Act shall be financed in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred and thirty-nine.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
