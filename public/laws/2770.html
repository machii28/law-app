Republic Act No. 2770
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIOS OF SANTA CLARA AND CUNIG IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF GONZAGA AND GATTARAN, RESPECTIVELY, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Maguing, Lapag, Mamanghag, Calanutan, Caronuan, Malabbeg, Hudong, Ugued, Annokugen and Marapang in the Municipality of Gonzaga, Province of Cagayan, are hereby constituted into a barrio in the same municipality to be known as the barrio of Santa Clara.

SEC. 2. The barrio of Madarag and the sitio of Cunig in the Municipality of Gattaran, same province, are hereby constituted into a barrio in the same municipality to be known as the barrio of Cunig.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
