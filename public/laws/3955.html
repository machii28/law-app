Republic Act No. 3955
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CALABAGAS COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF CALABAGAS, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN VICENTE, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO ORENDAIN COMMUNITY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Calabagas Community School in the barrio of Calabagas, Municipality of San Vicente, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Orendain Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
