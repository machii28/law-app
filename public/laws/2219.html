Republic Act No. 2219
May 16, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BABOY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALIBON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BARRIO SAN AGUSTIN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Baboy in the Municipality of Talibon, Province of Bohol, is changed to Barrio San Agustin.

SEC. 2. This Act shall Lake effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 16, 1959.
