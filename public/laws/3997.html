Republic Act No. 3997
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANNA IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO ESPIRITU.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Banna in the province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Espiritu.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval

Approved, June 18, 1964.
