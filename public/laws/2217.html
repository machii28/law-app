Republic Act No. 2217
May 7, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN ISIDRO COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALITBOG, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO OPPUS MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the San Isidro Community School in the Municipality of Malitbog, Province of Leyte, is changed to Oppus Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
