Republic Act No. 3377
June 18, 1961
AN ACT DECLARING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUPI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, AS DULY CONSTITUTED BARRIOS THEREOF.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrios of San Vicente, Buenasuerte, Napolidan, San Jose, Sooc, La Purisima, Haluban, Caibigan, Barrera Jr., Tapi, Badas, Bagong-Sikat, Colacling, Tanawan, Lourdes, Belcruz, Alleomar, Salvacion, Tible, Casay, Del Carmen and San Pedro in the Municipality of Lupi, Province of Camarines Sur, are hereby declared as duly constituted barrios of said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
