Republic Act No. 6545
August 26, 1972
AN ACT REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FIFTY-EIGHT HUNDRED FIFTY-FIVE WHICH AMENDED REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FORTY-SIX HUNDRED NINETEEN, AND REVIVING ALL THE PROVISIONS OF THE LATTER LAW.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Republic Act Numbered Fifty-eight hundred fifty-five which amended Republic Act Numbered Forty-six hundred nineteen, entitled "An Act Granting Visayan Electric Company, Inc., a Franchise for Electric Light, Heat and Power System in the City of Dumaguete and in the Municipalities of Bacong, Dauin and Valencia, all in the Province of Oriental Negros," is hereby repealed and all the provisions of the said Republic Act Numbered Forty Six hundred nineteen are hereby revived.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, August 26, 1972.
