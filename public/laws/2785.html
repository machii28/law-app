Republic Act No. 2785
June 19, 1960
AN ACT DECLARING THE SITIOS OF OCINI, OCTAWAN AND MAYLOCBAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAGNAY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, AS PART OF THE BARRIO OF TINOROGNAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Ocini, Octawan and Maylocban in the Municipality of Sagnay, Province of Camarines Sur, are declared as part of the barrio of Tinorognan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
