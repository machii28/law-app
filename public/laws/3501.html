Republic Act No. 3501
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF ILANG PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE DISTRICT OF TIBUNGCO, DAVAO CITY, TO SIXTO BABAO PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Ilang Primary School in the District of Tibungco, Davao City, is changed to Sixto Babao Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
