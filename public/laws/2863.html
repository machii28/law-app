Republic Act No. 2863
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ENRILE, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Liwan Sur, Liwan Norte and Roma in the Municipality of Enrile, Province of Cagayan, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of San Agustin, San Luis and Roma, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
