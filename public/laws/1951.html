Republic Act No. 1951
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BITULOK SAWMILLS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, MUNICIPALITY OF SABANI, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, TO FELIPE BUENCAMINO, SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Bitulok Sawmills Elementary School, Municipality of Sabani, Province of Nueva Ecija, is changed to Felipe Buencamino, Sr. Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
