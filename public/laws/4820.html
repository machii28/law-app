Republic Act No. 4820
June 18, 1966
AN ACT AMENDING SECTION ONE (B) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOUR THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED THIRTY-FOUR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section one (B) of Republic Act Numbered Four thousand one hundred thirty-four is amended to read as follows:
"SEC. 1(B).—The annual salary of the Presiding Justice of the Court of Appeals shall be twenty-six thousand pesos; the Department Secretaries, the Executive Secretary, the Commissioner of the Budget, the Social Welfare Administrator, the Administrator of Economic Coordination, the Chairman of the National Economic Council, the Chairman of the National Science Development Board, and officials with the rank of Secretary of Department, Associate Justices of the Court of Appeals, the Solicitor General, the Commissioner, National Integration Commission, and officials with equivalent rank, twenty-four thousand pesos each; the Secretary of the Senate, the Secretary of the House of Representatives, the Presiding Judge of the Court of Industrial Relations, the Presiding Judge of the Court of Tax Appeals, and the Deputy Auditor general, twenty thousand pesos; the Associate Judges of the Court of Industrial Relations, the Associate Judges of the Court of Tax Appeals, the Judges of the Courts of First Instance and officials of equivalent rank, the Judges of the Court of Agrarian Relations, the Judge of the Juvenile and Domestic Relations Court, and the Public Service Commissioner, nineteen thousand pesos each; the Chairman, Civil Service Board of Appeals, the Chairman, Code Commission, the Department Undersecretaries, the Assistant Executive Secretaries, the Chairman of the Games and Amusements Board, the Administrator of the Philippine Veterans Administration, the Commissioner, Tariff Commission, the Associate Commissioners of Public Service Commission, the Associate Commissioners of the Commission on National Integration, and officials with the rank of Undersecretary of Department, eighteen thousand pesos each; the Members of the Civil Service Board of Appeals and the Members of the Code Commission, sixteen thousand pesos each; the Assistant Commissioner, Tariff Commission, and the Deputy Administrators, Philippine Veterans Administration, twelve thousand pesos each."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
