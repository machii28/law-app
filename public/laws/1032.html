Republic Act No. 1032
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JACINTO, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO CATIGBIAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the municipality of San Jacinto, Province of Bohol, is changed to Catigbi-an.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
