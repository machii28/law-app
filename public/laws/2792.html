Republic Act No. 2792
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (aq), SUBPARAGRAPH (6), FOR THE PROVINCE OF BATAAN, PAGE TWO HUNDRED FOURTEEN, PARAGRAPH f, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (aq), subparagraph (6), for the Province of Bataan, page two hundred fourteen, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(aq) Gumitna-Eskina road, Samal—
Improvement P3,000.00."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
