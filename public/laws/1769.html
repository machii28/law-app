Republic Act No. 1769
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BALADING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUYO, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Balading in the Municipality of Cuyo, Province of Palawan, is converted into a barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Balading.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
