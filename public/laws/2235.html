Republic Act No. 2235
June 12, 1959
AN ACT TO AMEND REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWO THOUSAND FORTY-SIX, ENTITLED "AN ACT CREATING A PUBLIC CORPORATION TO BE KNOWN AS THE CAVITE ELECTRICITY DISTRIBUTING AUTHORITY," AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The title of Republic Act Numbered Two thousand forty-six is hereby amended to read as follows:
"An Act creating a public corporation to be known as the Communications and Electricity Development Authority, Cavite."
SEC. 2. Section one of the same Act is amended to read as follows:
"SECTION 1. Creation of the Communications and Electricity Development Authority, Cavite: its general purposes; zone and extent of jurisdiction; domicile and place of business of the corporation.—For the purpose of consolidating and centralizing the generation and distribution of electric light, heat and/or power under one control, direction, management and supervision, and the development of communication facilities in the Province of Cavite, there is hereby created a public corporation to be known as the Communications and Electricity Development Authority, Cavite, which shall be organized immediately after the approval of this Act.

"The Communications and Electricity Development Authority; Cavite, shall have jurisdiction and supervision over all electric systems, own and manage all government electric systems and shall develop communications facilities within the boundaries of cities and municipalities in Cavite.

"The domicile and place of business of the Authority shall be determined by the Board of Directors."
SEC. 3. Section two of the same Act is hereby amended by adding thereto the following subparagraph:
"(m) To construct, maintain and operate radio and telephone networks."
SEC. 4. Whenever in the same Act the name "Cavite Electricity Distributing Authority," "Public Utilities Commission" and "Rehabilitation Finance Corporation" appear, they shall read respectively as "Communications and Electricity Development Authority, Cavite," "Public Service Commission" and "Development Bank of the Philippines.”
