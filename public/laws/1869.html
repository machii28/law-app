Republic Act No. 1869
June 22, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THIRTEEN HUNDRED ONE, ENTITLED "AN ACT GRANTING JORGE J. TIRADOR A TEMPORARY PERMIT TO CONSTRUCT, ESTABLISH, MAINTAIN AND OPERATE PRIVATE FIXED POINT-TO-POINT AND PRIVATE COASTAL RADIO STATIONS FOR THE RECEPTION AND TRANSMISSION OF RADIO COMMUNICATION WITHIN THE PHILIPPINES."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section three of Republic Act Numbered Thirteen hundred one is hereby amended to read as follows:
"SEC. 3. This temporary permit shall continue to be in force during the time that the Government has not established similar service at the places selected by the grantee, and is granted upon the express condition that the same shall be void unless the construction of said station be begun within one year from the date of approval of this Act and be completed within four years from said date."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
