Republic Act No. 5120
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CUBAO HIGH SCHOOL, MURPHY ANNEX, IN QUEZON CITY TO JUAN SUMULONG HIGH SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Cubao High School, Murphy Annex, in Quezon City is hereby changed to Juan Sumulong High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
