Republic Act No. 1762
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF DINADIAWAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIPACULAO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Dinadiawan in the Municipality of Dipaculao, Province of Quezon, is hereby constituted into a new barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Dinadiawan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
