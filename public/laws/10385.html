Republic Act No. 10385
March 14, 2013
AN ACT CONVERTING THE GEN. ORDOÑEZ AVENUE TRAVERSING BARANGAY MARIKINA HEIGHTS, BARANGAY CONCEPCION UNO, BARANGAY CONCEPCION DOS AND BARANGAY PARANG IN THE SECOND DISTRICT, CITY OF MARIKINA INTO A NATIONAL ROAD
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Gen. Ordoñez Avenue traversing Barangay Marikina Heights, Barangay Concepcion Uno, Barangay Concepcion Dos and Barangay Parang in the Second District, City of Marikina is hereby converted into a national road.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall include in the Department’s program the improvement, maintenance and repair of the said road, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
