Republic Act No. 2164
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PATNOÑGON, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Patnoñgon, Province of Antique, are constituted as distinct and independent barrios, to wit:
  1. The sitios of Mag-ranting, Tigangcal, Agsaman, Epgas, Epayo and Eglapang to be known as the barrio of Alvaniz;
  2. The sitios of Igtuba Maradiona and Igtuba Aureliana to be known as the barrio of Magsaysay;
  3. The sitios of .Amarong, Tagbacan, Da-o, Yapo and Embalodan to be known as the barrio of Elio; and
  4. The sitios of Tigabobo, Tiglomabong, Maybangcal, Calagtingan and Bongbongan to be known as the barrio of Vista Alegre.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
