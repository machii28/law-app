Republic Act No. 1731
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF CERTAIN SCHOOLS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADADA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The names of the following schools in the Municipality of Padada, Province of Davao, are changed as follows:
  1. Padada Elementary School to Northern Padada Elementary School, and
  2. Roosevelt Elementary School to Padada Central Elementary School.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
