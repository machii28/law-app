Republic Act No. 3533
June 20, 1963
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF MEMORIAL MONUMENTS TO MARK THE GRAVES OF SOLDIERS BURIED AT THE LIBINGAN NG MGA BAYANI IN FORT WILLIAM MCKINLEY, AND OTHER PLACES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of five hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the construction of memorial monuments to mark the graves of soldiers buried at the Libingan ng mga Bayani in Fort William McKinley, and other places which may be designated by the Secretary of National Defense.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1963.
