Republic Act No. 2749
June 19, 1960
AN ACT AMENDING SECTION FOUR OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-ONE RELATIVE TO THE SALARY OF THE LIEUTENANT-GOVERNOR OF THE SUBPROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MISAMIS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section four of Republic Act Numbered Two thousand twenty-one is hereby amended to read as follows:

"SEC. 4. The lieutenant-governor of the Subprovince of Camiguin shall receive a compensation of six thousand pesos per annum."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
