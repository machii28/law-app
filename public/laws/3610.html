Republic Act No. 3610
June 22, 1963
AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE APPROPRIATION OF ONE HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS AS ADDITIONAL FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF THE NAVAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The additional sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the Naval School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Leyte, during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-four. The sums necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
