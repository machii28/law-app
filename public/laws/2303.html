Republic Act No. 2303
June 19, 1959
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE MAINTENANCE AND OPERATION OF A GENERAL HOSPITAL IN ORMOC CITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE ORMOC GENERAL HOSPITAL AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the Bureau of Hospitals, a twenty-five-bed general hospital in Ormoc City to be known as the Ormoc General Hospital, which shall be under the management and supervision of a chief who must be a physician of good repute, a graduate from a medical college of recognized standing and has been engaged in the practice of medicine for at least five years. The chief of said hospital shall be appointed by the Secretary of Health, upon the recommendation of the Director of Hospitals, and shall receive a compensation of not more than six thousand pesos per annum. The Chief of the Ormoc General Hospital shall have all the powers generally conferred on directors of government hospitals.

SEC. 2. The Chief of the Ormoc General Hospital shall have authority to promulgate, with the approval of the Director of Hospitals, such rules and regulations not inconsistent with law, as may be necessary to secure the efficient administration of the hospital and the proper enforcement of laws relating thereto.

SEC. 3. With the approval of the Director of Hospitals, the Chief of the Ormoc General Hospital may allow subsistence, quarters, and laundry service in kind to physicians, nurses or employees serving in the hospital whenever advisable to the best interest of the public service.

SEC. 4. The sum of eighty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said hospital for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty: Provided, That no amount be appropriated will be released unless the Secretary of Health certifies that a suitable hospital building is available. Such sum as may be needed for the same purpose in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated shall have bee provided for.

Approved, June 19, 1959.
