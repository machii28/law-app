Republic Act No. 7599
June 3, 1992
AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CUYO DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUYO, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Cuto District Hospital in the Municipality of Cuyo, Province of Palawan, is hereby increased from fifty 50) to seventy-five (75) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following is enactment into law thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 2, 1992
