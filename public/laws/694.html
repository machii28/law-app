Republic Act No. 694
May 9, 1952
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF NEW TOLONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL NEGROS, TO BAYAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of New Tolong, Province of Oriental Negros, is changed to Bayawan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
