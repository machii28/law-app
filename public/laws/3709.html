Republic Act No. 3709
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF INDANAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INDANAN, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO PANGLIMA INDANAN CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Indanan Elementary School in the Municipality of Indanan, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed lo Panglima Indanan Central Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
