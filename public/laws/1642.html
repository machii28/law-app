Republic Act No. 1642
June 20, 1957
AN ACT PROVIDING THAT THE SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOBOC, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, AT PRESENT KNOWN AS OY PRIMARY SCHOOL, SHALL HEREAFTER BE KNOWN AS BARTOLOME DORIA PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The school in the Municipality of Loboc, Province of Bohol, at present known as Oy Primary School, shall hereafter be known as Bartolome Doria Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
