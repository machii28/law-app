Republic Act No. 692
May 9, 1952
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN PEDRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of San Pedro in the Municipality of Tabaco, Province of Albay, is converted into a barrio to be called San Pedro.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
