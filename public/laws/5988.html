Republic Act No. 5988
August 4, 1969
AN ACT AMENDING PARAGRAPH 29.15, CHAPTER 29, SCHEDULE VI OF SECTION ONE HUNDRED FOUR OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED THIRTY-SEVEN, KNOWN AS THE TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Paragraph 29.15, Chapter 29, Schedule VI of Section one hundred four of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred thirty-seven is hereby amended to read as follows;

"29.15 Polyacids and their anhydrides, acid halides, acid peroxides and peracids, and their halogenated, sulphonated, nitrated, or nitrosated derivatives:


a. Phthalate plasticizers, esters of phthalic anhydride

n.w. kg. P1.00

or ad val. 75%

b. Phosphate plasticizers, esters of phosphorous oxychloride

n.w. kg. P1.00

or ad val. 75%

c. Sebacates, adipates, and azelates, esters of sebacic, adipic, and azelaic acids

n.w. kg. P1.00

or ad val. 75%

d. Epoxidized oils, esters of epoxidized oils

n.w. kg. P1.00

or ad val. 75%

e. Mixtures containing alkyl phthalates

n.w. kg. P1.00

or ad val. 75%

f. Other

ad val. 40%"



Enacted without Executive approval, August 4, 1969.
