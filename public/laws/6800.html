Republic Act No. 6800
December 12, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LABASAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO MOISES ABANTE MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Labasan Elementary School in the Municipality of Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Moises Abante Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 24, 1989 and September 22, 1989, respectively.
