Republic Act No. 6691
February 10, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BINAWANGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BINAWANGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF CAPALONGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO MARCA MURILLO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Binawangan Elementary School in Barangay Binawangan, Municipality of Capalonga, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Marca Murillo Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA
 (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House
of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 26, 1988 and December 2, 1988, respectively.
