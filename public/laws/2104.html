Republic Act No. 2104
April 3, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF P1TAWE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LABASON, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Bacong, Gabong, Pitawe and Banga-an in the Municipality of Labason, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Pitawe.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 3, 1959.
