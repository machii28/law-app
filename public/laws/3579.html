Republic Act No. 3579
June 21, 1963
AN ACT CREATING ADDITIONAL POSITIONS OF ASSISTANT PROVINCIAL FISCALS IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE SECTION SIXTEEN HUNDRED AND SEVENTY-FOUR OF THE REVISED ADMINISTRATIVE CODE, AS AMENDED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section sixteen hundred and seventy-four of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Ilocos Norte, is further amended to read as follows:
"IIocos Norte, six assistant provincial fiscals."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1963.
