Republic Act No. 2169
April 25, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN MUNICIPALITY OF TABANGO, PROVINCE LEYTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Tabango, Province of Leyte, are constituted as barrios, to wit:
  1. The sitios of Sumacab, Mina, Durabod, Lawis, Tipolo Tubig-Baho, So-oy and Payaw (Tabang-o side) to be known as the barrio of Inancatan;
  2. The sitios of Colonia, Tigo, La Fortuna II, Cabato-an, and Danaw to be known as the barrio of Manlawa-an;
  3. The sitios of Bahay-Dako, Hamorawon, Sabang, Palid Gamay, Hacienda Bough, Bulalakaw, Dina-ayan and Holipak to be known as the barrio of Gimarco;
  4. The sitios of Tangohay, Ruscidelsa, Cocolay and Bagakay to be known as the barrio of Catmon;
  5. The sitios of Banoyo, Cota, Palanog, Palanas, Bahay Camay, Batwan, Arimoso, Binanig, Manapaw and Panowayan to be known as the barrio of Gibacungan; and
  6. The sitios of La Fortuna I, Tambis, Tag-abaka, Lugapak, Divisoria and Pagsa to be known as the barrio of Omaganhan.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 25, 1959.
