Republic Act No. 1595
June 16, 1956
AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAMBUNAO HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAMBUNAO, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A REGIONAL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE ILOILO VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATIONS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Lambunao High School in the Municipality of Lambunao, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a regional vocational high school to be known as the Iloilo Vocational High School. The said school shall be under the supervision of the Bureau of Public Schools and subject to the provisions of the School Law. Courses of secondary level shall be offered in the said school.

SEC. 2. The sum of two hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the expenses of the said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred fifty-seven. The necessary funds for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
