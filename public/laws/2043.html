Republic Act No. 2043
June 23, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF TAGUIMTIN, MUNICIPALITY OF DAO, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO PACIENCIA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Taguimtin, Municipality of Dao, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Paciencia.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive Approval, June 28, 1957.
