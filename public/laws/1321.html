Republic Act No. 1321
June 16, 1955
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF MASARA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MABINI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled;

SECTION 1. The following territory is created as a barrio in the Municipality of Mabini, Province of Davao, to be known as the barrio of Masara. It shall be bounded on the North by the Compostela boundary; on the South by mountain ranges; on the West by sitio Panibasan; and on the East by the Municipality of Caraga.

Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
