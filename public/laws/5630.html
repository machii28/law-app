Republic Act No. 5630
June 21, 1969
AN ACT NAMING THE ROTONDA ON QUEZON BOULEVARD EXTENSION AND EPIFANIO DE LOS SANTOS AVENUE IN QUEZON CITY, ISAURO GABALDON ROTONDA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The rotonda on Quezon Boulevard Extension and Epifanio de los Santos Avenue in Quezon City shall hereafter be known and referred to as Isauro Gabaldon Rotonda.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
