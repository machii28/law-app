Republic Act No. 3391
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Kay Duque, Mabuhay, Dampulan and Talawan in the Municipality of Torrijos, Province of Marinduque, are converted into barrios to be known as the barrios of Kay Duque, Mabuhay, Dampulan and Talawan, respectively.

SEC. 2. The sitio of Bambangalon in the Municipality of Boac, same province, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio, of Bambangalon.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
