Republic Act No. 7435
April 23, 1992
AN ACT NAMING THE JAGNA DISTRICT HOSPITAL AS THE TEODORO B. GALAGAR DISTRICT HOSPITAL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Jagna District Hospital is hereby named as Teodoro B. Galagar District Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved: April 23, 1992
