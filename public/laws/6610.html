Republic Act No. 6610
October 23, 1972
AN ACT DECLARING THE CENTRAL CATANDUANES (BOCTOT) PARK IN THE PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES AS THE ALBERTO NATIONAL MEMORIAL PARK.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Central Catanduanes (Boctot) Park in the Province of Catanduanes is hereby declared as Alberto National Memorial Park.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved.

GIL J. PUYAT CORNELIO T. VILLAREAL
President of the Senate Secretary of the House of Representatives

Finally passed by the Senate on September 4, 1972 This Act, which origin the House of Representative, was finally passed by the April 25, 1972.
