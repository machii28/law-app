Republic Act No. 1208
April 20, 1955
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM TWENTY-ONE OF THE PUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS FOR THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON, SUB-SECTION (a), TITLE A, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item twenty-one of the public works projects for the Province of Quezon, subsection (a), title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Six hundred seventy, is to read as follows:
"21. For the construction of the Sto.
Cristo School Building, Sariaya ………. P5,000.00.”
SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 20, 1955.
