Republic Act No. 6221
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CONVERTING A CERTAIN DISTRICT INTO A BARRIO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUYAPO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The District No. III of the Municipality of Cuyapo, Province of Nueva Ecija, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Latap.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
