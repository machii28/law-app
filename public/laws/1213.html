Republic Act No. 1213
May 10, 1955
AN ACT REPEALING ITEM THIRTY-FOUR (e), SUBSECTION (a), TITLE C OF SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED AND TWENTY, AND APPROPRIATING THE AMOUNT OF FORTY-FIVE THOUSAND PESOS THEREIN PROVIDED FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF THE CALANAN-TUGA-PINUKPUK ROAD IN MOUNTAIN PROVINCE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item thirty-four (e), subsection (a), Title C of section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred find twenty, on page one hundred and fourteen thereof, is hereby repealed, and the sum of forty-five thousand pesos therein provided is hereby appropriated for the construction of the CalananTuga-Pinukpuk Road in Mountain Province.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 10, 1955.
