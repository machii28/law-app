Republic Act No. 9777
November 17, 2009
AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER 12 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GETAFE, PROVINCE OF BOHOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE "GETAFE FOUNDATION DAY"
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. October 12 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Municpality of Getafe, Province of Bohol to commemorate its founding anniversary and to be known as the "Getafe Foundation Day".

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,
