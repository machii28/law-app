Republic Act No. 6224
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BARANGAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ESPIRITU, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO HILARIO VALDEZ.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Barrio of Barangay in the Municipality of Espiritu, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Hilario Valdez.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
