Republic Act No. 1025
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SIT1O OF CASA REAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAKIL, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO CASA REAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Casa Real in the municipality of Pakil, Province of Laguna, is hereby converted to a barrio to be known as barrio Casa Real.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
