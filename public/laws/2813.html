Republic Act No. 2813
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TORIL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIBOJOC, PROVINCE OF BOHOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Toril in the Municipality of Maribojoc, Province of Bohol, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Toril.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
