Republic Act No. 6718
March 17, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TIGATO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BUHANGIN DISTRICT, DAVAO CITY, TO PILAR RODRIGUEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Tigato Elementary School in Buhangin District, Davao City, shall now be known as the Pilar Rodriguez Elementary School in honor of its donor, the late Pilar P. Rodriguez.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House
of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 26, 1989 and December 2, 1988, respectively.
