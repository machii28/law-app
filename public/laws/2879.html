Republic Act No. 2879
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE ECUAN PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF GOLICTOP, MUNICIPALITY OF DUMINGAG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, TO GOLICTOP PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Ecuan Primary School in the barrio of Golictop, Municipality of Dumingag, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is hereby changed to Golictop Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
