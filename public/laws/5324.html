Republic Act No. 5324
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TOKETEK IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Toketek in the Municipality of Tayug, Province of
Pangasinan, is hereby converted into a barrio of said municipality to be
known as Barrio Toketek.

SEC. 2. This. Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
