Republic Act No. 2213
May 16, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE RAGAN NORTE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGSAYSAY, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO SAN RAMON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Ilagan Norte Elementary School in the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province of Isabela, is hereby changed to San Ramon Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 16, 1959.
