Republic Act No. 5274
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PAOAY ELE­MENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ATOK, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, TO CELO HAIGHT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Paoay Elementary School located in the Municipality of Atok, Province of Benguet, is hereby changed to Celo Haight Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
