Republic Act No. 2861
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (97), PAGE 657, TITLE (A) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (97), page 657, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, is amended to read as follows:

"(97) Construction of Cabilauan Barrio School
Home Economics Building, Barotac Nuevo ................. P4, 000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
