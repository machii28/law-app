Republic Act No. 2176
April 26, 1959
AN ACT TO CONSTITUTE THE SITIOS OF BUGHO, SECO, AND CUATRO, ALL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BASEY, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO BACUBAC OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Bugho, Seco and Cuatro, all in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, .are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Bacubac of the same municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 26, 1959.
