Republic Act No. 1630
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALLON IN THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL TO MUNICIPALITY OF MOISES PADILLA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipality of Magallon in the Proving of Negros Occidental is hereby renamed Municipality of Moises Padilla.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
