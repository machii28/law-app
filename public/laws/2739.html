Republic Act No. 2739
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO CONSTITUTE THE SITIOS OF SALOKEGUE, CAN-ABAY, SOKSOKON, CAPATAGAN, DANGLAY AND LOLOGAYAN, ALL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BASEY, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO MAY-ET OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Salokegue, Can-abay, Soksokon, Capatagan, Danglay and Lologayan, all in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into barrio to be known as Barrio May-et of the same municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
