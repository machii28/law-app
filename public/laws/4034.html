Republic Act No. 4034
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MAñGI ELECTMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF UPI, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, TO PEDRO C. DOLORES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled;

SECTION 1. The name of Mañgi Elementary School in the Municipality of Upi, Province of Cotabato, is hereby changed to Pedro C. Dolores Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
