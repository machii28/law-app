Republic Act No. 1329
June 16, 1955
AN ACT TO CREATE THE BARRIOS OF UBA, MABA-HIN, TAG-ANOÑGAN AND MANLICO, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CORTES, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Uba, Mabahin, Tag-anongan and Manlico, in the Municipality of Cortes, Province of Surigao, are hereby constituted into barrios in the same municipality, to be known as Uba, Mabahin, Tag-anoñgan and Manlico, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
