Republic Act No. 10507
April 16, 2013
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GAUDENCIO ABORDO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BULAWIT, MUNICIPALITY OF LINAPACAN, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN TO BULAWIT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of a National High School. – The name of Gaudencio Abordo National High School in Barangay Bulawit, Municipality of Linapacan, Province of Palawan is hereby changed to Bulawit National High School.

SEC. 2. Location of the Main Campus. – The main campus of the Bulawit National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Bulawit, Municipality of Linapacan, Province of Palawan.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
