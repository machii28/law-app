SEC. 3. There is hereby appropriated, out of any in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the amount of seventy-five thousand two hundred pesos to carry out the provisions of the Act for the current fiscal year and the amount of one hundred sixty-seven thousand four hundred eighty pesos for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty. The corresponding appropriation for the succeeding fiscal years shall be included in the General Appropriation Acts for such years.

SEC. 4. Any savings realized in the appropriation authorized in this Act at the end of the fiscal year may be used by the Auditor General in strengthening his Office.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1959.
