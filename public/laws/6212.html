Republic Act No. 6212
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF EAST SIASI ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIASI, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO PANGLIMA JALMAANI IMLANI ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of East Siasi Elementary School in the Municipality of Siasi, Province of Sulu, is changed to Panglima Jalmaani Imlani Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
