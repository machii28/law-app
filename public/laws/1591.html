Republic Act No. 1591
June 16, 1956
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF FOUR HUNDRED THOUSAND PESOS ANNUALLY FOR TWO YEARS AS ADDITIONAL FUND TO CARRY OUT THE PURPOSES OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TEN HUNDRED EIGHTY-TWO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of four hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated annually, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, as additional fund to carry out the purposes of Republic Act Numbered Ten hundred eighty-two for the fiscal years nineteen hundred fifty-six to nineteen hundred fifty-seven and nineteen hundred fifty-seven to nineteen hundred fifty-eight, for the purchase of medical and dental supplies and also of medical and dental equipments and instruments for the use in the rural areas.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
