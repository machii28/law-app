Republic Act No. 4219
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GENERAL MACARTHUR, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Bagte, Tugop, Opong, Santa Fe and Lanipawan in the Municipality of General MacArthur, Province of Samar, are converted into barrios of said municipality, to be known as the barrios of Bagte, Tugop, Opong, Santa Fe and Lanipawan, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
