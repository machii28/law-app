Republic Act No. 2852
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (n), PAGE 224, GROUP I FOR THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, SUBSECTION (f), TITLE M, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWO THOUSAND NINETY-THREE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (n), page 224, Group I for the Province of Laguna, subsection (f), Title M, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Two thousand ninety-three is amended to read as follows:

"(n) Calamba Elementary School,

For construction and/or improvement ........... P8,000.00."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
