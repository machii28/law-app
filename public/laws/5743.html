SEC. 2. The Director of Lands shall, in consultation with the Municipal Council of Panabo and in accordance with principles of sound community planning, designate portions of the parcels of land described in the preceding section hereof for the following purposes:
  1. Site for school of fisheries;
  2. Site for a beach resort;
  3. Site for wharf facility;
  4. Communal site;
  5. Industrial site; and (f) Residential site.
SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
