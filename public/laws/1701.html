Republic Act No. 1701
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF CABUAYA, MUNICIPALITY OF MATI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO BARRIO DAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Cabuaya, Municipality of Mati, Province of Davao, is changed to barrio Dawan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
