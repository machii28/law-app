Republic Act No. 3274
June 17, 1961
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE LEYTE REGIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES TO OFFER TEACHER EDUCATION CURRICULA LEADING TO THE DEGREE OF BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN INDUSTRIAL EDUCATION AND THE DEGREE OF BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN INDUSTRIAL ARTS, TO OFFER EVENING CLASSES IN SUCH COURSES AND IN TRADE AND INDUSTR1AL EDUCATION, AND PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE PURPOSE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Leyte Regional School of Arts and Trades situated in the City of Tacloban, Province of Leyte, is hereby authorized to offer Teacher Education Curricula leading to the degree of Bachelor of Science in Industrial Education and the degree of Bachelor of Science in Industrial Arts. Evening classes shall be offered in such courses and in Trade and Industrial Education.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the curricula during the fiscal year nine­teen hundred sixty-one to nineteen hundred sixty-two. Thereafter, the necessary funds for the purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17,1961.
