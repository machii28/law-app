Republic Act No. 1656
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MARBER INTERIOR ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANSALAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO NEW CLARIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Marber Interior Elementary School in the Municipality of Bansalan, Province of Davao is changed to New Clarin Elementary School.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
