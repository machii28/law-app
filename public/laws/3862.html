Republic Act No. 3862
June 12, 1964
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT, OPERATION, AND MAINTENANCE OF AN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIKINA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, AND AU­THORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

Section 1. There shall be established, operated and main­tained under the direct supervision of the Director of Medical Services, an emergency hospital in the Municipality of Marikina, Province of Rizal, to be known as Marikina Valley Emergency Hospital.

Sec. 2. The sum of two hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation, and maintenance of said hos­pital during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-five. Thereafter, such sum as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1964.
