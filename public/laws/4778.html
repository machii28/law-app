Republic Act No. 4778
June 18, 1996
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO BALANTOY, MUNICIPALITY OF BALBALAN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sitios Balantoy, Ligayan, Kilayon, Bolo and Nawoy, Municipality of Balbalan, Mountain Province, are constituted into a barrio, to be known as Barrio Balantoy of said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
