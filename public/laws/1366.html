Republic Act No. 1366
June 18, 1955
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF SON-OC, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CORTES, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO FATIMA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of So-noc, in the Municipality of Cortes, Province of Bohol, is hereby converted to a barrio to be Known as barrio Fatima.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1955.
