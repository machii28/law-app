SEC. 2. There is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the amount of one hundred eighty-eight thousand, six hundred fifty pesos and forty centavos for the fiscal year nineteen hundred and sixty-one. The corresponding appropriation for the succeeding fiscal years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 1960.

Approved, June 18, 1960.
