Republic Act No. 1662
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALITAGTAG, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following barrios are created in the Municipality of Alitagtag, Province of Batangas:

Barrio Pingas, which shall be composed of sitios Pingas and Malukan; and
Barrio Sambi, which shall be constituted by the present sitio of Sambi.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
