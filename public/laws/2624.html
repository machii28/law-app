Republic Act No. 2624
June 14, 1960
AN ACT CONVERTING THE BURUANGA REGIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BURUANGA, PROVINCE OF AKLAN, INTO A NATIONAL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Buruanga Regional High School in the Municipality of Buruanga, Province of Aklan, is hereby converted into a national vocational high school.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry into effect the provisions of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1960.
