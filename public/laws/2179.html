Republic Act No. 2179
May 2, 1959
AN ACT TO RECREATE THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF IMUGAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA WHICH NAME IS TO BE CHANGED TO SANTA FE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipal District of Imugan in the Province of Nueva Vizcaya is hereby recreated.

SEC. 2. The boundaries existing between said Municipal District of Imugan and the towns of Aritao and Kayapa before the effectivity of Executive Order Numbered Three hundred sixty-eight, series of nineteen hundred fifty, are hereby restored.

SEC. 3. The name of Imugan is hereby changed to Santa Fe.

SEC. 4. The seat of the municipal government shall be located in Santa Fe.

SEC. 5. The officials of the recreated municipal district shall be appointed by the President of the Philippines.

SEC. 6. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 2, 1959.
