Republic Act No. 7858
January 21, 1995
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF RIZAL COLLEGE OF AGRICULTURE AND TECHNOLOGY IN SAMPALOC, MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO RIZAL STATE COLLEGE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Rizal College of Agriculture and Technology located in Sampaloc, Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Rizal State College.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA (Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR.
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 21, 1994 and November 15, 1994, respectively.
