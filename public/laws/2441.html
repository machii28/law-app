Republic Act No. 2441
June 21, 1959
AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NATIONAL VOCATIONAL SCHOOL IN CALBAYOG CITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE TIBURCIO TANCINCO MEMORIAL VOCATIONAL SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the supervision of the Director of Public Schools, a national vocational school to be known as the Tiburcio Tancinco Memorial Vocational School. Courses of secondary level shall be offered in the said school.

SEC. 2. The Director of Public Schools shall, with the approval of the Secretary of Education, issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the Purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is herein authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the stablishment, operation and maintenance of the said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty. Such sums as may be needed for the same purpose in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Acts.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1959.
