Republic Act No. 2522
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SANTOL I ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BIGAA, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO MARCIANO C. RIVERA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Santol I Elementary School in the Municipality of Bigaa, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to Marciano C. Rivera Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
