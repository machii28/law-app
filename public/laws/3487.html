Republic Act No. 3487
June 16, 1962
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF SAN LUIS, SUBPROVINCE OF AURORA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, INTO A MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipal District of San Luis, Sub-province of Aurora, Province of Quezon, is hereby converted into a municipality.

SEC. 2. The present mayor, vice-mayor and councilors of the Municipal District of San Luis shall be the mayor, vice-mayor and councilors, respectively, of the new municipality until their successors shall have been elected in the next regular elections for provincial and municipal officials and shall have qualified.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
