Republic Act No. 1967
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PULONG BUHANGIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, BARRIO OF PULONG BUHANGIN, MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO CORNELIA M. DE JESUS MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted y the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Pulong Buhangin Elementary School, barrio of Pulong Buhangin, Municipality of Sta. Maria, Province of Bulacan, is changed to Cornelia M. de Jesus Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
