Republic Act No. 6197
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO MABUNAW IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Mabunaw in Barrio Nangca, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is separated from said barrio, and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio in said municipality, to be known as Barrio Mabunaw.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
