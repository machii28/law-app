Republic Act No. 7434
April 23, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DOÑA MARIA D. TAN MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN THE CITY OF TANGUB, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL, TO NINOY AQUINO MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Doña Maria D. Tan Memorial Hospital in Tangub City, Province of Misamis Occidental, is hereby changed to Ninoy Aquino Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved: April 23, 1992
