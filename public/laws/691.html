Republic Act No. 691
May 9, 1952
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TRINIDAD IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ORAS, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Trinidad, Tula, Pota’ Palao, Sinalo-an, Tamse-on, Cagmalobago, and Maycorot, in the barrio of Agsam, are hereby separated from the barrio of Agsam, Municipality of Oras, Province of Samar, and constituted as a separate and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Trinidad in the Municipality of Oras, province of Samar.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 9, 1952.
