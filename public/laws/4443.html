Republic Act No. 4443
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF FORT WILLIAM MCKINLEY IN THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO FORT ANDRES BONIFACIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Fort William McKinley in the Province of Rizal, shall henceforth be known as Fort Andres Bonifacio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
