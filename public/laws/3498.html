Republic Act No. 3498
June 16, 1962
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE TABUNAN PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAGO, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO IRINEO MAKILAN PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Tabunan Primary School in the Municipality of Bago, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Irineo Makilan Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
