Republic Act No. 2407
June 21, 1959
AN ACT MAKING THE SITIO OF MABINI IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MATAG-OB, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, A PART OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF PALOMPON, SAME PROVINCE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Mabini in the Municipality of Matag-ob, Province of Leyte, is separated from said municipality and made a part of the barrio of Santiago, Municipality of Palompon, same province.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
