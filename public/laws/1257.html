Republic Act No. 1257
June 10, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN FERNANDO DIVERSION ROAD IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA, TO JOSE ABAD SANTOS BOULEVARD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of San Fernando Diversion Road in the Municipality of San Fernando, Province of Pampanga, is hereby changed to Jose Abad Santos Boulevard.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
