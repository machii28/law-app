Republic Act No. 2546
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PANTALAN BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARLES, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO TIBURCIO BETITA MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Pantalan Barrio School in the Municipality of Carles, Province of Iloilo, is changed to Tiburcio Betita Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
