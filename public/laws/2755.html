Republic Act No. 2755
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CERVANTES EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CERVANTES, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BESSANG PASS MEMORIAL HOSPITAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Cervantes Emergency Hospital in the Municipality of Cervantes, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Bessang Pass Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
