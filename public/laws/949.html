Republic Act No. 949
June 20, 1953
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BITULOK IN THE PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA TO THAT OF SABANI.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Bitulok in the Province of Nueva Ecija is hereby changed to that of Sabani.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
