Republic Act No. 2855
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF LEGUET THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE PALAWAN, INTO A BARRIO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF SANTA TERESA
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Leguet in the Municipality of Roxas, Province of Palawan, is hereby converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Santa Teresa.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval June 19, 1960.
