Republic Act No. 1706
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO COLONIA, MUNICIPALITY OF ABULUG, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO LIBERTAD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION l. The name of barrio Colonia, in the Municipality of Abulug, Province of Cagayan, is changed to Libertad.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
