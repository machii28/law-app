Republic Act No. 4046
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GABONG COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF PanDANAN, MUNICIPALITY OF BALABAC, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO TUAN HADJI JIAKARIA GENERAL COMMUNITY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Gabong Community School in the barrio of Pandanan, Municipality of Balabac, Province of Palawan, is hereby changed to Tuan Hadji Jiakaria General Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June. 18, 1964.
