Republic Act No. 5406
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO ALABAAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARCOS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO DAQUIOAG.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Alabaan in the Municipality of Marcos, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Daquioag.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
