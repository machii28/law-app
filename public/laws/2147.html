Republic Act No. 2147
April 24, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF COGON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIPOLOG, PROVINCE 0F ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Cogon in the Municipality of Dipolog, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Cogon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
