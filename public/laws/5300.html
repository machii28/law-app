Republic Act No. 5300
June 15, 1968
AN ACT MAKING THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOLANA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, AS THE SEAT OF THE HIGHWAY ENGINEERING DISTRICT OF THE SECOND CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICT OF THE SAID PROVINCE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1.The Municipality of Solana, Province of Cagayan, is hereby made the
seat of the highway engineering district of the second congressional district of the
same province.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
