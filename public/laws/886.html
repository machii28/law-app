Republic Act No. 886
June 19, 1953
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CAMANGAAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO 28, MUNICIPALITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO PLARIDEL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Camangaan Elementary School in the barrio 28, municipality of Laoag, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Plaridel Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1953.
