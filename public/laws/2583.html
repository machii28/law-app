Republic Act No. 2583
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE PROVINCE OF ISABELA
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Rang-ayan in the Municipality of Mallig, Province of Isabela, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Rang-ayan.

SEC. 2. The sitio of Sta. Rita in the Municipality of Aurora, same province, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Sta. Rita.

SEC. 3. The sitios of Balmarin, Belma and Salipan in the Municipality of San Agustin, same province, are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Damabal.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
