Republic Act No. 936
June 20, 1953
AN ACT TO CONVERT SITIO PILI IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DESPUJOLS, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON, INTO A BARRIO WITH THE NAME OF DONA TRINIDAD ROXAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sitio Pili in the municipality of Despujols, Province of Romblon, is hereby converted into a barrio with the name of Dona Trinidad Roxas.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
