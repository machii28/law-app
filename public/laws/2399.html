Republic Act No. 2399
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CONVERTING THE CALINOG REGIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO INTO AN AGRICULTURAL-INDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE CALINOG AGRICULTURAL-INDUSTRIAL HIGH SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Calinog Regional High School in the Province of Iloilo is hereby converted into an agricultural-Industrial high school to be known as the Calinog Agricultural-Industrial High School.
.
SEC. 2. The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated for the operation maintenance, acquisition of school site and construction of the school building and/or purchase of equipment not later
that June, nineteen hundred sixty. The sum needed in succeeding years for the operation and maintenance of the said school shall be included in the General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated are provided for.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
