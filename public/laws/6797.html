Republic Act No. 6797
December 12, 1989
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE KENRAM ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN KENRAM, ISULAN, SULTAN KUDARAT, TO J. P. GARCIA MEMORIAL SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Kenram Elementary School located in Kenram, Isulan, Sultan Kudarat, is hereby changed to J. P. Garcia Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 30, 1989 and September 22, 1989, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDWIN P. ACOBA (Sgd.) QUIRINO D. ABAD SANTOS, JR.
