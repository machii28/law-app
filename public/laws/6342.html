Republic Act No. 6342
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CONSTITUTING THE THIRD CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF CEBU INTO A SEPARATE HIGHWAY ENGINEERING DISTRICT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The third congressional district of Cebu is hereby constituted into a separate highway engineering district to be known as the Sixth Highway Engineering District of Cebu, with its principal office at the Municipality of Minglanilla, same province.

The highway district engineer of said highway engineering district shall be appointed in accordance with existing law.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1971.
