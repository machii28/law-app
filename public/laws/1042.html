Republic Act No. 1042
June 12, 1954
AN ACT TO CREATE THE BARRIO OF BADONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Badong in the municipality of Tago, Province of Surigao, is hereby constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Badong.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
