Republic Act No. 1592
June 16, 1956
AN ACT TO AMEND AN ITEM IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWELVE HUNDRED PERTAINING TO THE PROVINCE OF CAPIZ.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (17), paragraph (n) for the Province of Capiz, subsection three, Title A, section one of Republic Act Numbered Twelve hundred, is amended to read as follows:
"(17)
Repair and improvement of the Capiz
Grandstand, Roxas City ..........................
 P5,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
