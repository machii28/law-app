Republic Act No. 7281
March 24, 1992
AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TEN OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ABRA TO BE KNOWN AS ABRA DAY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March Ten of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Abra to commemorate the creation of the Province, to be known as Abra Day.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 4, 1991 and January 23, 1992, respectively.
