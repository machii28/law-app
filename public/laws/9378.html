Republic Act No. 9378
March 7, 2007
AN ACT DECLARING THE FOURTH DAY OF FEBRUARY OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MUNTINLUPA TO BE KNOWN AS "ARAW NG KALAYAAN NG MUNTINLUPA"
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. February 4 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the City of Muntinlupa to be known as "Araw ng Kalayaan ng Muntinlupa" in order to commemorate the heroes of World War II who fought for the independence of Muntinlupa.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

Approved:

(Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR (Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR.
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives


This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on June 6, 2005 and December 18, 2006, respectively.
