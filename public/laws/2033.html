Republic Act No. 2033
June 23, 1957
AN ACT PROVIDING THAT THE SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBIGON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, AT PRESENT KNOWN AS PANAYTAYON PRIMARY SCHOOL, SHALL HEREAFTER BE KNOWN AS JOSE BOJA PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The school in the Municipality of Tubigon, Province of Bohol, at present known as Panaytayon Primary School, shall hereafter be known as Jose Boja Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 23, 1957.
