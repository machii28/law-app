Republic Act No. 1206
April 20, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO JOSE ABAD SANTOS.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Trinidad, Province of Davao, is changed to Jose Abad Santos.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 20, 1955.
