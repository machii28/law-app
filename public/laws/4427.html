Republic Act No. 4427
June 19, 1965
AN ACT TO DECLARE BARRIO KIMPUSA OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SUYO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, AS THE POBLACION AND THE SEAT OF THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF THE SAID MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Barrio Kimpusa of the Municipality of Suyo, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby declared as the poblacion and the seat of the municipal government of the said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
