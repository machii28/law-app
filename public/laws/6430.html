Republic Act No. 6430
June 17, 1972
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE TO PROVINCE OF DAVAO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Province of Davao del Norte is changed to Province of Davao del Norte is changed to Province of Davao.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1972.
