Republic Act No. 2751
June 19, 1960
AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (ac), page 313, Group III, subparagraph (28), for the Province of Leyte, paragraph f, Section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(ac) San Isidro Social Hall, Malitbog—
Construction P3,000.00."

SEC. 2. Item (140), page 709, subparagraph (ab), for the Province of Leyte, paragraph b, Title A, Section three of the same Act is amended to read as follows:

"(140) Construction, Hogpa Barrio School, Malitbog P4,000.00."

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
