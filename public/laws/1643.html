Republic Act No. 1643
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAGDALENA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGDALENA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO ANANIAS LAICO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Magdalena Elementary School in the Municipality of Magdalena, Province of Laguna, is hereby changed to Ananias Laico Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
