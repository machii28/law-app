Republic Act No. 10733
December 29, 2015
AN ACT CONVERTING THE ANDRES BONIFACIO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY JALANDONI-WILSON, CITY PROPER DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS ANDRES BONIFACIO INTEGRATED SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Confess assembled:

SECTION 1. Conversion into an Integrated School. – The Andres Bonifacio Elementary School in Barangay Jalandoni-Wilson, City Proper District, Iloilo City is hereby converted into an integrated school to be known as Andres Bonifacio Integrated School.

SEC. 2. Transfer of Assets and Liabilities. – All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Andres Bonifacio Elementary School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Andres Bonifacio Integrated School.

SEC. 3. Appropriations. – The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Andres Bonifacio Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. Implementation. – The Secretary of Education shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 5. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
