Republic Act No. 1014
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF YABAWON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JONES, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios known as Mahaba, Angomon, Solocan, Kapanranan and Yabawon in the municipality of Jones, Province of Romblon, are hereby constituted into a separate and independent barrio to be known as the barrio of Yabawon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
