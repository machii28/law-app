Republic Act No. 2740
June 19, 1960
AN ACT TO CONSTITUTE THE SITIOS OF SAGNEY, LANAGA, ASGAD AND COMETA, ALL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BASEY, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO GUIRANG OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Sagney, Lanaga, Asgad and Cometa, all in the Municipality of Basey, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio Guirang of the same municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
