Republic Act No. 1721
June 21, 1957
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF CABUNGA-AN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TARANGNAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, INTO A REGULAR BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Cabunga-an in the Municipality of Tarangnan, Province of Samar, is hereby converted into a regular barrio to be called henceforth as the barrio of Cabunga-an.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
