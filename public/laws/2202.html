Republic Act No. 2202
May 7, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN NICOLAS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DAPITAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of San Nicolas, Domonob, Barita, Malobog, Pañgi and Lo-ok in the Municipality of Dapitan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of San Nicolas.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
