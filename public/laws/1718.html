Republic Act No. 1718
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF NASPI IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Naspi and Calaguitan are separated from the barrio of Putiao, Municipality of Pilar, Province of Sorsogon and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Naspi.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
