Republic Act No. 1250
June 10, 1955
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA, FROM BARRIO SAN MATIAS TO BARRIO STO. TOMAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The seat of the Municipality of Sto. Tomas, province of Pampanga, is hereby transferred from Barrio San Matias to Barrio Sto. Tomas. The municipal government shall occupy the site and building it formerly occupied before the said municipality was annexed to the Municipality of San Fernando in the same province.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
