Republic Act No. 1263
June 10, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MINDANAO STREET IN THE CITY OF CAGAYAN DE ORO TO TIANO BROTHERS STREET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Mindanao Street in the City of Cagayan de Oro is hereby changed to Tiano Brothers Street.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
