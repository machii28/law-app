Republic Act No. 1324
June 16, 1955
AN ACT TO CREATE THE BARRIO OF BUSGAHAN IN THE CITY OF LIPA
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Duhatan, Tagbakin, Initan, Malabong and Halang, all in the barrio of Pinagtungolan, City of Lipa, are separated from the said barrio, and constituted into a separate barrio in the said city to be known as the barrio of Buñgahan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
