Republic Act No. 6074
August 4, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TOMINOBO COMMUNITY SCHOOL IN ILIGAN CITY TO TOMAS CABILI MEMORIAL COMMUNITY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Hereafter, Tominobo Community School in Iligan City shall be known as Tomas Cabili Memorial Community School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, August 4, 1969.
