Republic Act No. 1727
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SABLAYAN, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrios of Batasan, Hinaya and Iriron in the Municipality of Sablayan, Province of Occidental Mindoro, shall hereafter be known as barrios Claudio Salgado, Buhay na Bato and San Isidro, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
