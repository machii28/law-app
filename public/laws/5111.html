Republic Act No. 5111
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF TUNGUSUNG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIMUNUL, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO PANGLIMA HUSSIN ARUPIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the many benevolent acts rendered by the late Panglima Hussin Ampin for the cause of education of his people in his native island of Simunul, Province of Sulu, the name Tungusung Elementary School in the Municipality of Simunul, Province of Sulu, is hereby changed to Panglima Hussin Arupin Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
