Republic Act No. 1647
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PUTOL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF PUTOL, MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO PUTOL-STA. ISABEL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Putol Elementary School located at the boundary of the barrios of Putol and Sta. Isabel, Municipality of Kawit, Province of Cavite, is changed to Putol-Sta. Isabel Elementary School.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
