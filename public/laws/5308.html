Republic Act No. 5308
June 15, 1968
AN ACT NAMING THAT PORTION OF THE NORTH DIVERSION ROAD PASSING THROUGH THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN "MARCELO H. DEL , PILAR HIGHWAY."


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. That portion of the North Diversion Road passing through the territorial jurisdiction of the Province of Bulacan is hereby named "Marcelo H. del Pilar High­way."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
