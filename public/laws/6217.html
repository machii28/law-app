Republic Act No. 6217
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO PUZOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINILI, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO TUGAOEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Puzol in the Municipality of Pinili, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Tugaoen.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
