Republic Act No. 3504
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MILIBILI BARRIO SCHOOL IN ROXAS CITY TO DON MANUEL ARNALDO MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Milibili Barrio School in Roxas City is hereby changed to Don Manuel Arnaldo Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
