Republic Act No. 2409
June 21, 1959
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF THE SUM OF ONE HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF THE APARRI FISHERIES SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SEC. 1. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise obligated, for operation and maintenance of the Aparri Fisheries School during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty. Such sum as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated are provided for.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
