Republic Act No. 1496
June 16, 1956
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SAN PEDRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of San Pedro, Abanico and Tiniguiban, Municipality of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the Barrio of San Pedro.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1956.
