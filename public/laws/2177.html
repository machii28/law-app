Republic Act No. 2177
April 26, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SALUYONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANUKAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Saluyong, Sipakong, Upper Dipani, Boob, Lupasang and Messes in the Municipality of Manukan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Saluyong.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 26, 1959.
