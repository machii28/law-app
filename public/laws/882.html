Republic Act No. 882
June 19, 1953
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE OLIMPIA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAKATI, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO FRANCISCO BENITEZ ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Olimpia Elementary School in the municipality of Makati, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Francisco Benitez Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1953.
