Republic Act No. 1633
June 20, 1957
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF GINES PRIMARY SCHOOL TO MARTIN CUBIL PRIMARY SCHOOL IN CABATUAN, ILOILO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the meritorious services rendered by Martin Cubil to the barrio of Gines, Municipality of Cabatuan, Province of Iloilo, the name of Gines Primary School is hereby changed to Martin Cubil Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
