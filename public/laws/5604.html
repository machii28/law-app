Republic Act No. 5604
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BAYBAY PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF ROXAS TO JUDGE CONRADO BARRIOS MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and; House of Representatives, of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Baybay Primary School in the City of Roxas, is hereby changed to Judge Conrado Barrios Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
