Republic Act No. 4646
June 4, 1966
AN ACT APPROPRIATING FIVE MILLION PESOS FOR AID TO AND REHABILITATION OF THE PROVINCES DAMAGED BY THE TYPHOON "IRMA."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of five million pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for aid to and rehabilitation of the provinces of Samar, Leyte, Southern Leyte, Masbate, Romblon, Oriental Mindoro, Occidental Mindoro, and other provinces of the Bicol region damaged by the typhoon "Irma" which occurred in May, nineteen hundred and sixty-six: Provided, That the said sum shall be used only for purposes of reconstruction and/or repair and not for new construction, on the basis of the report to be made by the Department of Public Works and Communications of recorded typhoon damages.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 4, 1966.
