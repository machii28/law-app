Republic Act No. 7374
April 10, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE COBOL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO PETRA Q. PIMENTEL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Cobol Elementary School situated in Barangay Petra Q. Pimentel, Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Petra Q. Pimentel Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 10, 1992.
