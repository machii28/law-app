Republic Act No. 1116
June 15, 1954
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIMBUHAN, PROVINCE OF MASBATE TO PIO V. CORPUS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the eminent and patriotic services rendered by the late Pio V. Corpus, the name of the municipality of Limbuhan, Province of Masbate, is changed to Pio V. Corpus.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1954.
