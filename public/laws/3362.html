Republic Act No. 3362
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF NAPO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DAPITAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, TO BONIFACIO.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Napo in the Municipality of Dapitan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is changed to Bonifacio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
