Republic Act No. 2144
April 24, 1959
AN ACT TO CREATE CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA FE, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following barrios in the Municipality of Sante Fe, Province of Romblon, are created:
  1. Barrio Magsaysay to be composed of the present sitios of Catolog, Palate, Layog, Simbiray and Mambong;
  2. Barrio Mat-i to be composed of the present sitios of Mat-i, Guinpasilan, Bolagsong, Aglagtang and Isabels;
  3. Barrio Agmanic to be composed of the present sitios of Agmanic, Capdang, Tipolo-Bahol, Bolocawe, Binaloca and Cabali-an;
  4. Barrio Tabugon to be composed of the present sitios of Tabugon, Cabungahan, Pittigo, Guinpoyungan, Grande, Cahawagan and Hinaclopan;
  5. Barrio Canyayo to be composed of the present sitios of Canyayo, Lonoc, Campong, Payong-payong and Capid;
  6. Barrio Pandan to be composed of the present sitios of pandan, Sayao, Guinda, Camonsel, Capinayan, Baybay, Candoyong, Lojo and Catopaz;
  7. Barrio Danao Norte to be composed of the present sitios of Danao Norte, Hacienda, Sabang, Timba-on, Danao Sur and Bunga;
  8. Barrio Pinamihagan to be composed of the present sitios of Pinamihagan, Pacol, Buyahon, Sugod, Pinaman-dayan, Pajo and Añgas; and
  9. Barrio Busay to be composed of the present sitios of Busay, Hinologan, Buenavista, Nausa, Tipocpoc, Ag-borot, Boyongboyoñgan and Mihason.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 24, 1959.
