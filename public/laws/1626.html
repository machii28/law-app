Republic Act No. 1626
June 20, 1957
AN ACT AMENDING APPENDIX A, PROJECT NO. B-412.72, PAGE 826, REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIXTEEN HUNDRED AND THIRTEEN, ENTITLED "AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS".
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Appendix A, Project No. B-412.72, page eight hundred twenty-six-A of Republic Act Numbered Sixteen hundred and thirteen, is hereby amended by merely deleting the words "Perez Boulevard" to read as follows:
"Pantal Bridge, Dagupan City .................. P 300,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
