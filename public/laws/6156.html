Republic Act No. 6156
November 9, 1970
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF DAVAO CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN DAVAO CITY TO CAPITAN TOMAS MONTE VERDE, SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Davao Elementary School in Davao City is hereby changed to Capitan Tomas Monteverde, Sr. Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, November 9, 1970.
