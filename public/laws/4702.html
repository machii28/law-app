Republic Act No. 4702
June 18, 1966
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BIGAA IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALAGTAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Bigaa in the Province of Bulacan is hereby changed to the Municipality of Balagtas.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
