Republic Act No. 3364
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIERRA-BULLONES, PROVINCE OF BOHOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Bagacay, Naga, Ricodo-on, Tubod, Abachanan Ilaud and Casili-on in the Municipality of Sierra-Bullones, Province of Bohol, are hereby converted into barrios of the said municipality to be known hereafter as the barrios of Abachanan Oriental, Magsaysay, San Carlos, Cagawasan, Abachanan Norte and Del Pilar, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
