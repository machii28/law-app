Republic Act No. 10622
September 6, 2013
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LAZI NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL IN BARANGAY TIGBAWAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LAZI, PROVINCE OF SIQUIJOR INTO LAZI TECHNICAL INSTITUTE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of a National Agricultural School . – The name of Lazi National Agricultural School in Barangay Tigbawan, Municipality of Lazi, Province of Siquijor is hereby changed to Lazi Technical Institute.

SEC. 2. Location of the Main Campus . – The main campus of the Lazi Technical Institute shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Tigbawan, Municipality of Lazi, Province of Siquijor.

SEC. 3. Effectivity . – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette .
