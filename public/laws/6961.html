Republic Act No. 6961
August 17, 1990
AN ACT REPEALING REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED THERE THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED EIGHTY-FOUR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Republic Act No. 3884 entitled: "An Act establishing a normal college in Dipolog, Zamboanga del Norte, and authorizing the appropriation of funds therfor" is hereby repealed.

SEC. 2. This repealing Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOVITO R. SALONGA (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 6, 1988 and May 31, 1990, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDWIN P. ACOBA (Sgd.) QUIRINO D. ABAD SANTOS, JR.
Secretary of the Senate Secretary of the House of Representatives
