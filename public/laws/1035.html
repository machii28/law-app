Republic Act No. 1035
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF DULAWAN IN THE PROVINCE OF COTABATO TO DATU PIANG.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the municipality of Dulawan in the Province of Cotabato is hereby changed to Datu Piang.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
