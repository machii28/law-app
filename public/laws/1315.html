Republic Act No. 1315
June 16, 1955
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF PATAG TO BARRIO OF SAN ISIDRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BASUD, PROVINCE OP CAMARINES NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

Section 1. The sitio of Patag in the Municipality of Basud, Province of Camarines Norte, is converted into a barrio to be called San Isidro.

Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1955.
