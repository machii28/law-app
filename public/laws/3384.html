Republic Act No. 3384
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF CANTAGDA IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAJIDIOCAN, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Cantagda in the barrio of Danao, Municipality of Cajidiocan, Province of Romblon, is separated from said barrio and converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Cantagda.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
