Republic Act No. 464
June 9, 1950
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO HACLAGAN, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAWAN, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO STO. NIÑO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The barrio of Haclagan, in the municipality of Tanawan, Province of Leyte, shall hereafter be known as "Sto. Nino."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 9, 1950.
