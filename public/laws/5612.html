Republic Act No. 5612
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MERCADO BARRIO SCHOOL IN BARRIO MERCADO, MUNICIPALITY OF HAGONOY, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO CAPTAIN PABLO TRILLANA MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Mercado Barrio School in Barrio Mercado, Municipality of Hagonoy, Province of Bulacan, is changed to Captain Pablo Trillana Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
