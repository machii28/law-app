Republic Act No. 2445
June 21, 1959
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (17), PAGE 4, SECTION l-A-a, TITLE (A) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (17), page 4, section 1-A-a, Title (A) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
"(17) Industrial Arts Annex, Iloilo Vocational
High School—Lambunao 20,000.00."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
