Republic Act No. 7706
May 5, 1994
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BUCANDALA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO IMUS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Bucandala National High School in the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Imus National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved.

Lapsed into law on May 5, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
