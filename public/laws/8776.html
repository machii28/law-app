Republic Act No. 8776
April 7, 2000
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AT STA. MARIA, MUNICIPALITY OF FLORA, PROVINCE OF APAYAO TO BE KNOWN AS THE MAYOR RICARDO DE SAN JOSE SR. COMPREHENSIVE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a national high school at Sta. Maria, Municipality of Flora, Province of Apayao to be known as the Mayor Ricardo de San Jose Sr. Comprehensive National High School.

SEC. 2. The school shall offer secondary education proper as well as undertake other activities to carry out its objectives as may be authorized by the Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.

SEC. 3. The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.

SEC. 4. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 7,2000.
