Republic Act No. 2131
April 7, 1959
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BUNOG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Isugod, Canikitan, Bunog and Tagbuaya in the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bunog.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 7, 1959.
