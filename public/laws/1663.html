Republic Act No. 1663
June 20, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN, REGARDING PUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS FOR THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Items (f), (g), (h) and (i), paragraph thirty, page eighty-eight of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, for the Province of Masbate, are amended to read as follows :

"(f) Placer Municipal Building Construction
P5,000.00
"(g) Uson Municipal Building Construction
5,000.00
"(h) Uson Municipal Building Construction
5,000.00"
"(i) Uson Municipal Building Construction
5,000.00


SEC. 2. Item seven, page two hundred ten of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, for the Province of Masbate, is amended to read as follows:

"7. Municipal Building, Placer
P5,000.00"


SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
