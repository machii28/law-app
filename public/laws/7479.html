Republic Act No. 7479
May 5, 1992
AN ACT NAMING THE AIRFIELD AT THE SANGLEY POINT NAVAL AND AIR TRAINING STATION TO MAJOR DANILO ATIENZA AIRFIELD, SANGLEY POINT
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The airfield at the Sangley Point Naval and Air Training Station is hereby named as Major Danilo Atienza Airfield.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 7, 1992 and January 30, 1992, respectively.
