Republic Act No. 10144
August 20, 2010
AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO EDWARDS A. ARTIS
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representative of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Edward A. Artis is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges, duties and obligations appurtenant thereto and with the same effects and subject to the same conditions as provided under the Constitution.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE
(Sgd.) PROSPERO C. NOGRALES
President of Senate
Speaker of the House of Representatives


This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 17, 2008 and May 31, 2010, respectively.
