Republic Act No. 6209
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SOUTH LAMINUSA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIASI, PROVINCE OF SULU, TO PANGLIMA SAIPUDIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of South Laminusa Elementary School in the Municipality of Siasi, Province of Sulu, is changed to Panglima Saipudin Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
