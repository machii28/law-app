Republic Act No. 3981
June 18, 1964
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE PALAWAN SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES TO OFFER TEACHER EDUCATION CURRICULA LEADING TO THE DEGREE OF BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN EDU­CATION, MAJOR IN INDUSTRIAL ARTS, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Palawan School of Arts and Trades situated in the Municipality of Cuyo, Province of Palawan, is hereby authorized to offer teacher education curricula loading to the degree of Bachelor of Science in Education, major in industrial arts.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said curricula during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-four to nineteen hundred sixty-five. Thereafter, the necessary funds for the purpose shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
