Republic Act No. 4782
June 18, 1966
AN ACT INCREASING THE NUMBER OF ASSISTANT PROVINCIAL FISCALS FOR THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL NEGROS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE SECTION SIXTEEN HUNDRED SEVENTY-FOUR OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE CODE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The provision of Section sixteen hundred seventy-four of the Administrative Code, as amended, relative to the number of assistant provincial fiscals in the Province of Oriental Negros, is further amended to read as follows:

"Oriental Negros, seven assistant provincial fiscals;"


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1966.
