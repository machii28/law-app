Republic Act No. 252
June 14, 1948
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUZURRIAGA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, TO THAT OF "VALENCIA."
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the municipality of Luzurriaga, Province of Negros Oriental, is changed to that of "Valencia."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1948.
