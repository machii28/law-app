Republic Act No. 475
June 9, 1950
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF FORTY-FIVE THOUSAND PESOS FOR THE MAINTENANCE AND OPERATION OF THE PORT OF CAGAYAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MISAMIS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of forty-five thousand pesos or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the improvement, operation and maintenance of the port of Cagayan, Province of Oriental Misamis.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 9, 1950.
