Republic Act No. 5580
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CREATING BARRIO JUAN CARTAS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABA, PROVINCE OF LA UNION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sitio Bas-ang in the Municipality of Caba, Province of La Union, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as Barrio Juan Cartas.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
