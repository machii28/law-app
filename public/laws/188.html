Republic Act No. 188
June 22, 1947
AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBAY, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing barrios of Tubay, La Fraternidad, Tinigbasan, Cabayawa, Victory, Sta. Ana, and Tagmamarcay, in the municipality of Cabadbaran, Province of Agusan, are hereby separated from said municipality and constituted into a new municipality to be known as the municipality of Tubay.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon the proclamation of the President of the Philippines.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 22, 1947.
