Republic Act No. 1006
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CREATING A SITIO IN THE EASTERN PART OF BARRIO TABANG, MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SITIO OF BAGONG SIKAT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A sitio in the eastern part of barrio Tabang, municipality of Plaridel, Province of Bulacan, is created to be known as the sitio of Bagong Sikat.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
