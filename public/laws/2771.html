Republic Act No. 2771
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIOS OF BUG-O AND MOLOBOLO IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ASTURIAS AND TUBURAN, RESPECTIVELY, PROVINCE OF CEBU.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Bug-o in the Municipality of Asturias, Province of Cebu, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Bug-o.

SEC. 2.The sitio of Molobolo in the Municipality of Tuburan, same province, is converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Molobolo.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
