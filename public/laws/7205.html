Republic Act No. 7205
March 2, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MANAT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, TO MANAT CENTRAL SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Manat Elementary School in the Municipality of Nabunturan, Province of Davao del Norte, is hereby changed to Manat Central School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on April 5, 1990 and December 11, 1991, respectively.
