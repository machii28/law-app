Republic Act No. 4992
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF TEJERON IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN LUIS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Tejeron in the Municipality of San Luis, Province of Batangas, is hereby converted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of Tejeron.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
