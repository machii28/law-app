Republic Act No. 1034
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF BANAWA, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABAGAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, TO BANAWAG.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Banawa, in the municipality of Kabagan, Province of Cotabato, is hereby changed to that of Banawag.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
