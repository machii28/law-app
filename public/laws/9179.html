Republic Act No. 9179
November 13, 2002
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LAVEZARES AGRICULTURAL AND INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL, MUNICIPALITY OF LAVEZARES, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR TO BASILIO B. CHAN MEMORIAL AGRICULTURAL AND INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Lavezares Agricultural and Industrial School in the Municipality of Lavezares, Province of Northern Samar is hereby changed to Basilio B. Chan Memorial Agricultural and Industrial School.

SECTION. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval

Approved,

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON (Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR.
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 6, 2002 and August 7, 2002, respectively
