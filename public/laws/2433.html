Republic Act No. 2433
June 21, 1959
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE BALANGIGA JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALANGIGA, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, INTO A RURAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BALANGIGA RURAL HIGH SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and Mouse of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Balangiga Junior High School in the Municipality of Balangiga, Province of Samar, is hereby converted into a rural high school to be known as the Balangiga Rural High School. The Director of Public Schools, subject to the approval of the Secretary of Education, is hereby empowered to reorganize said school accordingly.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos, in addition to what the school is now receiving, is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said school during the school year nineteen hundred and fifty-nine to nineteen hundred and sixty. Thereafter, the amount shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon the date the funds authorized herein to be appropriated shall have been provided for.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
