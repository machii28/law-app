Republic Act No. 3505
June 16, 1962
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TINIO-AN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABATUAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO DOROTEO LUJAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Tinio-an Elementary School in the Municipality of Cabatuan, Province of Iloilo, is changed to Doroteo Lujan Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 16, 1962.
