Republic Act No. 4185
April 21, 1965

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (v), page four hundred seventy-six, subparagraph thirty, for the Province of Leyte del Sur, paragraph "c," Section two of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred one is hereby amended to read as follows:

"(v)
Aid to the Municipality of Hinundayan for construction, improvement and/or repair of municipal projects............................................
P5,000.00"

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 21, 1965.
