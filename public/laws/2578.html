Republic Act No. 2578
June 21, 1959
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF CALLE GRAN CAPITAN IN THE CITY OF TACLOBAN TO JUSTICE NORBERTO ROMUALDEZ, SR. STREET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Calle Gran Capitan in the City of Tacloban is hereby changed to Justice Norberto Romualdez, Sr. Street.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
