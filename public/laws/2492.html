Republic Act No. 2492
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SANTA ROSA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACO, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO JULIO HERNANDEZ MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Santa Rosa Elementary School in the Municipality of Baco, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Julio Hernandez Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon.its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
