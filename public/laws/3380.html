Republic Act No. 3380
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BURNAY IN MOUNTAIN PROVINCE TO LAGAWE.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipal District of Burnay in Mountain Province is changed to Lagawe.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
