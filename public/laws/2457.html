Republic Act No. 2457
June 21, 1959
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (E), FOR THE PROVINCE OF CEBU, PAGE 258, PARAGRAPH F, SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, Item (e), for the Province of Cebu, page 258, paragraph f, section two of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:
"(e) Valladolid Elementary School building,
Carcar—
Completion and painting P3.500.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
