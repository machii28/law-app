Republic Act No. 4583
June 19, 1965
AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A NATIONAL RURAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISABEL, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS ISABEL NATIONAL RURAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND TO AUTHORIZE THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a national rural high school in the Municipality of Isabel, Province of Leyte, to be known as Isabel National Rural High School.

SEC. 2. The Director of Public Schools shall issue rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is here­by authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-six. There­after, the sums necessary therefor shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval

Approved, June 19, 1965.
