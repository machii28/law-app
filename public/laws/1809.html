Republic Act No. 1809
June 22, 1957
AN ACT FURTHER AMENDING THE SECOND PARAGRAPH OF SECTION SIX OF THE CHARTER OF THE CITY OF TAGAYTAY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The second paragraph of section six of Commonwealth Act Numbered Three hundred thirty-eight, otherwise known as the Charter of the City of Tagaytay, as a amended by Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred six, is further amended to read as follows:
"The Mayor shall receive a compensation of six thon pesos per annum. The city secretary shall receive compensation of three thousand pesos per annum. The members of the city council, including the vice-mayor, shall each receive a per diem of twenty pesos for each day of attendance at the session of the city council."
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
