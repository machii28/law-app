Republic Act No. 5100
June 17, 1967
AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE MINALUNGAO NATIONAL PARK


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

All points referred to are described on Plans (Bureau of Lands) Public Land System-49 and Land classification-386.
SEC. 2. The Minalungao National Park shall be withdrawn from sale, settlement, or other disposition, subject to any existing private rights.

SEC. 3. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the construction and development of recreational facilities in said park for the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-eight. The sum necessary for its maintenance in subsequent fiscal years shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
