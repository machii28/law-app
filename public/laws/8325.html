Republic Act No. 8325
June 30, 1997
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO, MUNICIPALITY OF TALAKAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON, TO BE KNOWN AS THE TALAKAG NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A public high school in Barangay San Isidro, Municipality of Talakag, Province of Bukidnon, shall be established under the direct supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, Lapsed into law on June 30, 1997, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
