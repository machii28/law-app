Republic Act No. 2480
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAGAMANOC, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Panuto, Pangcayanan, Quegaray and Cajan in the Municipality of Bagamanoc, Province of Catanduanes, are converted into barrios of said municipality to be known as the barrios of Panuto, Pangcayanan, Quegaray and Cajan, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
