Republic Act No. 1686
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF BALUK-BALOK IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN NICOLAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Baluk-Balok is separated from the barrio of Gipit, Municipality of San Nicolas, Province of Batangas, and constituted into a barrio in the said municipality to be known as the barrio of Baluk-Balok.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
