Republic Act No. 1907
June 22, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE DANAO PROVINCIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DANAO, PROVINCE OF CEBU, INTO A NATIONAL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE DANAO NATIONAL VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Danao Provincial High School in the Municipality of Danao, Province of Cebu, is hereby converted into a national vocational high school to be known as the Danao National Vocational High School. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize said school accordingly.

SEC. 2. The sum of twenty-five thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the operation and maintenance of the said school for the fiscal year ending June thirty, nineteen hundred fifty-eight. Such sum as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
