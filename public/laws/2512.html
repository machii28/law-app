Republic Act No. 2512
June 21, 1959
AN ACT TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF TALUKSANGAY DISTRICT, CITY OF ZAMBOANGA, TO PASILMANTA, SAKOL ISLAND.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The seat of government of Taluksangay District, City of Zamboanga, is hereby transferred from Taluksangay proper to Pasilmanta, Sakol Island.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
