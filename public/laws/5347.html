Republic Act No. 5347
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CONVERTING CERTAIN SITIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JAGNA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL,INTO BARRIOS


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Jagna, Province of Bohol, are hereby converted into barrios to wit:
(a) The sitio of Buyog is hereby converted into a barrio which shall be known as Barrio Buyog;

(b) The sitio of Can-ipol is hereby converted into a barrio which shall be known as Barrio Can-ipol.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
