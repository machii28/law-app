Republic Act No. 1665
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAPAYA IN THE PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA TO GENERAL TINIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Papaya in the Province of Nueva Ecija is hereby changed to General Tinio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
