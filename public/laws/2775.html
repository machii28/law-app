Republic Act No. 2775
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF LILOAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATARMAN, SUB-PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, PROVINCE OP MISAMIS ORIENTAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Manduaw, Pasanan, Bae, Kiab, Banao, Dumilag, Napo, Cogon, Tubos and Tag-ibo in the Municipality of Catarman, Sub-Province of Camiguin, Province of Misamis Oriental, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality to be known as the barrio of Liloan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
