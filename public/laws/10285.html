Republic Act No. 10285
November 14, 2012
AN ACT SEPARATING THE OCAMPO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL – HANAWAN ANNEX IN BARANGAY HANAWAN, MUNICIPALITY OF OCAMPO, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR FROM THE OCAMPO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS HANAWAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Separation and Conversion into a National High School. – The Ocampo National High School – Hanawan Annex in Barangay Hanawan, Municipality of Ocampo, Province of Camarines Sur is hereby separated from the Ocampo National High School and converted into an independent national high school to be known as Hanawan National High School.

SEC. 2. Transfer of Assets and Liabilities. – All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Ocampo National High School – Hanawan Annex are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Hanawan National High School.

SEC. 3. Appropriations. – The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Hanawan National High School, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year’s appropriations of the Ocampo National High School – Hanawan Annex. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. Implementation. – The Secretary of Education shall issue rules and regulations that may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 5. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
