Republic Act No. 3379
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF THE ISLAND, OF BULALACAO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CORON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Island of Bulalacao in the Culion Reservation, Municipality of Coron, Province of Palawan, is separated from the Culion Reservation and constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of the Island of Bulalacao.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
