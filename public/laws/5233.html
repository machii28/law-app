Republic Act No. 5233
June 15, 1968
AN ACT INCLUDING NUEVA ERA, ILOCOS NORTE, WITHIN THE SPHERE AND/OR JURISDICTION OF THE COMMISSION ON NATIONAL INTEGRATION.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The town of Nueva Era, Province of IIocos Norte, is hereby included within the sphere and/or jurisdiction of the Commission on National Integration.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
