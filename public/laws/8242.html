Republic Act No. 8242
December 20, 1996
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY PUG-OS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINAIT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BARANGAY DEAN LEOPOLDO YABES
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barangay Pug-os in the Municipality of Sinait, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Barangay Dean Leopoldo Yabes.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, December 20, 1996.
