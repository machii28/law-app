Republic Act No. 1486
June 16, 1956

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (b), Group I, paragraph seventeen, for the "Province of Cebu, subsection "f" section two of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:

"(b) Barrio Schools to be designated by the Municipal Mayor, Danao
Construction and/or repair…......................................................P18.000.00."

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
