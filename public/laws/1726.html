Republic Act No. 1726
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO MAT-I IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DULAG, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO PRESIDENT ROXAS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of barrio Mat-i in the Municipality of Dulag, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to President Roxas.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
