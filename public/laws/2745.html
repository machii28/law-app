Republic Act No. 2745
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF NAASAG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBAJAO, SUB-PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Naasag, Baring, Volcan, Tangub, Basiao and Looc in the Municipality of Mambajao, Sub-province of Camiguin, Province of Misamis Oriental, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the barrio of Naasag.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
