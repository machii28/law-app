Republic Act No. 1725
June 21, 1957
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF PAMOBLARAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DULAG, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS THE BARRIO OF SAN ANTONIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Pamoblaran in the Municipality of Dulag, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a barrio to be known as the barrio of San Antonio.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
