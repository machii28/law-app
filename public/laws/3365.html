Republic Act No. 3365
June 18, 1961
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BALAÑGIGAN BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUENAS, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BIBA BARRIO SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Balañgigan Barrio School in the Municipality of Duenas, Province of Iloilo, is changed to Biba Barrio School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 18, 1961.
