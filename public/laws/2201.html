Republic Act No. 2201
May 7, 1959
ACT CREATING CERTAIN BARRIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DAPITAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The following sitios in the Municipality of Dapitan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are constituted into barrios, to wit:
  1. The sitios of Urap and Talisay to be known as the barrio of Oro;
  2. The sitios of San Francisco, Villa Trinidad and Sigayan to be known as the barrio of San Francisco;
  3. The sitios of Sicayab, Larayan, Pagatpatan, Boton and Aligway Island to be known as the barrio of Sicayab;
  4. The sitio of Napo to be known as the barrio of Napo;
  5. The sitio of Oyan to be known as the barrio of Oyan;
  6. The sitio of Tag-olo to be known as the barrio of Tag-olo; and
  7. The sitio of Aseniero to be known as the barrio of Aseniero.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
