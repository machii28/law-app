Republic Act No. 6476
June 17, 1972
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PILOT PROJECT FOR ANIMAL INDUSTRY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAGUILIAN, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the direct Supervision of the Director of Animal Industry, a pilot project for animal industry in the Municipality of Naguilian, Province of La Union.

SEC. 2. The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said pilot Project during the fiscal year nineteen hundred seventy-Three. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act: Provided, That no Portion of this appropriation shall be transferred to any other item.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1972.
