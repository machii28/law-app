Republic Act No. 5407
June 15, 1968
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARRIO LACUB IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO BARRIO FABIAN MARCOS.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barrio Lacub in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to Barrio Fabian Marcos.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
