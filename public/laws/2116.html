Republic Act No. 2116
April 5, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF CABICALAN IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF POTIA, IFUGAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, TO BARRIO SAN DOMINGO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Cabicalan in the Municipal District of Potia, Ifugao, Mountain Province, is changed to barrio San Domingo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, April 5, 1959.
