Republic Act No. 7441
April 27, 1992
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE LEYTE STATE SCHOOL OF AGRICULTURE IN ALANGALANG, LEYTE, TO OFFER THE BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN AGRICULTURE AND BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN AGRI-BUSINESS AND OTHER AGRICULTURE-RELATED PROGRAMS
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Leyte State School of Agriculture in Alangalang, Leyte, is hereby authorized to offer the Bachelor of Science in Agriculture and Bachelor of Science in Agri-business and other agriculture-related programs.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 13, 1992 and February 6, 1992, respectively.
