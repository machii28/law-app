Republic Act No. 6229
June 19, 1971
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BATAC GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO MARCOS MEMORIAL HOSPITAL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Batac General Hospital in the Municipality of Batac, Province of IIocos Norte, is hereby changed to Marcos Memorial Hospital, thereby amending Republic Act Numbered Fifty-one hundred ninety-one.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1971.
