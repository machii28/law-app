Republic Act No. 3677
June 22, 1963
AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE APPROPRIATION OF FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS AS AID TO THE SULU HIGH SCHOOL IN THE PROVINCE OF SULU.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of fifty thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, as aid to the Sulu High School in the Province of Sulu during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-four. Such sums as may be necessary in subsequent years for the same purpose, shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Acts.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
