Republic Act No. 2772
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GATMAITAN STREET IN LIPA CITY TO TEODORO M. KALAW STREET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Gatmaitan Street in Lipa City is hereby changed to Teodoro M. Kalaw Street.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
