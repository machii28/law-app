Republic Act No. 2815
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN MARIANO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO MALAKING POOK BARRIO SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION. 1. The name of the San Mariano School in the barrio of Malaking Pook, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, is changed to Malaking Pook Barrio School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
