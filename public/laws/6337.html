Republic Act No. 6337
June 19, 1971
AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN EMERGENCY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANDAN, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO BE KNOWN AS NORTHERN ANTIQUE EMERGENCY HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the supervision of the Bureau of Hospitals, an emergency hospital in the Municipality of Pandan, Province of Antique, to be known as Northern Antique Emergency Hospital.

SEC. 2. The sum of one hundred fifty thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the hospital for the fiscal year in which this Act shall take effect. Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1971.
