Republic Act No. 717
June 6, 1952
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MATIMBO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF MATIMBO, MUNICIPALITY OF MALOLOS, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO GENERAL ISIDORO TORRES MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Matimbo Elementary School in the barrio of Matimbo, Municipality of Malolos, Province of Bulacan, is changed to General Isidoro Torres Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 6, 1952.
