Republic Act No. 1182
June 20, 1954
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAMES OF STREETS IN THE DISTRICT OF TONDO, OF MANILA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The streets of Pingkian, Kulambo, and Bang, kusay in the district of Tondo, City of Manila, shall hereafter be known as Gregorio Perfecto Street, Rosauro Almario Street, and Francisco Varona Street, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, without Executive approval, June 20, 1954.
