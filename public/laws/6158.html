Republic Act No. 6158
November 9, 1970
AN ACT CHANCING THE NAME OF POOK ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SITUATED AT BARRIO POOK, MUNICIPALITY OF STA. ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO JOSE ZAVALLA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Pook Elementary School situated at Barrio Pook, Municipality of Sta. Rosa, Province of Laguna, is hereby named and is to be known henceforth as Jose Zavalla Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, November 9, 1970.
