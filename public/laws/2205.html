Republic Act No. 2205
May 7, 1959
AN ACT TO CONSTITUTE THE SITIOS OF CAMBUÑGATOD AND SOLOP BOTH IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALALORA, PROVINCE OF SAMAR INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO SAN JUAN OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitios of Cambuñgatod and Solop in the Municipality of Talalora, Province of Samar, are hereby constituted into a barrio to be known as Barrio San Juan of the same municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, May 7, 1959.
