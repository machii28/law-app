Republic Act No. 1638
June 20, 1957
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF DACULANG-BOLO BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF DACULANG-BOLO, MUNICIPALITY OF DAET, PROVINCE: OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO SALVACION PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Daculang-bolo Barrio School in the barrio of Daculang-bolo, Municipality of Daet, Province of Camarines Norte, is changed to Salvacion Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1957.
