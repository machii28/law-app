Republic Act No. 437
June 7, 1950
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF RIZAL CITY TO PASAY CITY
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Rizal City created by Republic Act Numbered One hundred and eighty-three is hereby changed to Pasay City.

SEC. 2. Whenever the words "Rizal City" appear in Republic Act Numbered One hundred eighty-three, and other acts or part of acts, the said words shall be understood to mean or to refer to Pasay City.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 7, 1950.
