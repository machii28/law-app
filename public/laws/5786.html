Republic Act No. 5786
June 21, 1969
AN ACT EXCLUDING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND FROM THE FORBES PARK NO. 1 RESERVATION, CITY OF BAGUIO, TO BE LEASED TO THE BOY SCOUTS AND GIRL SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SEC. 2. The President of the Philippines is authorized to lease the same parcel of land for a period of twenty-five years and for a nominal sum of one peso1 to the Boy Scouts and Girl Scouts of the Philippines exclusively for purposes for which said organizations have been established : Provided, That said parcel of land shall not be sub-leased.

SEC. 3. Upon termination of the lease or purpose for which it was leased all the fixed assets and permanent improvements of the said parcel of land will automatically revert to the Government.

All the foregoing conditions shall be excluded in the lease to be executed by the President of the Philippines.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 21, 1969.
