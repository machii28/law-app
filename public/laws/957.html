Republic Act No. 957
June 20, 1953
AN ACT TO CONVERT THE SITIO OF BUCO IN THE BARRIO OF BALAS, MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, INTO A BARRIO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Buco in the barrio of Balas, municipality of Talisay, Province of Batangas, is hereby separated from said barrio and converted into a new barrio to be known as the barrio of Buco.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval
Approved, June 20, 1953.
