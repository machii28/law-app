Republic Act No. 3883
June 18, 1964
AN ACT TO AMEND ITEM (13-c), PAGE 917, TITLE (f) OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item (13-c), page 917, Title (f) of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred is amended to read as follows:

"(13-c) For the construction, repair and/or improvement of cot­tages or other

buildings of the Iloilo National Agricultural School, Lambunao ................... P15,000,00."


SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
