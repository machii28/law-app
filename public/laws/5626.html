Republic Act No. 5626
June 21, 1969
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MALINTA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, TO ENRIQUE LEGASPI, SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Malinta Elementary School in the Municipality of Masbate, Province of Masbate, is hereby changed to Enrique Legaspi, Sr. Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1969.
