Republic Act No. 7463
April 28, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE QUEZON NATIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO MANUEL S. ENVERGA MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF ARTS AND TRADES
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Quezon National School of Arts and Trades in the Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Manuel S. Enverge Memorial College of Arts and Trades to honor and perpetuate the memory of the best known and most accomplished congressman produced by the province.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES (Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA
President of the Senate Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on April 5, 1990 and February 6, 1992, respectively.
