Republic Act No. 3774
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN JOSE-DEL CARMEN BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF SAN JOSE, MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, TO MANUEL CACHO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the San Jose-Del Carmen Barrio School in the barrio of San Jose, Municipality of Talisay, Province of Camarines Norte, is hereby changed to Manuel Cacho Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
