Republic Act No. 4417
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CONVERTING THE SITIO OF TANZA, MUNICIPALITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, INTO A BARRIO TO BE KNOWN AS BARRIO TANZA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sitio of Tanza in the Municipality of Tuguegarao, Province of Cagayan, is hereby converted into a barrio of said municipality to be known as Barrio Tanza.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
