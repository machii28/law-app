Republic Act No. 10148
March 12, 2011
AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MARCUS EUGENE DOUTHIT
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Marcus Eugene Douthit is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.

SEC. 2. Upon taking and registration of the oath of allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines, Marcus Eugene Douthit shall enter upon the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
