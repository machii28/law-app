Republic Act No. 4170
August 8, 1964
AN ACT APPROPRIATING THREE MILLION PESOS FOR THE LUNETA DEVELOPMENT PROJECT AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum of three million pesos is appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the Luneta Development project, the same to be expended exclusively for the said purpose by the National Parks Development Committee The Auditor General shall audit, in accordance with law and administrative regulations, all expenditures from the sum herein appropriated. The appropriation to the extent of unreleased balances as of the date of approval of this Act provided for the Luneta Park, Manila, in item numbered nine, paragraph a, Title A, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Thirty-eight hundred; and forty-six is hereby repealed.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, August 8, 1964.
