Republic Act No. 3057
June 17, 1961
AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MARIANO R. GOROSPE


Be it enacted by the Senate avid House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Mariano R. Gorospe is hereby granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives appurtenant thereto.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 17, 1961.
