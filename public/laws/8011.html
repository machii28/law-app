Republic Act No. 8011
May 25, 1995
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LEYTE STATE SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES IN TANAUAN, LEYTE, TO LEYTE COLLEGE OF ARTS AND TRADES
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Leyte State School of Arts and Trades in Tanauan, Leyte, is hereby changed to Leyte College of Arts and Trades.

SEC. 2. The Leyte College of Arts and Trades shall be under the supervision of the Commission on Higher Education.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Lapsed into law on May 25, 1995 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
