Republic Act No. 1024
June 12, 1954
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PAMPANGA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF DAVAO TO VICENTE HIZON MEMORIAL SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Pampanga Elementary School in the City of Davao shall hereafter be known as the Vicente Hizon Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 12, 1954.
