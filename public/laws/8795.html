Republic Act No. 8795
June 27, 2000
AN ACT NAMING THE 2ND MANDAUE-MACTAN BRIDGE IN THE PROVINCE OF CEBU AS THE MARCELO B. FERNAN BRIDGE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The 2nd Mandaue-Mactan Bridge situated in the Province of Cebu is hereby named as the Marcelo B. Fernan Bridge.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON
 (Sgd.) MANUEL B. VILLAR JR.
