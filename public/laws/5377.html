Republic Act No. 5377
June 15, 1968
AN ACT NAMING THE LONGEST STREET IN BARRIO SAN ISIDRO, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FRANCISCO, PROVINCE OF CEBU, THE QUIRICO MANINGO ROAD.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The longest street in Barrio San Isidro, Municipality of San Francisco, Province of Cebu, is hereby named Quirico Maningo Road.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 15, 1968.
