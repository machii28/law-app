Republic Act No. 2604
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF A BARRIO AND OF SITIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLANCO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Loboc in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, is changed to San Miguel, and the names of the sitios of Lantoy and Biga-an in the same municipality are changed to Sto. Nino and Mabuhay, respectively.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
