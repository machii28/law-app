Republic Act No. 7943
March 3, 1995
AN ACT CONVERTING THE AMAI PAKPAK GENERAL HOSPITAL IN MARAWI CITY, LANAO DEL SUR, INTO A MEDICAL CENTER, TO BE KNOWN AS THE AMAI PAKPAK MEDICAL CENTER, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Amai Pakpak General Hospital located in Marawi City, Lanao del Sur, is hereby converted into a medical center with all the necessary personnel, equipment and facilities, to be known as the Amai Pakpak Medical Center.

SEC. 2. The Department of Health shall formulate the necessary guidelines for the operation of the said medical center.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
