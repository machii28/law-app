Republic Act No. 3992
June 18, 1964
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BANILAD BARRIO SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACONG, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL TO NAZARIO TALE MEMORIAL SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Banilad Barrio School in the Municipality of Bacong, Province of Negros Oriental, is hereby changed to Nazario Tale Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 18, 1964.
