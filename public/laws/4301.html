Republic Act No. 4301
June 19, 1965
AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A SCHOOL FOR PHILIPPINE CRAFTSMEN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TABANGO, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS TABANGO VOCATIONAL SCHOOL, AND TO AUTHORIZE THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A school of the Polangui School for Philippine Craftsmen type is hereby established in the Municipality of Tabango, Province of Leyte, to be known as Tabango Vocational School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education is authorized to organize the curricular instruction of said school and he shall have the power to issue rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the establishment, operation and maintenance of said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-six. Thereafter, the sums necessary therefor shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
