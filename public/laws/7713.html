Republic Act No. 7713
May 5, 1994
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF DASMARIÑAS RELOCATION CENTER HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DASMARIÑAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO DASMARIÑAS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

Section 1. The name of Dasmariñas Relocation Center High School in the Municipality of Dasmariñas, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Dasmariñas National High School.

Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Lapsed into law on May 5, 1994 without the President's signature, pursuant to Sec. 27(1), Article VI of the Constitution.
