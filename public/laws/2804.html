Republic Act No. 2804
June 19, 1960
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAMES OF CERTAIN BARRIOS AND SITIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLANCO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The names of the following barrios and sitios in the Municipality of Polanco, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, are changed as follows:
  1. Barrio Prenda to Barrio Labrador;
  2. Barrio Loboc to Barrio San Miguel;
  3. Sitio Lantoy to Sitio Sto. Niño;
  4. Sitio Biga-an to Sitio Mabuhay; and
  5. Sitio Ludloron to Sitio Salvador.
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
