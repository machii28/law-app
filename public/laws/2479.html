Republic Act No. 2479
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF CADAMORTISAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF UMINGAN, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO DON MONTANO.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of barrio Cadamortisan in the Municipality of Umingan, Province of Pangasinan, is changed to Don Montano.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
