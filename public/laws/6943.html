Republic Act No. 6943
April 5, 1990
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY SANTOL, MUNICIPALITY OF BALAGTAS, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO BARANGAY DR. NEMESIO G. GARCIA, SR.
SECTION. 1 The name of Barangay Santol, Municipality of Balagtas, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to Barangay Dr. Nemesio G. Garcia, Sr.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its publication in at least two national newspapers of general circulation.

Approved: April 5, 1990.
