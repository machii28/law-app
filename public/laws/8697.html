Republic Act No. 8697
July 9, 1998
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SANTA FE EXTENSION HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MARCELINO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, TO SAN GUILLERMO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Santa Fe Extension High School in the Municipality of San Marcelino, Province of Zambales, is hereby changed to San Guillermo National High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES
(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR.
President of the Senate
Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 12, 1998 and February 19, 1998, respectively.
