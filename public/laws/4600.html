Republic Act No. 4600
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CALLANG MU­NICIPALITY, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO SAN MANUEL MUNICIPALITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Callang Municipality in the Province of Isabela is hereby changed to San Manuel Municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
