Republic Act No. 4392
June 19, 1965
AN ACT CONVERTING SAMAR HIGH SCHOOL, IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATBALOGAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR, INTO A NATIONAL SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS SAMAR NATIONAL SCHOOL, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS THEREFOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Samar High School in the Municipality of Catbalogan, Province of Samar, is hereby converted into a national school to be known as Samar National School. General as well as vocational courses shall be offered in said school.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education is hereby empowered to reorganize said school accordingly.

SEC. 3. The sum of three hundred fifty thousand pesos, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby authorized to be appropriated out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out the purposes of this Act during the fiscal year nineteen hundred sixty-six. Thereafter, such sums as may be needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
