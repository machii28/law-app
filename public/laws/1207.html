Republic Act No. 1207
April 20, 1955
AN ACT TO CHANGE THE NAME OF THE BARRIO OF PAJARITO, MUNICIPALITY OF SIBALOH PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO CATMON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the barrio of Pajarito in the Municipality of Sibalom, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Catmon.

SEC. 2. Republic Act Numbered Eight hundred and ninety is hereby repealed.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, April 20, 1955.
