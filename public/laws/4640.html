Republic Act No. 4640
June 19, 1965
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE PEOPLE'S HOMESITE AND HOUSING CORPORATION TO ACQUIRE THAT CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND IN BARRIO PANSOL, QUEZON CITY, OWNED BY THE NATIONAL WATERWORKS AND SEWERAGE AUTHORITY TO BE SUBDIVIDED AND SOLD TO BONA FIDE OCCUPANTS THEREOF.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The People's Homesite and Housing Corpora­tion is hereby authorized to acquire that certain parcel of land situated in Barrio Pansol, Quezon City, owned by the National Waterworks and Sewerage Authority, and subdivide the same into lots which shall be sold to bona fide occupants thereof.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 19, 1965.
