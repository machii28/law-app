Republic Act No. 1249
June 10, 1955
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF MORON, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, TO MORONG.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Moron, Province of Bataan, is hereby changed to Morong.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
