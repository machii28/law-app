Republic Act No. 2757
June 19, 1960
AN ACT REAPPROPRIATING CERTAIN SUMS APPROPRIATED IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINETEEN HUNDRED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sum appropriated in item (k), page 109, subparagraph 48, for the Province of Sorsogon, paragraph a, Title B, Section one of Republic Act Numbered Nineteen hundred, to wit:

"(k) Dancalan-Pangpang-Gimagaan-Vinisitahan-Tinanogan-Sibago-San Rafael-Sta. Cruz Road, Donsol—
Construction P20, 000.00"

is hereby reappropriated for the construction of the Donsol-Banuangurang-Javellar Road, Municipality of Donsol, Province of Sorsogon.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
