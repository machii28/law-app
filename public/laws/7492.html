Republic Act No. 7492
May 13, 1992
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE HOMONHON PROVINCIAL HIGH SCHOOL IN HOMONHON, MUNICIPALITY OF GUIUAN, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR, TO HOMONHON HIGH SCHOOL
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Homonhon Provincial High School in Homonhon, Municipality of Guiuan, Province of Eastern Samar, is hereby changed to Homonhon High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, May 13, 1992.
