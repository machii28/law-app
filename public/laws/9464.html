Republic Act No. 9464
May 15, 2007
AN ACT RENAMING THE BACOLOD — MURCIA — SAN CARLOS ROAD (BACOLOD SECTION)IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD TO ROMEO G. GUANZON AVENUE
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The 9.62 kilometer stretch comprising the Bacolod Section of the Bacolod — Murcia — San Carlos Road from the junction of Araneta Street to the boundary of Bacolod City and the Municipality of Murcia in Negros Occidental, is hereby renamed as the Romeo G. Guanzon Avenue.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. (Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR
Speaker of the House of Representatives President of the Senate


This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on June 6, 2005 and February 19, 2007 respectively.
