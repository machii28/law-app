Republic Act No. 2557
June 21, 1959
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PATAO PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE BARRIO OF BAWOD, MUNICIPALITY OF BANTAYAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU, TO BAWOD PRIMARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Patao Primary School in the barrio of Bawod, Municipality of Bantayan, Province of Cebu, is changed to Bawod Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, June 21, 1959.
