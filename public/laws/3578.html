Republic Act No. 3578
June 21, 1963
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BUENAVISTA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUENAVISTA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO DOÑA ASUNCION PALMA GUEVARA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Buenavista Elementary School in the Municipality of Buenavista, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Doña Asuncion Palma Guevara Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1963.
