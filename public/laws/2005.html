Republic Act No. 2005
June 22, 1957
AN ACT TO AMEND SUB-ITEM (m), GROUP II, ITEM 27 FOR THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE, PARAGRAPH (a), TITLE C, SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED TWENTY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sub-item (m), Group II, item 27 for the Province of Leyte, paragraph (a), Title C, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty is hereby amended to read as follows:
"(m) Burauen-Albuera road P 56,000.00"
SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
