Republic Act No. 5124
June 17, 1967
AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF CULILANG BARRIO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARRIO CULILANG, MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, TO VEDASTO T. JAVELOSA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Culilang Barrio Elementary School in Barrio Culilang, Municipality of President Roxas, Province of Capiz, is hereby changed to Vedasto T. Javelosa Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 17, 1967.
