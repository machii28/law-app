Republic Act No. 5353
June 15, 1968
AN ACT STANDARDIZING SALARIES OF CERTAIN OFFICIALS OF THE CITY OF MANILA AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.


Be it enacted by the Senate, and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The provisions of the Revised Charter of the City of Manila, as amended, to the contrary notwithstand­ing the following officials of the City of Manila shall receive the annual compensation as fixed hereunder:

Mayor P36,000.00
Vice-Mayor 30,000.00
Twenty Councilors 26,000.00 each
City Engineer 24,000.00
City Treasurer 24,000.00
City Assessor 24,000.00
City Health Officer 24,000.00
City Auditor 24,000.00
City Public Service Officer 24,000.00
Chief, Manila Fire Department 24,000.00
Superintendent of City Schools

Chairman, Veterinary Inspection Board 24,000.00

22,000.00

Assistant City Auditor 22,000.00
Assistant City Public Service Officer 22,000.00
Deputy Chief of Police 22,000.00
Chief Detective Bureau 22,000.00
Deputy Chief, Fire Department 22,000.00
Three Members, Veterinary Inspection Board 22,000.00 each
Two Assistant Superintendents of City Schools 22,000.00 each
Two Assistant Secretaries to the Mayor 22,000.00 each
One Legislative liaison Officer to the Mayor 18,000.00

SEC. 2. The officials herein above mentioned shall re­ceive commutable representation allowance to be determined by the Municipal Board and as approved by the Mayor.

SEC. 3. The incumbent officials holding the positions affected by this Act shall receive the salary increases upon the effectivity thereof without the necessity of new ap­pointments.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 15, 1968.
