Republic Act No. 3653
June 22, 1963
AN ACT CREATING THE BARRIO OF SALANG IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALABAC, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN.


Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION. 1. The silios of Lagdong, Caritan and Bumaga in the Municipality of Balabac, Province of Palawan, are constituted into a distinct and independent barrio of said municipality, to be known as the Barrio of Salang.

SEC. 2. This Act shall lake effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1963.
